# 洗衣设备保养（LDY_011）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_011 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All laundry equipment must be dusted daily and regularly maintained, in the correct way to ensure the best possible oper（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 设备日常保养
- 可疑设备上报

## 目的

All laundry equipment must be dusted daily and regularly maintained, in the correct way to ensure the best possible operation and to minimize breakdowns and accidents.
洗衣房所有设备必须以正确方法每天除尘,定期清洁,确保最佳工作状态最大限度降低机器损耗及事故的发生

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

All equipment is the responsibility of every service team and the office will keep a log for all machines.
所有设备均由各组（机器维保商或酒店工程部）指定专人负责并报办公室备案。机器方面的说明书都整理好交给工程部，每次设备维修，都要留好维修记录。
Ensure that all accessible parts are clean, spotless, and clear as the surface.
机器内部凡可清除到的部位与表面一样无灰尘，无油污，无杂物
Keep the area around the machines clean and in order
机器四周地面清洁整齐
Only clean the surface of electric equipment. Avoid touching the inside for safety purpose
电器部位只清扫表面，不动内部以防止发生危险
Clean the pipes on top of the equipment.
清洁机器顶部的管道
Report spare parts of machine for replacement as soon as possible.
需要更换的机器附属件及时上报进行更换
Laundry shift leader will arrange associates to clean and maintain the equipment. The laundry manager will check it regularly
设备的清洁维护由各领班督促员工完成，经理不定期检查
洗衣机加油后，为了避免漏油污染布草，先洗涤抹布等布草，或黑色工服，或放少量报废干净布草洗涤，确认无漏油再洗涤。
