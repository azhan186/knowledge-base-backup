# 公共区域检查（PA_005）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_005 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All associates should finish assigned work according to Supervisor instruction and quality checks will be carried out du（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 公共区域日常检查

## 目的

All associates should finish assigned work according to Supervisor instruction and quality checks will be carried out during the shift to ensure all tasks are completed.
确保员工能按要求在工作时间内高质量的完成所分派的工作

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Ensure all associates work in their assigned working areas.
确保员工能在指定地点工作。
Make all associates work in an efficient way, correct any fault during inspection on time.
跟进工作进度及质量，发现问题及时指出
Inspect safety and security conditions.
检查工作中的安全情况
Ensure all associates use and maintain cleaning equipment and machines in a proper way.
确保员工能正确使用清洁剂和机器。
Observe all associates working performance including grooming and guest service.
检查员工的言行举止及对客时的表现。
Resolve problems for the associate if needed.
解答员工在工作中遇到的问题。
Observe all associates working performance, find out reasons for sub-standard performance and resolve problems if any.
观察员工的工作态度，找出原因，解决问题
Check and follow up maintenance issues.
检查工程维修情况
Ensure all supplies for Public Area are replenished on time.
确保清洁所需的物品及时补充到位
Ensure flowers and plants are in good conditions, report to Executive Housekeeper if any problems found and inform the supplier to change flowers and plants on time.
检查鲜花和植物，确保状态良好，如需更换及时与花房联系并向行政管家汇报
Inspect PA storerooms to ensure they are clean.
检查库房是否干净整洁
