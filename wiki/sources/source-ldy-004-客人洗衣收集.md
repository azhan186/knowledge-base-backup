# 客人洗衣收集（LDY_004）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_004 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Guest laundry must be collected within 10 minutes of guest’s request, must use professional and polite greetings as belo（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- DND（请勿打扰）处理
- 客衣服务

## 目的

Guest laundry must be collected within 10 minutes of guest’s request, must use professional and polite greetings as below:
客衣的收取必需在客人要求10分钟内完成, 必须使用专业的礼貌地问候语。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

When the runner receives a call of collecting guest dry cleaning / laundry from housekeeping coordinator, notes the time of phone call, guest’s room number and service style on the sheet immediately.
当收衣员接到管家部文员客衣收取电话时，立即记录电话时间、客人房号及洗衣服务方式。
Runner is to go promptly to the guestroom and press the doorbell with saying in a firm voice, “laundry service” two times. Wait 3 seconds and listen for any room noise. If no one answers, after three cycles then open the door to pick up laundry.
（楼层服务员在10分钟内收好洗衣，通知洗衣房服务员到工作间取衣）如果前台或总机不能通知到楼层服务员，则收衣员迅速至客人房间，按门铃一次，每次随后报“洗衣服务”一次，并等待3秒钟。房间无声响，轮回3次后, 然开门收洗衣。
When the guest answers, she is to greet the guest and say: “Good morning /afternoon /evening, Mr. /Mrs. ... I have come to collect your laundry.” with guest allowance, collect laundry, checking that laundry list with the room number on it, is inside the bag. Then say “Thank you, Mr. /Mrs. ... enjoy your stay here.” and leave the guestroom.
如客人在房内，问候客人：“早晨/下午/晚上好，XXX先生/女士，我来收取您的洗衣。”客人允许后， 收取衣物，检查洗衣单及房号填写是否正确。向客人道别：“再见，XXX先生/女士，希望居住愉快。” 并离开房间
After ensuring there is no one in the room and the laundry is there in the bag, collect and check room number if it’s correct on the list before taking it down to the laundry.
确认客人不在房内后，收取洗衣，检查洗衣单及房号填写是否正确离开客人房间，带洗衣回洗衣房。
If there is a “do not disturb” sign being displayed, don not knock or press doorbell. Report the DND to the housekeeping clerk immediately. Then the clerk will report to GRM, employee open the door to collect laundry MUST after GRM authorizes it. If GRM can’t connect guest, don’t open the door, just leave “DND card” under the door to remind guest we can’t pick up his laundry.
如果有一个“请勿打扰”迹象被显示，不要敲门或按门铃。立刻报告办公室文员。总机电话客人房间，等待客人开门取衣。如果客人不在房间，由文员/总机报告给宾客服务经理，在得到宾客服务经理授权后方能开门收洗衣。如果宾客服务经理联系不到客人，就门缝下塞入“DND卡”提醒客人未能收取洗衣。
