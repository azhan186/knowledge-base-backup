# 清洁公共电话（PA_001）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_001 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Due to heavy usage of public telephone, it is very important to ensure cleanliness standard are meet since it has direct（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 公共电话消毒清洁

## 目的

Due to heavy usage of public telephone, it is very important to ensure cleanliness standard are meet since it has direct personal contact with guests.
由于公共电话使用频繁,并且客人需要亲自接触它,因此保持其达到清洁标准是十分重要的。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of Hotel that the public telephones is cleaned and cared for at least once every hour. All public telephone facilities are kept clean and are immaculately maintained.
公共电话应至少每小时被清洁并检查一次。所有公共电话设施应保持清洁并完好无损。

## 程序

Clean all public telephones every hour.
公共电话应每小时被清洁一次。
All telephones are thoroughly washed with a clean, slightly damp cloth. Pay special attention that moisture does not seep into the receiver.
所有电话应使用一块干净，微湿的抹布彻底清洁。特别小心不要将水弄到话筒内。
Ensure that dialing instructions, either clipped under the receiver or printed on a dial-pad cover, are always in good repair and legible, report any required repairs to Style Office.
确保电话指南（无论夹在听筒下或是印在拨号盘上）应保持完好并清晰易读，如需修补应通知客房部办公室。
After cleaning the telephone, position the receiver so that the cord runs along the front of the telephone set.
清洁完毕应将话筒放好，使电话线在话机前端。
Empty all trash containers located in the vicinity.
清空所有周边的垃圾箱。
Inspect all telephone directories for cleanliness, replace if required. Put the removed directories back in place.
检查所有电话簿，看其是否需要清洁或更换。将指定位置移走的电话簿放回原处。
Remark 备注
All telephones must be disinfected with disinfectant after cleaning.
所有电话清洁后，必须用消毒剂消毒。
