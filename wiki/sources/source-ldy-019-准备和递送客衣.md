# 准备和递送客衣（LDY_019）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_019 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All cleaned guest garments after carefully checking must be returned to guestroom on time: within 4 hours for Express Se（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- DND（请勿打扰）处理

## 目的

All cleaned guest garments after carefully checking must be returned to guestroom on time: within 4 hours for Express Service and as soon as possible after 6:00PM the same day for Regular Service.
所有干净客衣仔细检查后必须按时送回客人房间：加快洗衣4小时内送回，正常服务当天下午6点以后送返。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

●Folded Items:
衣物的折叠:
Collect the laundry list, on which the charges are already posted. Sort the lists into the pigeon boxes according to the room numbers.
收集已入账的洗衣单。根据房号顺序将洗衣单排放在存衣格内。
Prepare basket and laundry wrapping paper ensuring they are clean, pack the guest laundry and put them into the delivery basket.
准备客衣包装纸及干净送衣篮，将客衣包装后放入送衣篮。
Fold the garments not bigger than 25 x 25 cm approximately, with marking tape visible.
折叠衣物面积不超过25X25厘米，码条位置朝外。
Take out the garments and the list from the pigeon box once all items are completed.
当某房间衣物齐全时，将洗衣与洗衣单一同取出。
Place the trousers into the bottom of the basket first if guest need it folded, under wear could be put above them in good order. Then the folded T-shirts and shirts placed on the top.
如客人需要，西裤折叠后放置送衣篮底层，内衣整齐排列在上，最上面摆放折叠T恤或衬衫。袜子、内裤放最底层
After checking all the garments in basket and guest special instruction on list, ensure the laundry quantity correct.
边装篮边对照洗衣单检查件数及客人特别要求，确保衣物数量正确。
●Items on hangers:
挂件衣物:
Check the hanging items against the Laundry list.
根据洗衣单检查挂件衣物。
Shirts and neckties and Scarves should be hung in front, followed by jackets, T-shirts, scarves and trousers.
衬衫、领带挂在最前方，依次是：上衣、T恤、围巾、西裤。
Place the delivery card with room number noted on the hanger necks, with a rubber band holding around all hangers for one room.
将所有同一房号的衣架套上洗衣房号卡。
●Delivery laundry:
客衣的送返:
Prepare the valet delivery trolley and ensure it is clean and the wheels are in good working order.
准备干净送衣车，确保车轮正常。
Collect the baskets and the hanging items and place them in room number order on the trolley, to ensure fast delivery.
收集送衣篮及挂件，按顺序排列，确保更快送返。
Using service lift, deliver laundry to correct guestroom after checking room number with the laundry number card.
通过服务电梯至楼层，根据记录确认送衣房号。
The procedure of entering guest room is the same as for laundry collection with saying “Good morning / afternoon / evening. Mr. / Mrs. …Your laundry is ready” if the guest is in the room.
进房程序与收取客衣进房程序一样：“早上/下午/晚上好，XXX先生/女士，这是您的洗衣。”
Place the basket on the luggage rack in guestroom or on the armchair if the luggage rack is occupied.
将送衣篮放置房间床的右下角。
Laundry on hangers are attached with delivery card and have rubber band around them, to be hang on the right side of the wardrobe nearest to the bar counter.  (to be hang on the right side of the wardrobe in the living room in suites)
洗衣挂件上附有挂牌及洗衣单，统一挂于靠近吧台衣橱内的右侧。套房挂在衣帽间右边进门的衣橱。
If ‘Do Not Disturb’ sign is on the door - place Laundry DND card under the entrance door and later return garments to laundry. Guest could call for laundry delivery.
如果客人“请勿打扰”，从门下放入洗衣DND卡，洗衣部可根据客人要求随时提供送衣服务。
VIP guests will be provided Hotel Logo suit bags, etc.
贵宾洗衣挂件提供酒店标志无纺布西装套袋和衬衣套袋等。
