# 清洁的标准（Floor_003）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_003 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure consistent quality standard of cleanliness at the hotel.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 标准操作流程

## 目的

To ensure consistent quality standard of cleanliness at the hotel.
确保任何时候酒店清洁工作的持续性及质量标准。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that cleanliness standards must be established to identify the level and quality of cleanliness expected after an area or object has been cleaned. All employees engaged in providing cleaning services throughout hotel guest rooms will be trained on how to perform their cleaning assignment to meet and exceed following STANDARDS OF CLEANLINESS.
为酒店建立一个制度，以鉴定需清洁的区域或某一物品应达到要求的水平和质量标准。酒店所有提供清洁服务的相关员工将被培训，以使他们的清洁工作, 能达到并超过下列的清洁标准。
