# 房间清洁（Floor_026）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_026 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To provide consistent service to all in-house guests.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 吸尘器使用规范
- 客房清洁

## 目的

To provide consistent service to all in-house guests.
为所有住店的客人提供始终如一的服务。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to ensure consistency of the room cleaning standard.
确保客房清洁标准被执行。

## 程序

Press the doorbell three times, wait for a few seconds for guest response before entering the room.
按门铃3次，在进入客房前等几秒钟让客人反应。
Place the basket in the guest room.
将清洁篮拿进客房。
Open all curtains
打开所有窗帘
To let sun light come in.
让阳光照进来。
To see if curtains function properly.
看窗帘是否完好。
Room attendant should conduct a quick visual inspection whole room to make sure there are no any out-of-place devices that resemble a hidden camera.
客房服务员应该进行快速的视觉检查整个房间，确保没有不合适的设备，如摄像头
Test all lights, change light bulb if any fused, report for any light out of order.
检查所有的灯，如有灯泡损坏应更换，报修所有坏灯。
Remove room service tray to staff lift landing area. Check if no guest’s belongings left behind.
将送餐服务的托盘移至员工梯处。检查是否有客人物品留下。
Collect all rubbish and put into trash bag.
清理所有垃圾并装进垃圾袋。
Put stripped duvet and pillows on sofa. Put stripped bed sheets away. (make sure no guest’s item is thrown away together with bed sheet.)
将需更换的被子及枕头放在沙发上，将床单拿走。（确认拿走床单时没有客人物品夹在当中）
Start making up the bed.
开始做床。
Dusting procedure:
除尘程序：
To avoid oversight, it is easier to dust systematically clockwise or counter clockwise around the room by starting at the entrance door, dust objects around the room and end at the entrance door.
为避免疏漏，更简单的方法是顺时针或逆时针从客房门开始，有次序地绕客房打扫，最后在房门结束。
Door- room numbers, door lock, door frames (use a ladder if you cannot reach the top).
门-房号牌，门锁，门框（如果够不着可使用梯子）。
Closet - door, shelves, hangers, clothes rail and luggage rack.
壁橱-门，架子，衣架，挂衣杆及行李架。
Refrigerator - dust top and bottom of the fridge.
冰箱-顶部及底部。
Picture and television cabinet- dust all the frames and surfaces, drawers of television cabinet.
画像及电视柜-所有边框及表面，电视柜的抽屉。
Television set and underneath the set.
电视机及其下部。
Writing desk, mirror, drawers, desk lamp, lamp shade.
写字桌，镜子，抽屉，台灯，灯罩。
Window sills, especially behind window curtains.
窗台，特别是窗帘后的部分。
