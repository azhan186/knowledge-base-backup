# 洗衣入职培训（LDY_018）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_018 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All Laundry team members must be familiar with the layout and basic operation of the Laundry.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 客衣打码/标注防错
- 新员工入职培训

## 目的

All Laundry team members must be familiar with the layout and basic operation of the Laundry.
每位洗衣房员工必須熟悉洗衣房整体设备布局及基本运作流程。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Every new employee will be given an orientation of the laundry department.
酒店洗衣房对所有新入职的员工进行入职培训。
This will include the following:
包括以下内容：
Housekeeping and laundry department organization chat
客房部及洗衣房的组织结构
Job description
本职工作操作描述
Department rule and regulation
部门规章制度
Work safe
工作安全
LSOP & Skill knowledge
工作技能知识
Chemical knowledge
药剂知识
Laundry manning and posts introduction
洗衣房人员岗位介绍
Laundry sorting area, including marking area.
洗衣房分拣区域，包括打码区域。
The washing machines and the load capacities
水洗机的种类及装载量
The Dry-Cleaning machine operation
干洗机的基本操作
The spotting board and the techniques used to clean garments
去渍台的使用
The Flat work Ironer and the automatic folding machine for the linen. The different types of pressing machines
介绍烫平折叠机，介绍各类烫机
The working flow of Guest Laundry Service and linen/uniform washing
客衣服务及布草制服洗涤流程
The chemical room and chemicals introduction
洗衣房日常使用化料介绍及化料仓库位置
The location of the communications log book and operation trolley introduction
记录交接本存放地点，运营用的布草车/客衣车介绍
