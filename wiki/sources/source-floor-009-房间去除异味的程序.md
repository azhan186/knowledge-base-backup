# 房间去除异味的程序（Floor_009）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_009 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

The procedure of remove the bad smell in the guest room.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 房间除味/除烟味程序

## 目的

The procedure of remove the bad smell in the guest room.
房间去除异味的程序

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of how to remove the bad smell in the room
让客房部的所有员工都知道房间去除异味的程序

## 程序

Normal situation:
一般情况
When cleaning the room, employees found slight odor in the room, turned on the air conditioner, used air freshener and reported it to the supervisor.
员工打扫房间时发现房内有轻微异味，空调开大档，使用空气清香剂，并报告给主管。
Emergency measures should be taken when the supervisor's find strong bad smell in the room.
主管查房发现有浓烈异味时，应该采取应急措施
spray the four corners of the room with "odor remover" chemical.
使用“异味去除剂”喷洒在房间的4个角
use odor-removing machines, such as air purifier and ozone machine, to treat the room (this room status can’t release to vacant).
使用去除异味的机器，如：空气净化器、臭氧机对房间进行处理（此房间不可放可卖房）
Notes for using air purifier, ozone machine and dehumidifier:
使用空气净化器、臭氧机和抽湿机的注意事项
Open the ozone machine for half an hour, not allow to stay in the room, after half an hour into the room to open the air conditioning.
打开臭氧机放置时间为半小时，人不可待在房间，半小时过后再进房间把空调风速调大。
Use the dehumidifier. Drain the water in the dehumidifier every morning and night.
使用抽湿机，每天早晚各一次将抽湿机内的水倒掉。
Special situation:
特殊情况
When the guest is sensitive to the odor or the room bad smell can not to be removed, the bed linen & cushion in the room should be taken to the laundry room for drying and cleaning.
客人对异味比较敏感，或是房间浓重异味无法去除时，应将房内的棉织品拿到洗衣房烘一下并清洗。
Encounter special season, the room has musty smell or mildew, put the dehumidifier in the room to remove musty smell.
遇到特殊季节，房间有霉味或发霉，房内可放抽湿机去除霉。
