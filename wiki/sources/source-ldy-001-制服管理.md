# 制服管理（LDY_001）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_001 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Every team member should be provided with a neat set of the specified uniform, to enable him / her to perform his/her du（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 员工制服管理（领用/更换/盘点）

## 目的

Every team member should be provided with a neat set of the specified uniform, to enable him / her to perform his/her duties without any delay.
确保酒店每位员工及时更换制服，保持工作时间内良好工作形象。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

New team members on board collect uniform
新员工入职领用制服
The Human Resource Department will bring new hire employee who will be on board at next month to the uniform room in advance, so that the uniform room can prepare the corresponding uniforms.
人力资源部会提前带下月进店员工至制服房，以便制服房准备相应制服。
On board employee bring “Uniform Distribution Record” should go to Uniform Room to receive the uniforms (uniform, shoes, socks, headdress or jewelry, etc.).
新员工带着“制服发放表”至制服房领取上岗制服(制服、鞋、袜子、头饰或首饰等)。
Uniform Room Supervisor should issue uniform numbered according to “Uniform distribution record” and complete uniform card with signatures from each other.
制服房主管根据“制服发放表”核对员工情况，填写制服卡并双方签字，最后发放编号制服。
Replacement of staff uniform.
员工制服的日常更换
Team member must replace dirty uniform for clean ones within during the operation hour of the uniform / linen room.
规定时间内，员工更换制服，必须以脏换净。
Uniform member must fill in “RECEIVE & CHANGING UNIFORM LOGBOOK” when changing uniform.
更换制服时，制服间员工必须根据实际情况填写“收换制服记录单”。
When changing soiled uniform, both team member and uniform / linen room attendant should check soiled uniform to make sure that all items are complete without any tears or buttons missing etc.
更换脏制服时，双方必须检查脏制服，有无破损、少扣现象。
When changing clean uniform, both team member and uniform / linen room attendant should check clean uniform to make sure it’s in good condition. Return and mend the uniform immediately if necessary.
更换干净制服时，双方必须检查干净制服，有无破损、少扣现象，如有立即返回制服布草房处理。
If a uniform is lost, the team member must inform uniform / linen room immediately. If it is still unknown, uniform / linen room attendant should try to find where the uniform should be according to record. Then report it to the director of service, and report to concerned department head in a week if it still cannot be found.
员工一旦发现制服遗失，必须立即通知制服布草房。制服布草房立即核查架上制服及送洗记录，判断制服所在处，如仍然未发现，此事件必须报告给服务总监，并在一周内上报对方上级。
Every team member is responsible for his/her own uniform.
每位员工必须对自己的制服负责。
If it is confirmed that the Uniform is lost or not returned, the employee shall compensate for the uniform according to the hotel policy and procedure.
如果确定制服遗失、不归还，该员工需要按照酒店政策和流程来赔偿。
The employee (including outsourcing) is required to compensate the cost as follows:
员工（包括外包员工）制服赔偿如下所示：
Worked within 1 year                                    	      	 Pay for 100﹪
工作1年内                                                                	                  赔偿制服价的100﹪
Have been worked for 1 year to 2 years                                    Pay for 80﹪
工作1年-2年内                                                                                      赔偿制服价的80﹪
Have been worked over 2 years                                                                    Pay for 50﹪
工作超过2年                                                                                            赔偿制服价的50﹪
Employee internal support to another department
员工的借调
Using department should bring internal support employee to uniform room three days in advance, so that the uniform room can prepare the corresponding uniforms.
使用部门需提前三天带借调员工至制服房，以便制服房准备制服。
The employee should go to uniform room to collect uniforms, and sign on the uniform loan record.
员工至制服房领取上岗制服, 并在借用记录上签字确认
Finish supporting, the employee will return the uniform to the uniform room. And sign out on borrowing record
借调结束，员工将制服归还给制服房，核对无误后员工在借用记录上签还。
