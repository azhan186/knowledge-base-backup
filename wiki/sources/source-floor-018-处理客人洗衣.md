# 处理客人洗衣（Floor_018）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_018 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure employee know how to handling guest laundry.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- DND（请勿打扰）处理
- 客衣服务

## 目的

To ensure employee know how to handling guest laundry.
确保在任何时候服务员知晓如何处理客人洗衣。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of how to handling guest laundry.
建立关于处理客人洗衣的程序。

## 程序

Laundry bags/slips are available in all guest rooms.
洗衣袋/单在所有客房可用。
Check all the guest rooms on the assigned floor from 10am except “DND” room.
除“DND”房外，10点开始检查分配楼层所有客房有无送洗衣物。
Mark down on room attendant worksheet if the rooms are “DND”.
如客房挂出“DND”牌，在工作表上注明。
Put the laundry in the floor pantry and record on the worksheet.
将送洗衣物带到楼层备品间，并在工作表上做记录。
Call down to laundry department immediately.
立即通知洗衣房。
When Pick Up laundry, need pay attention as below:
收客衣时需注意的问题
Check guest room number, date and quantity on the laundry form
查看洗衣单上的房号，日期，数量。
Report to office immediately if guest request express service.
看清是否有加快服务和其他特殊要求，如果有要立即通知办公室。
If room is DL or DND, the room attendant can’t pick up laundry, The notice of did not pick up the laundry should be pass under the door to inform the guest.
如果遇反锁或DND的房间，服务员未能收取洗衣，需将未能收取洗
衣的通知塞在门缝下让客人知道
If having found the laundry without list of signatures, don’t remove from the room. Call Supervisor for reference. then the supervisor will to decide whether to pick up or not. And write on the work sheet.
如果有客衣，但无洗衣单或未填单，服务员要通知主管，由主管决
定是否收洗客衣。并在报表上写清有衣无单或有衣未填单。
If the guest or the front desk, AYS notice to pick up laundry, the room attendant found did not fill in the list can also accept, date and room number written by the room attendant. But if guest put do not disturb sign, the room attendant must inform office, after reconfirmation, and getting the guest agree then can knock on the door to collect laundry.
如果是客人或前台，AYS通知要收客衣，没有填写单子也可以收，由服务员写好日期，房号。但收取客衣时，门上挂请勿打扰，要打电话到办公室，再次确认后，得到客人确认后才可以敲门，收取客衣。
If room attendant has any question when collect the guest laundry, must report to supervisor, ask for help.
当服务员收取客衣时，有任何疑问，都必须向主管报告，不要自作主张，私自操作，避免发生客人投诉。
If room attendant made mistake to forgot collect laundry, must inform Supervisor immediately, decisions are made by the supervisor.
当服务员由于自己工作失误，造成漏收或其他差错，及时向主管报告，由主管做出定夺。
