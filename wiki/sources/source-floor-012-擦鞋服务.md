# 擦鞋服务（Floor_012）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_012 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To achieve guest satisfaction.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 擦鞋服务标准

## 目的

To achieve guest satisfaction.
使客人满意。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to provide 24 hours complimentary shoeshine service to our hotel guests. All items should be shined and return as soon as possible.
为客人提供24小时免费擦鞋服务。鞋应擦好后, 尽快交还客人。

## 程序

Guests placed shoes in the available shoeshine basket for service.
客人将鞋放入擦鞋篮内要求服务。
Room attendant will collect the item and mark down the room number on a piece of paper and placed it inside the shoe shine basket. Bring it to the floor pantry for service.
客房服务员收集后，记下房号在一张纸片上，并放入擦鞋篮，将其带入楼层备品间。
Clean leather shoes including shoe rim with wet dust cloth.
擦皮鞋时要用湿的鞋布将皮鞋的边沿擦干净
Re-clean all shoes with brush/cloth, then identify the colour carefully. Black shoe polish use for black colour shoes, and the rest use white shoes polish.
用鞋刷/布进行初步清洁，然后仔细分清颜色。黑色皮鞋用黑色鞋油，其他颜色皮鞋都用白色鞋油
Apply cream on shoes accordingly and shine them with shoe brush.
使用鞋油并用鞋刷将其擦亮。
Place all polished shoes put bedside shoeshine basket and return to the guest rooms.  (should not be put  back to shoe basket)
将擦好的鞋放入擦鞋篮, 并将之放回客房内。(注意不要放回鞋篮内)
Remark:  The employee must remember don’t to forgot to remove the note with room number from the shoe basket.
备注：员工切记不要忘了，将之前写有房号的纸条，从鞋篮内拿掉。
