# 分拣脏布草并洗涤（LDY_022）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_022 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All soiled linen from guestrooms and F&B outlets are to be carefully sorted into different categories using the methods （洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转
- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

All soiled linen from guestrooms and F&B outlets are to be carefully sorted into different categories using the methods outlined as below before being sent to the washing or dry-cleaning section.
任何客房、餐饮布草水洗前，必须先按照以下方法仔细分类再洗涤处理。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Soiled linens are to be sorted into the following classifications: similar colors, heavily soiled and types of fabrics/linens.
脏布草的分类根据：颜色、污迹程度及面料的性质。
The most of Linen washed for machine wash, only few ones for dry cleaning.
布草洗涤分水洗和干洗，大部分布草选择水洗，布草需要干洗的比较少。
The sorting soiled linen procedure as follows:
分配脏布草程序如下：
Stain pretreatment:  Steam wash (Black stain), Hand wash protein stain: (include blood, soy source), after stain pretreatment, then the soiled linen to be put together with others to machine wash.
污渍预处理:  蒸汽去渍枪去渍（黑色污渍）， 手洗（蛋白质污渍：包括血渍，酱油渍），把污渍预处理以后的布草和其它脏布草放一起机器洗涤。
Washing:  Cold water soluble soils(30°-50°) , Hot water-soluble soils(60°-80°). Usually dark colored linen are washed in cold water and light colored linen in hot water.
水洗：冷水(30°-50°)洗涤、热水(60°-80°)洗涤，通常深色布草选择冷水，浅色布草选择热水洗涤，水溶性污渍。
Dry Cleaning: only for oil stain removed, oil soluble soils. Usually the oil stain will be removed by high temperature washing with emulsified detergent, the linen is rarely dry cleaned.
干洗：仅用于油污洗涤，油溶性污迹。通常油渍污渍都可以用高温水洗加乳化剂去除，很少会用干洗洗涤布草。
After the completion of classifying into different categories, the soiled items are then sent to washing or dry-cleaning process.
布草经过仔细分类后再进行相应的处理。
