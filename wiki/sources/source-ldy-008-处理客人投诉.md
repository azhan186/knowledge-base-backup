# 处理客人投诉（LDY_008）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_008 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Any guest laundry complaints must be listened to carefully, recorded in detail and apologized for before handling or rep（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 客人投诉处理 SOP

## 目的

Any guest laundry complaints must be listened to carefully, recorded in detail and apologized for before handling or reporting
客人任何洗衣投诉，服务员必须首先仔细倾听，安慰客人并详细记录，再处理或汇报上级。

## 政策规定

Ensure employee are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

To make it clear: when guest complains, team member must make it clear and handle it in different way according to situation.
弄清情况：客人投诉时，服务员应弄清情况，区别对待。听清楚客人投诉什么。
Guest complains that the laundry has not been returned.
案例分析：客人投诉没有收到客衣：
Let guest calm down and let him/her know that we will check it immediately and ask him/her about the date on which the laundry was sent.
让客人冷静下来，让他/她知道我们会马上检查，并询问洗衣日期。
Check the record immediately, to make sure that the guest had sent the garments, and then check the runner’s record to make sure that the garments had already been returned to the room.
立即查对洗衣记录，确认确有此份客人洗衣。查对送衣记录，确认该份洗衣已送进房间。
Contact with the guest and let him/her know that we had received and returned the garments at XXX time. Remind the guest to look into the wardrobe. If the guest still cannot find the garments, check the laundry sheet to see if there is any miss-delivery. Look into the items of the guest laundry, analysis and check the similar room number and similar items to find if there is any miss-delivery, if there is, return it and apologize to the guest immediately. If not, report it to the Laundry manager at once.
与客人联系，告之客人确已收到其洗衣，并已经在XXX时间送回提醒客人查找衣橱。如客人回答仍没有，说明有可能衣物送错房间，核查洗衣单，根据客人所洗衣服，分析可能送错的房间（相近的房号，相近的洗衣内容项目）立即到房间查找，如确系送错，立即送还客人并向客人道歉，如仍末找到必须立即向经理汇报
Guest complains that some items are missing：
客人抱怨部分洗衣未收到
Let the guest calm down, and let him/her know that we will check it immediately, and ask him/her which items are missing.
先请客人不要着急，告诉立即为其查对，同时问清客人缺少什么衣服
Check the laundry sheet to make sure that the guest had sent the garments for washing.
查对洗衣单确认客人确洗过此类衣服
Check the runner’s sheet to make sure that the garments had been returned to the room.  (It is most likely that the guest has not found it)
查对送衣记录，进一步确认此类衣服已送回房间。（这时很可能是客人未发现）
Contact with the guest by telephone to remind him/her to look into the closet or do it by yourself after getting the permission from the guest.
与客人电话联系，提醒客人查找衣橱或征得客人同意后，前往房间帮助查找。
If still cannot find the items, report it to the GRM and Laundry manager at once.
如仍未找到，立即向宾客服务经理和经理汇报
Guest complains that the garments from laundry have been damaged.
客人投诉衣服被洗坏时
Let the guest calm down and tell him/her that it will be reported to GRM and laundry manager immediately.
先请客人不要生气，同时告诉客人此情况会立即报告宾客服务经理和洗衣房经理
Check the garments damage record to find out if the garment had been damaged before washing but the guest had not discovered. You can even take back the garments after getting permission from the guest.
查对洗衣损坏洗前问题确认记录，核查是否衣物洗前就有破损（衣物已经退回到客人房间），而客人未注意到，在征得客人确认后同意后送回取走问题衣物，再洗涤。
After the manager or supervisor on duty looking through the case, if it is the guest’s cause, explain it patiently to the guest. If the guest does not accept it, report it to the duty manager. If it is due to laundry causes, apologize to the guest sincerely and consult the settlement (compensation or mending) with the guest to reach the guest satisfaction. Find out the event reason and submit an accident report to the manager.
还可将衣服取回查看。经理或值班领班了解全部情况后，如属客人原因，耐心向其解释,如客人不接受，汇报值班经理，如属洗衣房操作不当，必须与客人协商解决办法（赔偿或修理）并查明洗坏原因，做出处理，确保宾客满意，同时完成事故报告汇报经理。
Every guest complaint resolved or not must be reported to the laundry manager.
客人投诉的任何问题，不管服务员已处理的或未处理的均应向洗衣房经理报告。
如果衣服被送错房间，不能肯定被送错的衣物在哪个房间，需要立刻通知宾客关系经理，洗衣房配合宾客关系经理处理投诉，提供必要的信息，及时弥补。
