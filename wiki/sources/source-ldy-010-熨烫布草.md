# 熨烫布草（LDY_010）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_010 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All linen must be checked for cleanliness during pressing with no or as few creases as possible.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转
- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

All linen must be checked for cleanliness during pressing with no or as few creases as possible.
所有床单类的布草熨烫时，必须仔细检查洗涤效果，并确保面料无褶皱或较少褶皱。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements.
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作。

## 程序

Ensure that the machine is in perfect working condition before any operation. Inform Laundry shift leader as soon as possible if there is any fault.
检查烫机工作状态，如有问题，立即通知洗衣房主管/经理。
The flatwork ironer must be preheated and maximum steam supply has to be ensured, before any items can be fed through it.
大烫机在熨烫任何物品之前，机器必须完全预热，蒸汽供应充足。150度以上
Prepare the washed articles according to their sizes and colors, then feed through flat into the ironer.  Sort and put aside badly stained linen for treatment and reject any torn ones and those from the flatwork ironer with folding marks on them.
根据洗涤物品的颜色及尺寸依次熨烫。发现污渍布草挑出处理。熨烫过程中发现破损布草立即折角做出记号，布草出大烫机后挑出。
Small items such as napkins and pillowcases should be prepared flat so as to feed in machine continuously.
小件物品口布、枕套等烫前需抖开放平整理再依次送大烫机熨烫。
The chest and the roller padding have to be cleaned daily by pressing the recommended cloth with applying wax powder on it at least 3 times every morning before operation.
每天早晨熨烫前在垫布上撒蜡粉，送入大烫机熨烫至少三次，充分润滑身。适度不能太多
Remark 备注:
Female employees must wear hair net, prevent hair from getting caught up in the belt conveyor.
女员工必须佩带头花网兜，防止头发卷进皮带输送带。
When the flat iron machine is blocked, the power must be turn off, then the linen can be removed.
一旦发生大烫机或折叠机堵住时，必须先关闭机器电源，然后再取出布草。
所有大平烫员工都要知道大平烫机所有熨烫程序，紧急停止按钮，电眼感应，以及会换引带。
每次换好滚筒毛毡，需要登记好时间，以便计算下次更换毛毡。
