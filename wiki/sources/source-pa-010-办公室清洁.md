# 办公室清洁（PA_010）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_010 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All offices should be cleaned and kept tidy daily.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 吸尘器使用规范
- 办公室清洁

## 目的

All offices should be cleaned and kept tidy daily.
确保酒店所有办公室每天保持清洁

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Daily Cleaning
日常清洁
Have all necessary tools at hand before doing the cleaning in offices.
准备好清洁办公室所需的全部工具。
Knock on the door politely; you can enter when you get permission.
有礼貌地敲门经同意后方可进入
Empty all rubbish bins and replenish the rubbish bags.
收集全部的垃圾, 更换垃圾袋
Vacuum from inside to outside, including the table and chair under
吸尘从内至外，包括台子及椅子下面。
Waiting for a while if found somebody on the phone. Wait for call is over, ask if can vacuum. When get positive reply, can start vacuuming.
吸尘前如发现有人在通话中，需稍等片刻。等通话结束后，询问是否可以吸尘。得到肯定的答复，方可开始吸尘。
Found chair and table leg with black mark must clean in time when vacuum the floor.
吸尘时发现椅脚和台脚有黑印需及时清洁。
If found stain on the carpet, clean it immediately.
地毯上小面积的污渍，及时去污处理。
Pay attention to the internal guest’s personal habits: do not move things etc.
留意内部客人个人习惯
Report any maintenance issues or carpet for shampoo to PA Supervisor as soon as possible.
有设备维修或清洗地毯，及时报告给PA领班
GENERAL CLEAN
计划清洁
Wipe the doors and doorframes with cleaning cloth.
用抹布清洁门及门框
Clean windows, windowsills and floor wooden skirting boards.
清洁窗、窗台、地角线
Keep the power socket, switches and wallpaper clean.
保持开关和墙布的清洁
Clean the desk divider
办公桌隔断挡板清洁
Telephone cleaning and disinfection
电话清洁消毒
Vacuum fully, including the areas under the furniture.
全面吸尘。包括家具下面、沙发缝隙
Carpet for shampoo
地毯清洗
Clean ceiling, vent and window regularly.
定期清洁高空天花板、空调口.玻璃及其它大清洁项目。
