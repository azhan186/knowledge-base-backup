# 客房服务员关门打扫房间的标准（Floor_013）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_013 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure guest room door security when cleaning service is in progress. And housekeeping cleaning standard for guestroo（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 关门清洁标准

## 目的

To ensure guest room door security when cleaning service is in progress. And housekeeping cleaning standard for guestroom closed door cleaning
确保在客房清洁工作进行当中客房门的安全性。以及客房服务员关门清洁客房的标准。

## 政策规定

Standard for servicing of guestrooms with guestroom door opened, or closed during servicing, with guest absent or present
客房服务员在打扫房间时关闭房门的标准，客人在与不在的操作流程。

## 程序

No guest in the room:
客人不在房间内
Guestroom door must remain closed during the cleaning process.
客房门在清扫过程中必须保持关闭。
Housekeeper tools and supplies must be placed inside the guestroom that the housekeeper is working in.
客房服务员在做房间时必须将工具及物品放在房间里面。
Place a sign on the door handle (TIDYING UP) stating that the room is being serviced
在门把手上挂“悉心打扫”的牌子，表明工作正在进行中。
Guest in the room
客人在房间内
If guest in the room, the guestroom door must be kept open. If the guest has any objection, the employee should explain in time. If at any time the housekeeper is uncomfortable servicing the room in the presence of the guest, that housekeeper should exit the room and report it immediately
如果客人在房间，客房门必须保持开启。如果客人有异议的，员工需及时解释。如果服务员打扫时客人有不当行为，客房服务员应该立即离开房间并及时报告。
If a guest enters their guestroom while a housekeeper is open or close the door, the housekeeper must double check the guest room key and ask when would be a more convenient time to complete the servicing of the room. The housekeeper should then excuse themselves from the room and return at the requested time. However, should the guest request the housekeeper continue servicing the room, the guestroom door must be kept open.
当客房服务员开关客人房门时，客人要进入客房，客房服务员必须试客人的房卡并询问何时再方便打扫。确认时间后客房服务员应及时离开房间，在规定的时间内返回。但是，如果客人要求服务员继续打扫时，客房门必须保持开启。
In all circumstances, whenever a housekeeper is in a guestroom with a guest or a second associate, the guestroom door must be kept open
在任何情况下，每当客房服务员与客人或第二同事在一起时，客房门必须保持开启。
If guest ask Housekeeper to open a locked room door because they either have lost their key or are having trouble opening a door, the Housekeeper should direct guest to the front desk to get another key and call HSKP office to report it.
如果有客人要求客房服务员打开房门, 是因为他们丢失了钥匙或很难打开门，客房服务员要礼貌地请客人，直接到前台办理新的房卡并打电话给办公室。
Notes:
Whenever you depart a guestroom, the door MUST be secured. Whether going to pantry for additional items, or leaving the room completely for that time, you must ensure that the door is securely closed.
无论何时离开客房时，门必须安全关上。无论是到备品间拿备品，或是完全离开房间，你必须确保门是安全关上的。
Should you FIND that a guestroom door a jar or propped open,  IMMEDIATELY Knock and check to see if there are any guests in, if they are not in the room,  inform HSKP clerk and close the door directly. Housekeeping clerk inform GRM and Security duty supervisor after received call. The room attendant not allowed to enter the room to clean until received any instructions.
当发现客房门是开的时不要进入！立即敲门并查看是否有客人，如果客人不在房内，关门并通知客房部办公室文员。办公室文员再通知大堂经理及保安部当班主管。员工在未接到任何指示前，不得进入房间打扫。
