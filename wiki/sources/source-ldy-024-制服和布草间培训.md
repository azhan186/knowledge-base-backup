# 制服和布草间培训（LDY_024）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_024 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All uniform/linen room team members should be familiar with the uniform/linen room layout and basic operation.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转
- 员工制服管理（领用/更换/盘点）
- 新员工入职培训

## 目的

All uniform/linen room team members should be familiar with the uniform/linen room layout and basic operation.
制服布草员工必须熟知制服布草房工作流程及设施布局。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Every new uniform room employee will be given an orientation and working flow overview of the uniform/linen room.
酒店制服房的新员工会给予入职培训及工作流程。
This will include the following:
包括以下内容：
Hotel uniform information
酒店制服情况介绍
Hotel linen information
酒店布草情况介绍
Uniform/linen room articles for loan
制服布草房物品借用
Any loan of uniforms needs recording and signatures of uniform owner, borrower and uniform/linen room attendant.
制服的借用：登记，制服拥有人，借用人及制服房员工签字。
Lending of articles needs recording and permission from the uniform/linen room shift leader or laundry shift leader.
其他物品的借用：登记，物品借用人，制服房领班或洗衣房值班领班及制服房服务员签字。
Washing of hotel articles
酒店物品的洗涤
Articles needing washing, such as uniforms, linen etc. must be received by uniform/linen room first then handed over to laundry for processing.
日常物品的洗涤：制服布草房首先接收再交接至洗衣房洗涤 。
Periodic washing of articles, such as shower curtains, room curtains, table wool/silk skirts etc. should be informed to the uniform/linen room one week in advance for decision of exact and proper time.
物品周期洗涤：浴帘、窗帘、桌裙等需定期清洁的物品，使用部门必须提前一周通知制服房，双方决定送洗时间。
Any article issued from the uniform/linen room should have a detailed record.
制服布草房内任何物品的发放需有详细记录。
Laundry service for hotel management
酒店管理层洗衣服务
Uniform/linen room shift leader working tasks
制服布草主管工作任务
Uniform/linen room attendant working tasks
制服布草服务员工作任务
