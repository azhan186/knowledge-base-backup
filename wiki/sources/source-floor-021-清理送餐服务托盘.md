# 清理送餐服务托盘（Floor_021）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_021 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure NO tray in the hallway in order to provide a clean perception to our guests.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 送餐托盘回收

## 目的

To ensure NO tray in the hallway in order to provide a clean perception to our guests.
确保走廊上没有托盘，以使客人有一个洁净的感觉。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that our hotel guest sees everyone working in the hotel as one member of whole team, trays in the hallway make the hallway look dirty. Therefore, it is not only room service responsibility to clean it. Let’s work as ONE TEAM!
使客人将每一个在酒店工作的员工看作是酒店整体的一员。在走廊上如果有托盘的话会看起来很脏乱，因此清理它们不仅仅是送餐部的工作，让我们象一个团队一样工作吧！

## 程序

Tray removal is a simple procedure, but keep the following in mind to avoid any trouble:
清理托盘是一项简单的程序，但为避免麻烦要记住以下几点：
All trays must be removed from guest rooms and hallways to the staff lift landing area.
将所有托盘从客房及走廊移至员工梯。
Liquids should be dumped in the toilet to reduce weight and the chance of spilling when removing trays from guest rooms. Throw leftover food into the trash bag.
在将托盘从客房移走前，应将饮料拿到洗手间倒掉以减少重量并避免液体泼洒。将吃剩的食物导入垃圾袋内。
Place items towards the centre of the tray to help balance the weight.
将托盘上的物品移到中间以保持平衡。
Remove some plate and take them to the staff lift landing area separately if the tray is too heavy.
如果托盘上面餐盘过多过重，需分批将托盘及餐盘移至员工梯。
Use proper lifting techniques at all times when handling trays.
处理托盘时使用合理的托盘技术。
Call the room service to take away the trays
通知送餐服务收盘子
