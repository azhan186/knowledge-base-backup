# 客房洗浴用品瓶子清洁程序（Floor_041）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_041 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

It’s crucial for the Housekeeping Team to follow proper cleaning procedure of the Residential Bath Amenities during dail（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 客房洗浴用品管理

## 目的

It’s crucial for the Housekeeping Team to follow proper cleaning procedure of the Residential Bath Amenities during daily Housekeeping Cleaning.  Associate need to familiarize the cleaning procedure
在日常的客房清洁中，客房部团队遵循适当的洗浴设施清洁程序是至关重要的。员工需要熟知清洁洗浴瓶的流程

## 政策规定

Follow the correct procedure for cleaning bottled bath products in guest rooms
按照正确的流程清洁客房瓶装沐浴用品

## 程序

Below are the cleaning procedures for bottled toiletries in guest rooms：
以下是客房内瓶装洗浴用品的清洁程序：
Check: During stay over housekeeping service and upon checkout, the Room Attendant should check if the bath amenity level reaches the replacement line, damaged or missing. Room Attendant should replace the bottle if any of these scenarios occur.
检查：在打扫在住客房间或退房，客房服务员应检查瓶装沐浴用品的剩余量是否达到更换线、是否损坏或遗失。如果出现上述任何一种情况，服务员应更换瓶装用品。
Test: If the bath amenity level does not reach the replacement line and the bottle condition is fine, Room Attendant should test all pumps to ensure the nozzle work. Replace a new bottle if the pump is not working.
测试：如果瓶装洗浴用品剩余量未达到更换线且瓶装用品的状态良好无损坏，客房服务员需要测试所有瓶子的按压泵，以确保喷嘴能正常工作。若不能正常使用，请更换新瓶。
Disinfection and Rinse: Spray the approved Bathroom Cleaning Chemical on the bottles, brackets and nozzles as part of shower room cleaning. Allow time for the Cleaning Chemical to rest on bottle (Refer to the product label instructions on timing). Scrub the bottles, brackets and nozzles gently and pay attention to the nozzle to ensure no build-up. Rinse with water.
消毒和冲洗：作为冲淋房清洁环节的一部分，使用经过批准的浴室清洁药剂喷洒在瓶身、支架和喷嘴上。让清洁药剂在瓶身上停留一段时间（请参照产品标签上有关时间的说明）。轻轻地擦拭瓶身、支架和喷嘴，并确保喷嘴处没有残留物。再用清水冲洗即可。
Dry: Wipe the bottles, brackets and nozzles with a dry and clean cloth.  Follow the Asia Pacific Continent HI-5 Standards.
擦干：用干净的干布擦拭瓶身、支架和喷嘴。具体请参考亚太地区HI-5标准。
Prime: Fill the pumps with the bath amenities.  If pumps have an excess product in the nozzle, ensure the nozzle is cleaned accordingly.
准备好：向瓶装沐浴用品中补充对应产品。如果喷嘴上有多余的用品，请确保喷嘴清洁干净。
Final Check: Room Attendant should ensure the bottles are installed in this order from left to right: (1) Shampoo, (2) Conditioner, (3) Shower Gel. All pumps forward to the same direction as well as the bath amenity above the replacement line.
最终检查：客房服务员应确保瓶子按照从左到右的相应顺序摆放:(1) 洗发水，(2) 护发素，(3) 沐浴露。所有喷嘴都朝同一方向，且所有用品剩余量都在更换线以上。
