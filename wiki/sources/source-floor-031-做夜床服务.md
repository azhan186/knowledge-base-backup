# 做夜床服务（Floor_031）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_031 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To achieve guest satisfaction and loyalty.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 夜床（Turn-down）服务标准
- DND（请勿打扰）处理

## 目的

To achieve guest satisfaction and loyalty.
使客人满意并提高对酒店的忠诚度。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that to provide a comfortable and “ready-to-rest” impression to our guests upon their return in the evening. Turndown service will be provided between 5:30p.m.-9:30p.m.
为晚间回到房间的客人提供一个舒适和“准备休息”的印象。做夜床应在下午5:00至晚上8:00进行。

## 程序

Check all lights and replace burned-out bulbs if necessary.
检查所有灯，换掉烧坏的灯泡。
Empty the waste bin and take away dirty towel（no need change the towel hang on towel rack.
收垃圾及脏毛巾（但挂在毛巾架上不用更换）。
Close the curtains/drapes in the bedroom.
拉上关掉卧室的窗帘。
Turn down the bed.
做夜床。
Miter the bed sheets up with the duvet as a corner shape for king size bed.
(turndown at one side of window).
大床-将床单连同被子拉起成一个斜角。(做靠窗床头柜的一侧)。
The location of the turndown should be based on the guest's preference, if we
know.
开夜床的位置必须根据客人的喜好，如果知道的话。
If room stay with two guests, it needs do the both sides.
如果房间住两位客人，将两边都要开。
Put all pillows down on the mattress. (side by side)
将所有枕头靠放在床垫上。（并排放）
Fully cover goodnight towel on the bed, "good night" font is face the bed.
将晚安巾铺平在床边，“晚安”字体正对床边。
Take out the slippers and hold up the flattened uppers of the slippers, then put the
slippers are middle of the “goodnight” mat.
取出拖鞋，将压扁的鞋面撑起，最后将拖鞋平放在晚安巾的中间。
Placed mineral water、drink glass and coaster on the bedside table of turndown
side (oblique 45)
将矿水、水杯及杯垫放在开夜床一侧的床头柜上（斜45度）
Place the weather forecast card and designed turndown amenity on the turndown
tray, then put it top of the bed according to the	standard.
依据标准在床上放置天气预报卡及夜床礼物。
Clean the room and waste bin.
打扫房间和垃圾桶。
Clear the melted ice in the ice bucket, if any.
清理冰桶中已经融化的冰。
Remove all room service trays and tables to the pantry from guestroom.
将所有的送餐服务托盘及桌子从客房移走并带至员工梯区域。
Change the used drinking glasses and return to places. (if find liquid in the glasses,
do not clear, and cover it with coaster).
更换所有使用过的玻璃杯并放回原处。（如发现杯中仍有饮料，则不要清洗
而是用杯垫将其盖住）。
Replace the used towels and hang correctly.
