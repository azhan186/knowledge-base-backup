# 客房服务员自放房资格政策（Floor_044）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_044 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

The policy combined with room cleaning incentive policy to further mobilize room attendant’s morale and passion, improve（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 客房服务员自放房资格政策

## 目的

The policy combined with room cleaning incentive policy to further mobilize room attendant’s morale and passion, improve work efficiency and cleanliness quality and better control the labor costs, reduce room attendant turnover rate.
该政策与客房清洁激励政策相结合，进一步调动客房服务员的士气和热情，提高工作效率和清洁质量，更好地控制人力成本，降低客房服务员的流失率。

## 政策规定

Standardize the application for the qualification of employees' self-release housing and the assessment process to reduce the staff turnover rate.
规范员工自放房资格申请，以及考核流程，降低员工流失率。

## 程序

Qualification Requirement 资格要求
Room Attendant who meets below requirements is entitled room release qualification and will get extra incentive payments per month accordingly. For qualified Room Attendants, they will sign room release & performance review agreement before getting qualification. The specific requirements are as below:
符合以下要求的客房服务员可获得自放房资格，并按月获得额外奖励。对于合格的客房服务员，在获得资格之前，他们将签署房间释放和绩效考核协议。具体要求如下：
More than six month with same position experience in Hotel.
加入本酒店工作半年以上并一直从事相同性质工作。
No any complaints happened in the last half year and didn’t get any warning letter within a year.
最近半年内没有客人投诉发生，一年内没有获得过失单的。
Room cleanliness quality and working attitude get finally approved by supervisors in the past year.
在过去的一年内房间清洁质量和工作态度获得主管最终认可的。
Qualified room attendant must be in charge of routine and clean work in their designated floor, and assist supervisor, keeping amenity, helping trainee for problem solution
获得自放资格的员工，需要全面负责自己管辖区域的清洁工作和日常事务，并配合主管协调好工作，帮助新员工解决工作中的困难。
Performance Review 绩效考核
To ensure room cleanliness quality and better departmental operation, HSKP will continuously check & motivate  Room Attendants who got room release qualification, encourage more Room Attendants to  actively participate in and get career development.
为了保证客房清洁质量和部门更好的运作，客房部将持续检查和激励获得自放房资格的客房服务员，鼓励更多的客房服务员积极参与并获得职业发展。
Conduct monthly performance review for Room Attendant who is authorized to release room, according to room cleanliness quality  and working attitude, HSKP department has right to decide whether they have room release qualification for next month.
每月对被授权自放房的客房服务员进行绩效考核，根据客房清洁质量和工作态度，客房部有权决定其是否具有下个月的放房资格。
Floor supervisor will check room cleanliness randomly every day, Room Attendant who more than 2 rooms per month is not up to standard; HSKP department will cancel room release qualification for next month.
每日所属区域主管会对自放房员工房间进行抽查，每月抽查的房间有2间以上不符合清洁标准的，客房部将会取消其下个月自放房资格。
Guest complaint was caused by Room Attendant’s negligence, according to the actual circumstances, HSKP department has right to decide whether cancel room release qualification and incentive payments for this month.
自放房员工由于过失造成客人投诉的，客房部根据其情节严重程度有权决定是否立即取消其自放房资格和取消当月奖励。
Room Attendant who has been canceled room release qualification will not be entitled this qualification within the following three months; HSKP department will give reassessment according to his or her work performance after three months.
凡被取消自放房资格的员工三个月内不得拥有自放房资格，客房部将在三个月后根据其工作表现重新给予评估。
Room Attendant who applies for resignation, HSKP will cancel room release qualification and monthly incentive payments immediately.
凡员工提出离职申请，客房部将立即取消其自放房资格和当月自放房奖励。
Incentive Policy 奖励政策
The purpose is to appropriately reduce Floor Supervisor manning, encourage more Room Attendants to take part in HSKP daily management and improve work efficiency.
目的是适当减少楼层主管的人员编制，鼓励更多的员工参与日常管理和提高工作效率。
For Room Attendant who is entitled room release qualification will get 300RMB incentive payments per month which pay together with salary at the end of month.
获得自放房资格的客房服务员每月将获得300元人民币的奖金，并在月底与当月工资一起发放。
During the period of getting extra incentive payments, guest complaints caused by Room Attendant’s negligence, according to the actual circumstances HSKP department has right to decide whether cancel their room release qualification or extra incentive payments.
对于获得自放房资格的客房服务员，如果由于服务员的过失造成客人投诉，客房部根据其情节严重程度有权决定是否取消其当月奖金和自放房资格。
For outstanding performance, Room Attendant will be priority to give promotion as Floor    Supervisor.
对于表现优异的自放房员工将给予优先考虑提升为楼层主管。
Hotel management can adjust the policy according to real operation condition.
酒店管理层可根据酒店的实际运作情况对该政策做调整。
After Room Attendant confirmed and signed room release agreement, it will have the same effect as labor contract, employees should strictly obey it.
自放房协议经客房服务员签字确认后，将作为劳动合同的附件，具有与劳动合同同等的效力，员工应当严格遵守。
