# 吊灯清洁（PA_006）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_006 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Chandeliers should be clean and shining at all times.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 灯具清洁

## 目的

Chandeliers should be clean and shining at all times.
使酒店里的吊灯保持高标准的清洁水准

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Spraying
喷洗
Switch off the power.
关闭电源
Lay the appropriate discarded linen under the chandelier.
在吊灯下面铺好报废布草。
Dilute the glass cleaner (ammonia inclusive) at 1:20. Keep the diluted cleaner in a sprinkling pot.
稀释玻璃清洁剂（有氨） 1：20倒入喷壶中
Spray the cleaner from top to bottom of the chandelier, standing on a safety ladder.
架好梯子（固定好）由上至下将清洁剂喷在吊灯表面
Let it take effect for a while, then spray it again with clean water to wash away the remaining cleaner, and then wipe it dry.
待反应一会后在喷壶中加入清水，冲洗吊灯将吊灯表面的清洁剂冲洗干净。用干抹布
擦干吊灯上的水。
Cleaning
彻底清洗
Prepare all cleaning materials and equipment-bucket, glass cleaner, ladder, and a clean rug.
准备清洗吊灯所需的工具－水桶、玻璃清洁剂、梯子、干净抹布
Fix the ladder, and remove the chains of the crystal chandelier one by one.
架好梯子，摘下水晶灯链
Dilute the cleaner in the bucket, and put the chains into the cleaner solution.
在水桶中稀释玻璃清洁剂，将灯链泡在其中
Wash chains and the chandelier with clean water again and dry it. Then return the chains properly.
洗净灯链并擦干，用干抹擦干灯具。将灯链按原位挂好。
Note： 注意事项
when using a ladder, safety- precautions must be followed, i.e. assistance to hold the ladder etc.
当使用梯子时，一定要注意安全。例如：有人在梯子旁守护。
all cleaning operation high more than 1 meter, it should work by night shift of the outsourcing company, hotel staff can’t do the high-altitude cleaning operations
所有一米高空清洁作业都由夜班外包公司负责，酒店员工不得私自进行高空清洁作业。
