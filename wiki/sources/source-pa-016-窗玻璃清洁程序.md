# 窗玻璃清洁程序（PA_016）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_016 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All windows and mirrors should have an unobstructed and sparkling view and without smears.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 窗玻璃清洁

## 目的

All windows and mirrors should have an unobstructed and sparkling view and without smears.
确保酒店公共区域的玻璃和镜子干净

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Install glass scraper and glass squeegee.
组装玻璃铲刀及玻璃刮刀。
Remove stains on the glass with glass shovel.
先用玻璃铲刀将玻璃上的附着性污渍去除
Lay the discarded linen on the floor under the window of the area to be cleaned.
将报废布草横铺在所要清洁区域下面
Place notice signs around cleaning area.
在清洁区域周围设置告示牌。
Make appropriate glass cleaning water in accordance with the stains.
根据污垢程度配制玻璃水
Immerse the strip washer in the diluted glass cleaner; absorb it (no more dripping). Apply on the glass surface evenly. Note: Do not spread it in a big area, to avoid it getting dry before squeezing. Dilute the glass cleaner with hot water in winter.
将涂水毛套放入配制好的玻璃水中蘸湿，挤干（不滴水为止），均匀的涂在玻璃表面，注意一次不要涂的面积过大，以免风干。冬季要使用热水配制清洁剂。
When window cleaning is completed, wipe the frame and sill, and return all cleaning equipment to the correct storage area in the storeroom.
每刮完一块玻璃后，用抹布擦干窗框及窗台上的水迹，将工具洗净后放回库房指定的位置。
Hotel Outside Window Cleaning will be done by Outside Wall Vendor 4 times a year.
酒店外墙玻璃清洁外包,基本每年4次.
