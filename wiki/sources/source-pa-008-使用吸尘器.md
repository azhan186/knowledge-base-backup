# 使用吸尘器（PA_008）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_008 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Vacuum cleaner is a primary tool for a PA Attendant, it is her responsibility for keeping the vacuum cleaner in good con（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 吸尘器规范使用

## 目的

Vacuum cleaner is a primary tool for a PA Attendant, it is her responsibility for keeping the vacuum cleaner in good condition. Cannot do a good job without proper tools.
吸尘器是公区服务员的基本工具, 服务员有责任保持吸尘器的良好状态。没有合适的工具就不能做出良好的工作。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to ensure hotel property is well- kept at all times in order to achieve top performance standard as well as personal safety.
确保酒店财产被很好地维护，以达到最佳的工作表现，同时有助于人身安全。

## 程序

Check the plug, flexible tube, dust bag and vacuum system before starting vacuuming. Check for any loose parts or damaged.
在使用前检查插头、软管、尘袋、吸尘系统。看是否有地方松动或损坏。
Plug in socket nearest to entrance, loose wire, move machine to room corner, take up curtains, and pick up big pieces of rubbish.
将插头插入最近的插座，放开电线，将机器移到房间角落，打开窗帘，捡起较大的垃圾。
Switch on machine to start vacuuming from inside to outside, one side after another, do not omit any area. Make sure go under furniture and move backward towards entrance to avoid foot prints.
打开机器从由里向外一行一行吸尘，避免遗漏。确保家具下被打扫到，并向门的方向倒退着作业以防止留下脚印。
Remove spots or stains immediately when found (or record it and remove it after vacuuming).
发现污渍立即去除(也可以记录下来吸完再去渍)
Stop the machine immediately and report to supervisor if you find something unusual (machine is too hot, strange noise or smell or low-vacuum effect).
吸尘时发现异常情况(机身热、异声、异味、吸力弱)立即停止，并报告主管。
Do not vacuum water or broken glass, which will damage the engine and dust bag.
不可吸水或玻璃碎片，以免损伤电机或尘袋。
Be sure to keep back straight.
背挺直。
During vacuuming, do not drag the flexible tube and wire too hard, to avoid damage to the joint and plug.
在清洁时，不要用力拉吸尘软管和电线，避免损坏接口和插头。
Empty dirt bag and clean machine afterwards at daily.
每天清空吸尘袋，然后清洁机器。
Use another machine to vacuum the dust bag weekly. Clean the rake; wipe the cylinder and wire with a duster.
每周对吸。清洁吸尘扒头，并用抹布擦滚筒内壁和电线。
Except the cord, nothing is allowed to be hanging out on the cover of the vacuum machine.
除编码外，吸尘器表面不得有任何张贴物
