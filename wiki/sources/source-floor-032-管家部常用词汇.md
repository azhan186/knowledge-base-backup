# 管家部常用词汇（Floor_032）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_032 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Accepted abbreviations used throughout the hotel that specifically refer to the housekeeping department. Should be known（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- DND（请勿打扰）处理

## 目的

Accepted abbreviations used throughout the hotel that specifically refer to the housekeeping department. Should be known by all housekeeping associates.
所有管家部员工在工作中, 对管家部的常用词汇可以熟练地运用。

（未提取到政策字段）
