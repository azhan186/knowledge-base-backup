# 墙纸及墙布的清洁（PA_020）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_020 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure guests have a comfort & clean room to stay - delight customer.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 墙纸/墙布清洁

## 目的

Ensure guests have a comfort & clean room to stay - delight customer.
确保客人逗留期间感到舒适和干净-让顾客满意

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

How to Clean wall cloth、WALL-PAPER
怎样清洁墙布和墙纸
First, determine the type of stain and use the correct agents and cleaning tools.
首先确定污渍种类，使用正确的药剂和清洁工具。
When discovering besmirch, use wet dishcloth to remove surface besmirch first, reoccupy the dishcloth that gush has muti-function cleaner or toothbrush use the way that make a circle to clean from outside to inside, do not enlarge polluted area as far as possible
当发现污渍时，先用湿抹布将表面污垢去除，再用喷有多功能清洁剂的抹布或牙刷用打圈的方式从外到内清洁，尽量不要扩大污染面积
Types of method to use（grease can be cleaned with mousse, dust can be multi-functional cleaners, and graffiti cleaner can be used for key decontamination）
不同的污迹有不同的清洁剂（油脂类可以使用清洁摩丝，灰尘类使用多功能清洁剂，重污类可以使用涂鸦清洁剂）
Test if the fabric is color fast - press a rag or blotter dampened with cleaning solution on the fabric’s deeper colors on some unnoticeable place. Test on unnoticeable area so that any damages that chemical may cause to the carpet/fabric is hidden from guests.
测试墙布和墙纸是否掉色-用小块的布或吸水纸蘸上清洁剂在不显眼的地方先擦拭一下，看是否会掉色。在不显眼的地方测试是为了如果药剂损坏了布料或地毯，那也不会被客人发现从而影响美观
Check the texture of the filler and cloth. If it is fabric filler, the cloth will turn brown and yellow when exposed to water
查看填充物和布料的质地，是织物填充物的话，那么遇水后布料会发棕黄色
Never use abrasive cleaning agent or scouring pad to clean the area as this will cause color fading and damage to the surface.
不要使用研磨剂或百洁布擦拭，会掉色和损坏表面
Use a clean dry cloth to dry the wall-paper、wall cloth
最后用干净的干抹布擦干墙纸和墙布
In order not to leave water mark on soft wrap wall cloth and wallpaper, use blower to blow dry immediately.
为了不让软包墙布和墙纸上留下水痕，要立即使用鼓风机吹干
If guests spill red wine and other stains on the wall cloth and wallpaper and cannot remove them, they should inform the supervisor and GRM and restaurant supervisor in time.Any indeterminate stains should be promptly notified to superior for help
如果客人将红酒等污迹洒在墙布和墙纸上并无法去除的，需及时通知主管及GRM和餐厅主管。任何无把握去除的污迹需及时通知上级寻求帮助
If any damage is found, it should be reported for repair immediately
一旦发现破损需要及时报修
