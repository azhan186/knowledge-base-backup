# 楼层员工工作注意事项（Floor_002）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_002 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure employees are aware of the matters needing attention .（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- 楼层员工规范

## 目的

Ensure employees are aware of the matters needing attention .
让员工清楚地知道平时工作中相关注意事项, 掌握整个的操作流程。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Polite greetings and sincere hospitality. Using the 5 / 15 step principle, and the guest's name with a warm smile, and make eye contact to make the guest feel the welcome.
礼貌问候，真诚待客。用5步/15步的原则，称呼客人的姓名，面带热情的微笑，目光接触让客人感到备受关注。
Actively obey the work arrangement of the department
积极服从部门的各项工作安排。
Maintain good appearance and working conditions, and observe the grooming standards
保持良好的仪容仪表和工作状态，遵守着装标准，遵守仪容仪表标准。
Comply with the key sign in & out procedure (the action discipline will be given if key is missing).
遵守钥匙签领签还制度（钥匙遗失做违纪处理）。
Don’t open the door for the guests, polite inform guest to the front desk to make the key again. If the guest demands to open the door strongly, the employee should report to the housekeeping office and GRM immediately.
不随意为客人开门，应该有礼貌的请客人到前台重新做钥匙。如果客人强行要求开门,需马上报告办公室后, 经宾客服务经理来处理，不可以与客人发生正面冲突。
Don't open the door for visitors. Polite inform visitor to the front desk
不可以为访客开门，有礼貌的请客人到前台。
Don’t help the guest to deliver the package or leave a message, and politely guide the guest to the concierge at the front desk to handle relevant procedures.
不帮客人转交包裹，留言，有礼貌的指引客人到前台礼宾部办理相关手续。
Not allow buy goods or provide medicine for the guest, and inform Concierge to contact the guest.
不随意给客人买东西或提供药品，需告知礼宾部联系客人。
Don’t open the door for the internal employee without any reason, for example, room maintenance is accompanied by room attendant if room status is occ. Maintenance of VR room needs to change the room status to VD, and without accompanied. But after fixed problem, the room attendant must to enter the room to confirm the maintenance results and follow up the cleaning, then inform floor supervisor to release the room.
不随意为内部员工开门。例如，有客房间维修需楼层服务员陪同。干净房态维修需要更改房态。脏的退房需维修可以记下维修人员的姓名，可以不用陪同。但在任何维修结束后，需进房间进行确认维修结果，并且跟进做清洁。然后通知主管检查并更改房态
Inspect the all floor area before off duty, to make sure the all area is clean, tidy and without room door open.
下班前必须巡视楼层所有区域，确保楼层所有区域干净整洁并所有客房门都是关闭的。
Report any suspicious persons or objects to the housekeeping office and LP on duty in a timely.
发现可疑的人或物品及时报办公室和安保部。
Make sure the room windows are closed after making the room. If the guest requires to open , inform the GRM immediately。
必须保证做房后, 房间窗户的关闭，如客人要求打开门通知宾客服务经理。
Immediate report room status discrepancy.
发现房态异常立即报告。
Guestroom door must remain closed during the cleaning process. If guest in the room, the guestroom door must be kept open.
在清洁房间时，房间必须关闭。客人在房间时必须开着房门做清洁。
Must ensure the pantry door and light always closed, fire exit door as well
必须确保工作间的门和灯及消防门一直保持关闭。
Do not make loud noise and run in the guest area, and make sure to be " 3 light ".
不在客人区域大声喧哗、跑动，确保做到“三轻”- 走路轻、说话轻、操作轻。
Replace the bedding linen according to department regulations, and all the bedding for check-out room must be replaced. Action discipline will be given if room attendant did not follow.
根据部门的规定更换床上用品，退房的床上用品必须全部更换。违者将做严重违纪处理。
Attention to the removal the bedding dirty linen according to the procedures, Discipline action will be giving if room attendant roll down guest items.
注意撤床上用品，根据撤床程序，不将客人的物品卷下，违者做违纪处理。
If found bed linen with special dirty, it should be marked (tied in knots) or bagged separately and sent to the laundry for washing
发现脏的布草，需做记号（打结）或分开装袋送洗衣房洗涤。
Bedding products shall be guaranteed to be without stain, damage and hair, if found shall be replaced in time, discipline action will be given if room attendant did not follow
床上用品确保“三无”无污迹、无破损、无毛发，发现后需及时更换，违者做违纪处理。
