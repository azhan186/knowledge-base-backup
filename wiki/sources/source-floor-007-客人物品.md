# 客人物品（Floor_007）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_007 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Guest privacy should always be respected and guests’ belongings will be handled carefully when cleaning the room. To ens（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 客人贵重物品
- 客人物品管理

## 目的

Guest privacy should always be respected and guests’ belongings will be handled carefully when cleaning the room. To ensure proper way of handling guest belonging during cleaning.
客人的隐私要得到尊重，在清洁房间时对待客人的私人物品要特别仔细认真。确保在清洁客房时正确处理客人物品。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that guest belongings are handled with the utmost care and are never rearranged the position. Belongings are only lifted to clean surfaces and underneath. Always put back to originally place.
客人物品将被最小心地对待，不得变动。清洁时才能将其移动，并在打扫完后放回原处。

## 程序

Collect newspapers, books and/or papers and place them neatly on the desk.
将收集的报纸、书籍、纸张等整齐的放在桌子上。
Neatly fold on sofa or hang in closet clothing items found lying on bed, floor or chairs.
如发现客人衣物在地上、床上或椅子上，应将其叠好放在沙发上或挂在衣橱内。
Carefully lift or move the guest belonging, in order to properly clean the room; put back to in their place after cleaning.
清洁时小心地移动物品，并在打扫完后放回原处。
Do not touch guest belongings more than necessary to clean and service the room.
在日常清洁时如果不需要不要碰任何客人物品
All items in the trash bin must be carefully checked before they are discarded.
所有在垃圾桶里的物品，员工需仔细确认，方可丢弃。
Guest put more cosmetics or items on the bathroom counter, after clean the table, puts the face towel or hand towel on the table and place the guest's supplies on the towel
客人堆放了较多的化妆品在卫生间台面上，服务员清洁台面后，垫上面巾或中巾，并将客人用品整齐摆放在毛巾上
Do not move or open guest’s suitcases, or examine items.
不要移开或打开客人的手提箱检查里面的物品。
Report valuables or large amount of money displayed in the room to the supervisor.
发现客人的房间里有贵重物品或大量现金，马上打电话通知主管。
Do not re-arrange the room items if the guest has arranged them in a way which is more convenient for him/her. This applies especially to long-staying guests.
如果客人根据自己的需要重新安排房间的物品，我们不要改变它，尤其是长住客人的房间。
