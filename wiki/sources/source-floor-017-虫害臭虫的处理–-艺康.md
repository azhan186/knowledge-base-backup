# 虫害臭虫的处理– 艺康（Floor_017）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_017 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To proactively address an alleged or confirmed infestation of bed bugs in guest rooms（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 虫害/臭虫处置（外协艺康）
- 吸尘器使用规范

## 目的

To proactively address an alleged or confirmed infestation of bed bugs in guest rooms
积极解决被确认的臭虫感染问题。

## 政策规定

In conjunction with our Ecolab pest elimination, we will institute a program to train housekeeping associates on how to identify the signs of a possible infestation of bed bugs. All members of the housekeeping and maintenance staffs must complete the training. This periodic semi-annually training is to be conducted by Ecolab pest elimination.
与艺康有害生物防治相结合，建立培训计划，客房部员工如何识别臭虫可能感染的迹象。客房部和工程部的所有人员必须完成培训。每半年培训是由艺康有害生物防治进行。

## 程序

Inspections by housekeeping and other hotel associates are the most proactive measures that can be taken against bed bugs. housekeeping associates must visually inspect the guestrooms each time a room is cleaned looking for signs of bed bugs as per the Bed Bug training each associate has received. Such associates must report any sightings or indication of bed bugs in a guest room or other area immediately to the HSKP office or director of rooms. The DOS or DOR should immediately take the following steps.
客房部和酒店其他同事要根据应付臭虫的措施主动检查。 客房部员工每次打扫房间时，必须要目视检查客房。所有员工在客房或酒店的任何地方发现有臭虫的迹象必须立即向服务总监或房务总监报告。服务总监或房务总监应立即采取下列措施。
Infestation Reported:
感染的报道：
All incidents of actual or suspected bed bug sightings or guest complaints must be reported immediately to the director of service, director of room, front office manager, and the hotel loss prevention department.
所有实际发生或疑似臭虫或客人投诉的事件,必须立即报告给服务总监、房务总监、前厅部经理和酒店防损部。
If the hotel receives a guest complaint, it is important to follow all procedures listed in this policy after immediately calling Ecolab pest elimination.
如果酒店接受客人投诉，应该根据相关程序与政策立即致电艺康有害生物防治。
At the first sign of suspected bed bug activity, the room should be quarantined and all access should be prevented. Nothing should be removed from the room.
在疑似臭虫活动的最初迹象时，房间应该被隔离，所有的通道都应该被阻止。房间里任何东西都不应该搬出。
Call Ecolab pest elimination’s customer service to request an emergency service inspection for bed bugs. Ecolab will inspect the room to correctly identify the pest and inform the property if bed bugs are found.
致电艺康有害生物防治的客户服务，要求臭虫急救服务检查。艺康将检查房间以正确识别虫害，如果发现臭虫将会告知酒店。
If one live bed bug is found, that room will be declared infested and rooms on either side, above and below will also be inspected. If more bed bugs are found, the inspection process will be expanded until all activity is encapsulated with in the block of rooms to be treated.
如果发现了一个活臭虫，该房间将被宣布感染，同时，在上方和下方的房间也将被检查。如果发现更多的臭虫，检查过程将扩大，直到所有虫害被控制在相关区域。
Ecolab will contact DOR to determine the next steps regarding treatment.
艺康将联系房务总监确定对于下一步的跟进。
Director of rooms will inform the GM，DOF and DOM.
房务总监将通知总经理、财务总监和市场总监。
Housekeeping must ensure that the front office has been notified and advises the room must place on manual blocker
客房部必须确保通知前厅部并将相关房间封起来。
Front office will only remove the manual blocker until cleared to do so by the DOR or DOE.
除非房务总监或工程总监允许，前台不得将封的房间放出卖房。
Infestation Confirmed:
感染证实：
All linens should be removed from the bedding but must stay in the room for inspection. Do not remove the vacuum if they have been in the room. These need to be inspected and treated if bed bugs are found. No items should be removed and any personnel should immediately inspect their own clothing for bed bugs.
房内所有布草都应撤下，但必须留在房间内待检查。如果房间里有吸尘器请不要拿走。如果发现臭虫这些需要检查和处理。不应移除任何物品，任何人员都应立即检查自己的衣服是否有臭虫。
Mattress and box spring should be stood upright against the wall, furniture should be moved away from walls at least 2 feet. Head boards should be removed and left in the room.
床垫和床箱应垂直于墙面旁，家具应远离墙面至少2英尺。头板应该移走并留在房间里。
If the room is occupied, the guest should be relocated to another room, preferably well away from the suspected room. All guest belonging should remain in the room until inspected to determine if infected.
如果房间是住客房，应及时的帮客人换房，最好远离可疑的房间。但所有客人的物品应留在房间内直到检查完毕，以确定是否感染。
Guest clothing, once released, should be washed/dried at high temperatures (140 degrees) or dry cleaned to kill any bed bugs eggs.
客人的衣物一旦被确认排除后应在高温（140度）清洗或烘干，以杀死任何臭虫卵。
Other guest belongings, suitcase, computer, etc. need to be inspected, treated if necessary or bagged and discarded if infested.
其他客人物品、手提箱、电脑等需要检查，必要时予以处理，如有感染则予以丢弃。
Mattress and box spring MUST be wrapped in plastic in the room and discarded. We recommend that they be destroyed in a fashion that they cannot be used elsewhere.
床垫和床箱必须用塑料纸包起来或丢弃。我们建议将它们销毁，不能在其他地方使用。
If a room is determined to be infested (one live bed bug) then a multi service treatment program (initial service, 24 hour follow up/room release, two week follow up) should be implemented to ensure that all life stages of the bed bug infestation are destroyed.
如果一个房间被确定为被感染（一个活臭虫），那么应该实施额外的杀虫服务计划（初始服务，24小时随访/房间释放，两周随访），以确保臭虫感染的所有生命阶段都被破坏。
After Ecolab services the infested rooms and non-infested rooms included in the treatment protocol, the infested room should be blocked and remain closed until released by Ecolab.
在艺康服务包括滋生的房间和非滋生的房间，这些房间应被封闭直到艺康确认服务结束。
