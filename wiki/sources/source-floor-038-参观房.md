# 参观房（Floor_038）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_038 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Show room should be kept in the best condition and clean at all times for showing to visitors. Show room flower should b（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 参观房准备与接待

## 目的

Show room should be kept in the best condition and clean at all times for showing to visitors. Show room flower should be kept fresh as well.
参观房要保持良好的清洁状态以接待来访者的参观。参观房内鲜花一样必须保持新鲜。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Show room should be maintained clean every day.
不同房型的参观房每天保持清洁。
Every morning the concerned room attendant should check and clean the show room, and ensure it is ready to be shown.
每天早晨楼层服务员检查并清洁所负责楼层的参观房，确保随时可以参观。
Every morning before 9:00 o’clock, the supervisor should first check the show room to make sure everything is in a good condition.
每天早晨9：00以前楼层主管首先检查这些房间，保证房间的良好状态。
All lights in the show room should be on.
参观房内的所有灯都打开。
Any visitor to the show room should be informed housekeeping before and after visiting the show room. After the visit, the supervisor will double check the show room to make sure everything is in a good condition.
所有来访者参观参观房前后都要要通知管家部，参观结束后主管要重新检查房间，确保房间物品的完好。
Cleaning showroom four times every day and ensure that the guests will feel the best state of our guest rooms.
每日2次检查跟进参观房清洁,确保每批参观客人看到客房最佳状态.（8:30am and 16:00pm）
When supervisor inspect the show room found plants not fresh, must inform florist to change it immediately.
主管检查参观房时，发现绿植不新鲜需及时通知花匠更换。
