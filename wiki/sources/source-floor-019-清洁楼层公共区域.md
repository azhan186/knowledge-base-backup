# 清洁楼层公共区域（Floor_019）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_019 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

The guest floor public area and service areas, as well as emergency stairways are NOT considered secondary or less impor（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 吸尘器使用规范
- 楼层公共区域清洁

## 目的

The guest floor public area and service areas, as well as emergency stairways are NOT considered secondary or less important areas for a cleanliness and sanitation stand point and are, therefore, thoroughly cleaned is required. To ensure all area are well-kept at all times.
楼层公共区域连同服务区及紧急楼梯不应被认为是次要区域，清洁卫生同样重要。而应该像其他区一样。确保所有区域在任何时候都清洁。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that floor public area and service areas, and emergency stairways are checked three times a day. These areas are always maintained in a clean, neat and presentable manner. the guest corridor is presentable to the guest at all times. The corridors vacuumed are always free of room service carts, trays or dishes.
楼层公共区域、服务区、及紧急楼梯应被一天检查三次。这些区域应保持整洁并无碍瞻观。客人走廊在任何时候应让客人感到满意。走廊吸尘时应无送餐服务手推车，托盘或器皿。

## 程序

Guest area:
客区
Use vacuum cleaner to vacuum corridors, pay special attention to corners and edges, is careful not to mark baseboards, walls, doors, and frames with vacuum.
使用吸尘器清洁走廊，特别注意角落及边缘地带，小心不要在脚板，墙面，门及门框处留下印记。
Take any room service carts, trays or dishes to the service elevator landing area.
将送餐服务手推车，托盘或器皿送到服务电梯升降台。
Check walls, doors / frames and baseboards for spots. Spot wash all these, as necessary with slightly damp yellow color rag.
检查脚板，墙面，门及门框处有无污点。使用微湿的黄色干净抹布清洁。
Dust all surfaces in corridors (including the guest elevator landing and furniture) with slightly damp yellow color rag.
使用微湿的黄色干净抹布将走廊上的所有表面的灰尘去除掉（包括客用电梯内部及其设备）。
Clean all mirrors and glasses (including glass of artwork) with window cleanser sprayed on clean blue color rag. Ensure that all mirrors glasses or artwork frames are dusted and clean.
使用喷洒窗户清洁剂的干净蓝色抹布清洁所有镜子及玻璃（包括玻璃艺术品）。确保所有镜子，玻璃及艺术品的框被清洁。
Repeat all of the above steps when checking corridors in the evening hours. NOTE: no vacuuming can be done after 6:00 p.m., no retention room service carts, trays and dishes on the floor to ensure that guest room corridors are always attractive and free of clutter.
在晚间检查走廊时重复上边的所有步骤。注意：晚6点以后不得进行吸尘工作，客人走廊上无
送餐服务手推车，托盘及器皿的滞留，以使其看起来整洁而且井井有条。
Please observe the following: guest corridors are never vacuumed before 10:00 a.m. on weekdays and 11:00 a.m. on Saturdays and Sundays, in an effort to observe the majority of “Do Not Disturb” signs and ensure for the comfort of guests sleeping in.
请遵守下列事项：周一至周五的上午9点前及周六周日的上午10点前不要在客人走廊进行吸尘工作。观察如大多房门挂“请勿打扰”指示牌时应确保睡觉客人的舒适性。
Vacuum all corridors once a day, including edges of the carpet.
一天两次给走廊吸尘，包括地毯的边缘部分。
For the first time, the floor attendant up to guest floor will inspect the lift area and corridor, and vacuum the guest public area at 9am.
楼层服务员上楼层第一时间, 巡视楼层客梯厅及走道。 并在早上9点开始吸尘。
Check corridors for debris, three times daily (beginning of the morning and afternoon shift).
一天最少三次检查走廊有无纸屑垃圾（早上、午饭后及下班之前）。
Back area:
后区
Mop all service areas, once a day, with a clean mop and water.
一天一次，使用干净拖把和水清洁服务区。
Mop all stairways twice weekly, with a cleaned mop and water, changing the water as frequently as needed.
一周两次用干净拖把和水清洁所有楼梯，清洁时根据需要换水洗拖把。
Check all stairways for debris twice per day, pick-up all trash and discard.
一天两次检查楼梯上有无纸屑，拣拾清理垃圾。
Sweep stairways, three times a week, from top to bottom.
一周三次清扫楼梯，从上至下。
Vacuum daily any all carpeted stairways in the public areas.
一天一次为公共区域铺地毯的楼梯吸尘。
Understand that no food or beverages may be kept in the service area.
不在服务区域存放餐饮部的食品和酒水。
No lost property is allowed to be kept in the pantry.  All items found must be handed to the housekeeping office as soon as possible.
不得将拾到的物品放在楼层工作间内， 应将拾到的物品及时送至管家部办公室。
All linen and guest supplies are placed in the designated shelves and set out to incorporate the logical workflow.
