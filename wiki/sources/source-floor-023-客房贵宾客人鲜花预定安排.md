# 客房贵宾客人鲜花预定安排（Floor_023）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_023 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To provide fresh and quality flowers to guestroom, VIP guests on a as needed basis.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- VIP 鲜花预定
- VIP 房准备

## 目的

To provide fresh and quality flowers to guestroom, VIP guests on a as needed basis.
在需要时为客房贵宾客人提供高质量的鲜花。

## 政策规定

It is the policy to consistently provide fresh and quality flowers to VIP guests. Always handle and keep flowers with care to ensure maximum life and freshness.
始终如一地为贵宾客人提供高质量的鲜花。应细心处理以使鲜花保持长寿命及新鲜。

## 程序

Submit the on-day order form to HSKP office 2 hours in advance.
提前2小时将预订当天的鲜花表递交到客房部办公室。
HSKP clerk will consolidate the quantity and different types of flowers, then fill out the purchase request for director of service approval. Informed the florist to prepare the flower.
客房部文员整理要求鲜花的数量及品种，填写申购单并取得服务总监的批准，然后通知花匠准备鲜花。
Florist will arrange flower accordingly and deliver to the room, floor supervisor must check the quantity and types of flowers upon delivery.
花匠安排鲜花并将其送到相应的客房，鲜花送入在接收时，楼层主管需检查鲜花的品质，数量及品种。
The floor supervisor should sign the flower list after confirm
主管在鲜花确认后无误，在送花单上签字确认。
HSKP office will submit the total monthly flower purchase orders and consumption to Finance office for record keeping.
客房部办公室应将月度鲜花采购及消费总量提交财务部作记录。
Flower Set Up鲜花摆放
All VIP flower place in the room before guest arrivals.
所有贵宾房的鲜花必须在客人到达之前摆放到位。
Florist deliver flower to room, the associates must ensure the vase is not cracked or chipped.
送入房间的鲜花,服务员需确保花瓶完好无损
Place a single flower vase on the bathroom basin counter.
单枝花瓶放在卫生间台面上。
Different size of pot flower will be put in rooms depending on VIP status and room type. The flowers arrangement will be placed on the round table in bed room or tea table in living room.
房间盆花的摆放根据贵宾客人的级别以及房型摆放不同的盆花。放在房间的圆桌上或客厅的茶几上。
Check freshness of the flower daily.
每天检查鲜花状态。
Replenish water in flower vase if necessary.
必要时为鲜花换水
Inform the supervisor to send fresh flowers for replacement when needed.
如果鲜花需要更换，及时通知主管。
After guests’ checkout, clean the vase and re-use the flower if still fresh.
客人离店后，清洁花瓶，新鲜的花可以重新使用。
