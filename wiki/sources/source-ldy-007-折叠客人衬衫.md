# 折叠客人衬衫（LDY_007）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_007 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Shirts are required to be folded with as few creases as possible and look like new ones with nice packaging base on gues（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

Shirts are required to be folded with as few creases as possible and look like new ones with nice packaging base on guest requests.
客人要求时，衬衫折叠应尽量无褶皱，包装精美，外观崭新。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to establish guidelines and procedures on folding guest shirts
建立关于折叠客人衬衫服务项目的指导方针及程序。

## 程序

Prepare the accessories for folding a shirt, shirt board, shirt band, collar band, shirt bag, paper bow tie and stapler.
准备衬衫折叠物品：纸板、封条、领条、衬衫袋、领花、订书机、透明胶等
Remove the shirt on the hanger and lay the shirt on the table with the front part up.
将衬衫从衣架上取下，平放桌上，正面朝上。
Place the collar board in place by tucking neatly under the collar.
将领条整齐放入衬衫领下
Button up alternate buttons 1st, 3rd and 5th.
衬衫纽扣每隔一粒扣上。第一、三、五粒
Remove the laundry tag and tape to the shirt bag.
撕下码条，放入衬衫袋  把撕下的条码先用透明胶，贴在衬衫领子中间，以便包装时再次检查核对。
Turn over the shirt and place the shirt board at the back and push the top under the collar, fold the sides of the shirt inwards and place the sleeves on top
翻转衬衫，将纸板从后领推入。将衬衫两边依次向内折，同时重叠两袖。
Fold bottom part towards the shirt board not exceeding the shoulder..
衬衫下摆向内折叠，以不超过衬衫肩部为标准。
Turn the shirt over and ensure is straight on the board, place the paper bow tie and shirt band around the middle of the shirt with the Griffin logo in the middle front.
再次翻转衬衫，上领结，用订书机打透明胶封条并检查店徽LOGO是否处于衬衫正面中心位置。
Write marking numbers on shirt band for checking and remove laundry tag out of the shirt bag.
在衬衫封条上记录码条房号，以便核查同时取出码条。再次检查撕下来贴于衬衫领子中间的条码，核对无误后再去掉码条。
Place in the shirt bag and seal it.
衬衫装袋，封口。折叠好的衬衫用白色宣纸包起来，贴透明胶封边。
