# 布草管理（LDY_015）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_015 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All clean linen in uniform/linen room must be placed in good order/condition with proper par-stock for each department’s（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转

## 目的

All clean linen in uniform/linen room must be placed in good order/condition with proper par-stock for each department’s operation.
确保制服布草房内各类干净布草摆放整齐、状况良好、备量充足、运转正常。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Storage of linen
布草的储存
There are three or four pars of linen for circulation. One par is in housekeeping floor, one par is in Laundry and the rest are stored in Uniform/Linen Room.
酒店提供3-4套布草，以供周转。1套在客房，1套在洗衣房，其余储藏在布草房 （我们有3Par布草）
Linen should be categorized and kept in good order on shelves.
各类布草需存放架上，分类摆放。（按照布草货架上类别、数量摆放）
Uniform/linen room attendant should clean uniform/linen room ground every day.
每日需确保制服布草房的清洁。
Patrol linen store at least two times each day, and pour out the water of dehumidifier machine in time.
每日必须巡视至少2次布草仓库，并且及时将仓库内抽湿机内水倾倒。
Linen issue
布草的发放
Uniform/linen room attendant issues F&B linen and rags within required time. Receives soiled ones at the same time.
在规定时间内，制服布草房洗衣房服务员接收餐饮脏布草，同时发放干净布草并记录（包括抹布）。小方巾制服房发放。
Uniform/linen room prepares clean linen for the next day exchange, keep in good order on shelf in time.
每天制服布草房洗衣房核查接收洗衣房送返干净布草，及时上架，做好楼层布草发放准备。
The uniform room will collect floor rags and send them to the laundry room every afternoon. The next morning the laundry will send back the clean cloth to the uniform room.
每天下午制服房将收集楼层服务员及公共区域服务员把抹布送洗至洗衣房规定区域桶内。第二天上午洗衣房将洗干净抹布装袋放在布草车内，送至客房部办公室门口。制服房。
Washing of linen
布草的送洗
Dirty F&B linen need to be recorded and hand over to uniform/linen room. Then be send to laundry for washing. Housekeeping dirty linen received from floor is left in laundry for washing directly.
餐饮脏布草经制服布草房洗衣房检查记录后,交至洗衣房洗涤。楼层脏布草可直接送至洗衣房洗涤。
Uniform room attendant must check and record clean linen from the laundry. return any unqualified linen to laundry for rewash.
制服房必须检查记录洗衣房送返的洗衣房布草运输员再次检查任何干净布草，质量不合格的布草需退回洗衣房重新洗
Linen discarding and reuse
布草的报损及再利用
Any clean linen for guest use must be inspected carefully to ensure no damage or stain before being delivered to Uniform /Linen Room. Laundry Shift leader could decide those not qualified linen will be handled again or discarded.
洗衣房当班领班经理主管必须核查当天生产的破损污渍布草,判断可否送至制服布草房报损或再处理。
The discarded linen could be used in case of leaking. with the marks for scrap linen, and sort out them to keep in the store.
报损布草上做好报废标记后，单独存放于布草仓库。一旦发生漏水等情况，可以领用处理使用。
Discarded linen should be noted daily and reported to the Laundry Manager monthly in the end of each month..
制服布草房每天记录报损布草情况，月底交报损表给洗衣房经理。
月底做布草报损记录，拍照片打印，填报损单。
