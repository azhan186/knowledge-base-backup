# 优先打扫（Floor_029）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_029 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Always clean vacant or “check-out” rooms first so that there is always a supply of “ready” rooms available for the guest（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- 客房优先打扫排序

## 目的

Always clean vacant or “check-out” rooms first so that there is always a supply of “ready” rooms available for the guest to check in.
永远首先清洁空房或离店房，以便为客人入住提供可用的“准备好”的房间。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that the goal is trying to clean all checked-out and vacant rooms by afternoon to minimize guest waiting period especially on high occupancy day.
尽量将所有离店房及空房在下午前清洁完毕，以减少客人等候时间，特别是在高住房率的时候。

## 程序

Priority cleaning procedure are as follow:
优先打扫程序如下：
The HSKP clerk will prepare your daily assignment in the morning and assign you to your section/floor.
客房部文员会在早晨分配当日优先打扫房间。
Will run an arrival reports and note the room types that will be in demand by communicating with front office.
打印抵店报告，注明从前厅部得到的将住客房的房型。
Will run a separate “VD-vacant dirty” report for cross reference check.
运用单独的“VD-脏空房”报告作为检查的参照。
Will relate all above-mentioned information to the floor supervisor for proper actions.
将上述信息交所在楼层主管负责处理。
If a housekeeper has a lot of early arrivals or check outs at the time, it is the floor supervisor’s responsibility to switch room to ensure all of those rooms are cleaned on time.
如有大量提前抵店客人或离店客人同时出现，楼层主管的责任是改变房间以使它们被及时打扫。
After the room is cleaned and inspected, floor supervisor will report to the office to ensure room status are updated.
在房间清洁完并检查后，楼层主管应向办公室报告以确保房态更新。
Any VIP arrival room informed by morning must finished cleaning before 12noon.
所有早上接到通知的贵宾预到房必须在中午12点前清洁完毕。
All the occupied room and VIP occupied room must finish cleaning before 4pm.
所有住客房及贵宾住客房必须在下午4点前清洁完毕。
