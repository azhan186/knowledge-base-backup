# 贵宾房的准备（Floor_039）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_039 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

VIP guests should be provided excellent service and treatment upon their arrival and the room should be ready and VIP-in（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- VIP 房准备

## 目的

VIP guests should be provided excellent service and treatment upon their arrival and the room should be ready and VIP-inspected.
贵宾到达房间时，房间内的所有设备设施都应该按照贵宾的待遇准备好，贵宾应该得到完美的超一流的服务和待遇。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Every morning 8am, asst floor manager will get VIP arrival list daily from FDM
每天早上8点，楼层副经理会致电前台经理，确认当天预抵VIP 的房号并打印出报表。
The housekeeping clerk will check the expected arrival VIP room number through the trace report and inform the concerned supervisor at once.
管家部文员每天通过跟踪报告检查预抵贵宾的房号，并且要立即通知相关的楼层主管。
Room service will set up the VIP amenities (different size) according to the different VIP grade on the different place depending on the room type. No amenities should be placed before the room attendant prepares the room.
送餐部将会根据不同级别的贵宾赠送相应的欢迎品，并根据房间类型的不同，放在房间内相应的位置。在服务员准好房间之前，不要将欢迎品放在房间内。
A welcome card from the general manager will be placed in the room before the guest arrives in the hotel.
在客人到达饭店之前，由总经理发出的欢迎卡应该放在房间里。
The housekeeping put the plants into the room as required.
管家部负责按不同要求放绿植
When room attendant gets the VIP room number, they should first prepare it. The assistant floor manager should check the VIP room after supervisor checked to make sure the VIP room is in good condition.
当服务员得到贵宾的房号后，应该首先准备贵宾房间。在楼层主管查过之后, 楼层副经理应该再去检查一遍，以确保贵宾房间保持良好的状态
Usually the VIP room should be preparing well before one hour according to the expected arrival time.
通常贵宾房间应该在客人预抵饭店的一个小时之前准备好。
If there is no expected arrival time for VIP, the room should be prepared well at least before 12 o’clock in the noon.
假如贵宾房间没有预抵时间，房间应该最迟在中午12点之前准备好。
When the VIP room is checked by the last person in the housekeeping, the room status should be changed into vacant inspect.
当贵宾房间被管家部的最后一个人检查后，房间的状态应该变成已检查过的可卖房。
