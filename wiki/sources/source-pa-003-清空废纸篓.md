# 清空废纸篓（PA_003）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_003 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Maintain wastebasket cleanliness standard, and always not to throw away items which are not inside the wastebasket.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 垃圾/废纸篓处理

## 目的

Maintain wastebasket cleanliness standard, and always not to throw away items which are not inside the wastebasket.
保持废纸篓清洁标准，不得将未放在废纸篓中的物品扔掉。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of Hotel that all wastebaskets are emptied and cleaned each time is serviced. If the hotel’s logo is printed on wastebasket, ensure that logo face the guest at all time.
在清洁服务时所有废纸篓应被清空并打扫。如废纸篓上印有酒店标记，应使其面对客人。

## 程序

Empty all wastebaskets on the designated area.
清空指定区域的所有的废纸篓。
With a slightly damp, clean rag wipe out the entire wastebasket each time a guestroom is serviced.
使用一块微湿，干净的抹布清洁废纸篓。
Ensure that the logo on each wastebasket always faces the guest.
应使废纸篓上印有的酒店标记面对客人。
Discard items that are inside the wastebasket ONLY!
只能丢弃废纸篓内的物品！
The employee must confirm that the garbage is collected in the trash bin before it is dumped
废纸篓内的垃圾在倾倒之前,员工必须确认废纸篓内是否是垃圾
If the garbage has strong odor, it should be poured into the garbage bag and tied tightly at the mouth of the bag. The smelly garbage should be taken away from the public area and thrown into the garbage room as soon as possible
如果垃圾有浓重的异味，需倒入垃圾袋并将袋口打结扎紧，尽快将有异味垃圾带至垃圾房
If the rubbish is broken glass, it should be put into the garbage bag separately and deliver it individually to the garbage room and inform the cleaners of the garbage room.
如果垃圾为碎玻璃或尖锐物，需单独装入垃圾袋，单独送至垃圾房并告知垃圾房的清洁人员
Employees shall not directly grab rubbish to avoid hand injury
员工不得将手直接抓取垃圾，以免手受伤
All rubbish bag must be ligated before sent to the garbage room
所有垃圾在送至垃圾房前必须打结扎紧。
