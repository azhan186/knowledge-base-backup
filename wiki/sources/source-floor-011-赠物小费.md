# 赠物小费（Floor_011）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_011 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To avoid any unnecessary confusion between departments when guests left gratuity for employees.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 赠物/小费处理

## 目的

To avoid any unnecessary confusion between departments when guests left gratuity for employees.
当客人给员工留下小费时，避免在部门之间产生任何不必要的混淆。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that monies left by guests in an obvious manner without any doubt that it is intended as gratuity for services provided are to be handled in the following way.
客人明显是出于礼貌留下奖赏服务的赏钱将被按如下方式处理。

## 程序

Tips found in guestrooms are placed on top or bottom of the pillow to be considered to floor attendant’s tips, unless clearly stated as in the following examples:
客房内放置的小费只有放在枕头上下的，被认为是给楼层服务员的，除非像如下列举的情况：
Monies left on top or bottom of pillow, but amount is RMB100 or above，the supervisor should be informed before taking it, after supervisor confirmed then take it.
虽小费放在枕头上，但金额在100元或以上的，需通知主管确认后，方可拿取。
Money left with a note from the guest and designated to the employee.
留有字条的小费是给指定人员的。
No money can be taken from any place except the pillow. If it is vacant dirty room, you should pass the money to the housekeeping department for L&F process.
除了枕头以外任何地方的钱都不可拿取，如果是退房，也需将钱交至客房部做遗留物品。
Any tip left by a guest for housekeeping services will go to the room attendant who serviced the room on that day.
任何为感谢客房服务留下的小费将被交给当值的楼层服务员。
Tips are to be collected at time of servicing the room. Vacant dirty room tips can be collected as soon as the room attendant knows the room is vacated by the guest.
小费一般在为房间服务时收取。退房的小费在楼层服务员知晓客人腾出房间时收取。
If the room attendant can’t confirm it is tips or not, must inform and confirm with supervisor.
服务员不能确定是否是小费的，需通知主管来确认。
