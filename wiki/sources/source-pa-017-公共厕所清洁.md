# 公共厕所清洁（PA_017）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_017 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

It is the Marriott Brand Standard that the hotel provides adequate sanitary service to all Marriott guests.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 公共厕所清洁消毒

## 目的

It is the Marriott Brand Standard that the hotel provides adequate sanitary service to all Marriott guests.
这是万豪品牌标准酒店提供充足卫生服务给所有万豪的客人。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

The storage room in each toilet are to have PAR level of necessary supplies at all times and this will be replenished at the end of each shift or when in need.
每个卫生间的储藏室在任何时候都要有足够数量的必要用品，这些用品将在每班结束时或需要时补充。
Always keep the storage room clean and organized
总是保持储藏室干净及整齐
All the janitor carts are to be cleaned thoroughly before being stored
所有的清洁车在储存前必须彻底清洁
All tissues are to be folded and refilled as per Brand Standard
所有的纸巾都要按照品牌标准折叠和重新填充
Patrolled public restroom every hour.
每间隔一小时巡视公共厕所
Male Toilets男卫生间:
Basin counter no water or stain
台面无水渍、污渍。
Basin and faucet no water or stain, and no maintenance problem with faucet
面池及水龙头无水渍污渍, 面池水龙头无工程问题
No water stain and stain on the mirror
镜子无水渍及污渍
Sufficient hand sanitizer and no watermark and soap stain on the bottle
洗手液充足，并洗手液瓶无水印及皂渍
Plenty of tissues paper
面巾纸充足
Toilet cubicles are to be checked regularly to ensure there are no cigarette butts on the floor and no odor.
卫生间隔间要定期检查，确保地上没有烟头，没有异味。
There must be enough toilet paper between each toilet seat
每个马桶间必须有充足的卷纸
Ensure restroom cleaning and condition, including wall, door , ceiling, A/C and light
确保公区洗手间清洁及状态，包括墙壁，门，天花板，通风口，照明等
All urinals are frequently checked to ensure there is no odor.
所有小便器频繁查确保有没有的气味。
Ensure all taps and flush are working.
确保所有水龙头和冲洗装置正常工作。
Place odor suppressant balls in the urinal bowl which must be presentable in look and size.
将抑臭球放入尿斗内，外观和大小必须合适。
Clean the urinal bowl at least TWICE in each shift to keep the stains from forming.
每班至少清洁便池两次，以防止污渍形成
All tissue papers are to be removed each time in order to ensure no odor is present.
及时清理脏纸巾，确保没有异味
Ensure that restroom décor items without dust, water and stains
确保卫生间装饰品无灰尘，水渍及污渍.
Ensure the floor is clean and in good condition
