# 布草盘点（Floor_001）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_001 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Inventory of all linen will be done every month, to ensure correct supplies and minimize losses of linen.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转

## 目的

Inventory of all linen will be done every month, to ensure correct supplies and minimize losses of linen.
布草盘点每月一次，以确保布草正确使用及最低限度的布草损耗。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Housekeeping linen inventory will be done once a month.
客房布草一月盘点一次。
Laundry manager and housekeeping manager will decide the exact date of the inventory together according hotel occupancy.
制服房与楼层根据客房率共同确定盘点日期。
Housekeeping linen inventory:
客房布草的盘点
Laundry linen inventory operation process：
洗衣房布草盘点操作流程
The night before inventory day, all floor pantry linen supplement completed by 21:00 PM.
盘点前一天晚上21点前，将所有工作间的布草补充。
The linen runner will collect the all dirty linen to laundry section by 17:00 on inventory day, and the laundry attendant will count the number of dirty linen.
所有盘点当日下午5点前的脏布草由收布草专员从楼层收下，并由洗衣房清点数量。
Uniform/linen room and laundry count linen in their own area. The uniform supervisor is responsible for the linen store, the laundry Assistant manager is responsible for laundry area.
制服布草房和洗衣房核点各自区域实际布草数量。制服房主管负责布草仓库的点数，洗衣房副经理负责洗衣房区域点数。
Floor linen inventory operation process：
楼层布草盘点操作流程
The room attendant shall ensure that all dirty linen is removed to the dirty linen trolley in the front of elevator as soon as possible before 5pm.
楼层服务员需确保下午5点前,尽可能将所有脏布草撤至员工梯口的脏布草车内。
After 5pm, the room attendant is responsible for counting all the dirty linen.
下午5点后，所有脏布草的数量清点由楼层服务员负责。
Housekeeping room attendant counts actual guestroom linen amount. Pay attention to extra beds, cribs, pillows, etc.  These quantities need to be added to the amount.
楼层服务员需核对客房实际布草数量。特别注意如有额外加床、婴儿床、枕头等情况的，需将这些数量加入盘点数内。
After cleaning the room, the room attendant counts the number of clean linens in the pantry, fill out the inventory form.
服务员完成自己当天的房间清洁后，清点工作间干净布草数量，填写盘点表
The floor supervisor collects the linen inventory report, and is responsible for the number of floor pantry to check again. If find the problem and ask the staff to count again until the number is correct.
楼层主管收集员工布草盘点表，并负责楼层工作间数量的再次核对。发现问题让员工重新清点，直至数量正确为止。
The floor assistant housekeeper according to the inventory report, to spot check the linen quantity of pantry. Summarize the quantity and give the inventory report to the uniform supervisor.
楼层副管家根据盘点表，抽查工作间数量并汇总表格后交给制服间主管。
Uniform/linen room supervisor collects floor linen inventory report for summary.
制服布草房主管根据楼层布草盘点汇总表，完成客房部布草盘点报告。
Discarded linen must be included in the linen inventory.
报废布草的数量必须体现在盘点报表内
The inventory report with a comparison to previous month’s inventory will be send to laundry manager and director of service for signature.
根据当月盘点结果与上月盘点结果比较，完成布草盘点报告。并上报洗衣房经理及服务总监。
Copy and file of inventory report signed and confirmed by the director of service, and submit the original copy to the financial department.
将服务总监签字确认后的盘点表复印留档，将原件上交财务部。
