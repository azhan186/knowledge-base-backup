# 浴室清洁（Floor_028）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_028 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure our guests have a clean, sanitized bathroom upon their arrivals.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 浴室清洁标准

## 目的

To ensure our guests have a clean, sanitized bathroom upon their arrivals.
确保客人到来时有一个洁净，卫生的洗手间。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to ensure maintain consistent cleanses standard.
确保清洁标准被一贯地执行。

## 程序

Before starting to do work, first to check if the cleaning detergent has been filled up in the bottle, and properly labelled.
在清洁工作开始前，首先检查清洁剂瓶是否装满，标签是否正确。
Take cleaning bucket into bathroom.
将清洁桶拿到洗手间。
Remove rubbish and soiled linen, place them into trash bag and staff lift landing area.
清理垃圾和脏布草，将其分别移至垃圾袋和员工梯区域。
Collect and send the dirty cups to the pantry.
将脏的杯具收出送至工作间。
Flush toiled, spray bowl cleaner in toilet, wait for the detergent to act chemically.
冲洗抽水马桶，喷洒清洁剂在里面，等待清洁剂起化学作用。
Wash ice bucket if necessary, put them upside down to let water dripping.
清洗冰桶，将它们倒置让水滴下。
Spray chemical into basin, use scrubbing pad to clean thoroughly including drain holes plug, taps, soap dish and marble counter. Rinse with water and let water drip down for drying water.
将清洁剂喷入洗脸池，使用擦洗垫彻底清洁包括排水塞，水龙头，皂碟及大理石台等位置, 然后用水冲洗并滴干。
Spray chemical into bath tub or shower room, wall tile, then scrub with scrubbing pad. Scrub rubber seal around tub, shower head, wall tile, water taps, drain holes and plug. Rinse with water then let water run down for drying later.
喷洒清洁剂在浴缸或淋浴间，墙面瓷砖处，然后用擦洗垫擦洗。擦洗浴帘，淋浴头，瓷砖，水龙头，排水孔及排水塞。用水冲洗然后晾干。
Get cleaning rags and glass cloth together. Start from hand basin area. Polish all mirrors in case of stain, use glass cleaner.
同时使用干净抹布和擦玻璃布。从洗手池区域开始。如有污迹用擦玻璃布擦亮镜子。
Cleaning rag- dry hand basin, taps, soap dishes, marble area, counter. Polish all fixtures.
抹布—擦干洗手池，水龙头，皂碟及大理石台，擦亮所有物品。
Dry shower head, wall rubber seal, toilet bowl and bath tub side.
擦干淋浴头，浴帘，马桶及浴缸。
Wipe dry toilet seat cover, seat, toilet bowl and underneath. Do not forget the hinge and underneath.
擦干马桶盖，马桶及下面。不要忘记马桶盖铰链。
Wipe clean rubbish bin.
擦干净垃圾桶。
Wipe floor from inside corners to outside.
从里到外擦净地面。
Take clean cups & glass from the workshop and fill the room
从工作间拿干净的杯具补入房间
Put things back to position. Replace guest supplies and hang towels in position.
将物品放回原处。替换客用品并挂好毛巾。
Take a final look to see if there is anything missing.
最后检查一下看有无遗漏。
Remark:
备注
No washing of glassware and chinaware in the guestroom, all glassware and chinaware only can be cleaned and disinfected in the pantry， and use pink stripe cloth is polished and clean
不允许在房间内清洗玻璃器皿和瓷器，所有的玻璃器皿和瓷器只可以在工作间清洗并消毒，并用粉条纹口布抛光清洁。
Five colour code for cloth cleaning guideline: red colour duster for clean the toilet, green colour duster for clean the basin and bathtub, yellow colour duster for dust, blue colour duster for clean the floor, and use blue mirror rag for cleaning the mirror and window glass
服务员清洁的抹布必须按规定区分：红色抹布清洁马桶，绿色清洁面盆 蓝色冲淋房和浴缸，黄色抹灰，大浴巾地面，蓝色镜布清洁窗玻璃及镜子。
