# 怎样清洁空调口（PA_019）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_019 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Keep clean and hygiene with high standards to show sincerity to guest and meet his/her expectations.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 空调出风口清洁

## 目的

Keep clean and hygiene with high standards to show sincerity to guest and meet his/her expectations.
保持高标准的清洁和卫生水平，展示我们对客人的诚挚，以期能达到他们的期望

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Clean air outlet
清洁出风口
Turn off the air condition first and then clean it.
先将空调先关闭，再做清洁
Use ladders for auxiliary cleaning
使用登高器或梯子进行辅助清洁
Remove dust and cobwebs in A/C grill.
去除空调口的灰尘和蜘蛛网
Pour the appropriate amount of multifunctional cleaner onto the yellow cloth
在黄色抹布上倒上适量的多功能清洁剂
In case of edges, corners and crevices, the garbage can be cleaned out with a wet toothbrush and then wiped with a wet cloth
如遇边角缝隙可以使用湿牙刷将垃圾清洁出并再用湿抹布擦干净
Wrap a yellow wet cloth around your toothbrush handle or fingers and clean the air conditioning grille in sequence
用黄色湿抹布包着牙刷柄或手指，按顺序的清洁空调格栅
Wipe it with a damp cloth again
再用微湿的抹布擦拭一遍
Wipe off around of A/C grill with cleaning cloth.
用抹布擦拭空调口的四周
Turn the A/C on according to standard
根据标准启动空调
Make sure the air conditioning in the public area is odorless
确保公共区域空调无异味
According to the air conditioning system cleaning plan of the engineering department, the housekeeping department will arrange the air conditioning outlet cleaning
根据工程部的空调系统清洁计划，客房部将安排空调出风口的大清洁
