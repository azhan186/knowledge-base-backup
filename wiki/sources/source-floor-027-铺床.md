# 铺床（Floor_027）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_027 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure our guests have a clean, comfortable and “ready-to-sleep” bed upon their return.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 铺床标准流程
- 铺床标准

## 目的

To ensure our guests have a clean, comfortable and “ready-to-sleep” bed upon their return.
在客人回来之前为他们准备一张洁净，舒适并且“准备睡觉”的床。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to ensure consistency in service quality.
确保服务品质的一贯性。

## 程序

Remove the duvet from the bed. Place them on sofa.
将被子从床上移走放在沙发上。
Strip off the pillow cases.
将枕套取下。
Strip off the bed sheets and ensure no guest’s belongings（all the dirty linen must be shaken before it is removed）.
将床单取下并确保里面没有客人物品（所有脏布草撤出之前,都必须抖一下）。
Remove the soiled linen to the linen trolley at staff lift landing area.
将脏布草放到员工电梯厅的脏布草推车内。
Pull bed out and take a set of correct size and number of bed sheet and pillow cases.
将床拉出一些，拿出一套与床尺码和床数相同的床单及枕套。
Adjust the mattress and box spring in the right position.
调整床垫及弹簧垫到正确的位置。
Straighten the bed pad on the mattress, change it if is stained.
拉直床垫上的衬垫，如有污迹应更换。
Place the sheet on the bed, tuck it into the mattress at the head of the bed, then miter it the corner sides and tuck it into the mattress on both sides of the bed. At last, proceed to tuck it into the mattress at the foot side of the bed.
将床单铺在床上，先将床头位置的床单塞在床垫下，然后将床单边角斜折直角塞进床垫两侧下，最后将床尾的床单塞到床垫下。
Miter the corner at 90 degree and tuck in at both sides of the mattress.
床单边角斜折成90，然后塞在床垫两侧。
Place the duvet with cover on the bed.
将被子连同被单铺到床上。
Put the pillows into the cases and neatly stand them up against the head board.
将枕头套上枕套并靠床头板放好。
Final check to ensure the duvet is evenly hanging down on both sides of the bed.
最后检查确保被子均匀地铺在床上。
Push the bed back to its original position.
将床推回原处。
Make bed standard
铺床标准
Bed sheet:
Bed sheet - bottom sheet up front, middle line opposite (no deviation on both sides)
Bag angle - 90 degree package angle, straight is not loose, four sides package tight not loose
Overall - tightly packed and scraped
床单：
床单——正面朝上，中线对正（两边无偏差）
包角——90度包角，直挺不松，四边包紧不松
整体——包紧且挺
Duvet cover: when make the duvet cove, the quilt cannot be ground
The head of duvet - full, all the top foot, flanging to scratch
Both sides - the edge of the duvet is aligned with the edge of the duvet cover without wrinkle
The end of duvet - duvet is not retractable on the mattress, single bottom and single flush, two natural falls
