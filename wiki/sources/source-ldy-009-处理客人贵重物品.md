# 处理客人贵重物品（LDY_009）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_009 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Any valuables, which are found in guest garments, must be recorded in detail and returned to guest as soon as possible（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 客人贵重物品处置（上报/记录）
- 客人贵重物品
- 客人物品管理

## 目的

Any valuables, which are found in guest garments, must be recorded in detail and returned to guest as soon as possible
任何贵重物品，被发现在客人洗衣内，必定记载详细，尽快返回到客人。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Any money or other valuables found in guest garments must be reported to the Laundry shift leader. Guest laundry group will record it in detail.
从客人衣物内发现的任何钱或贵重物品应立即报告上级，客衣组服务员将详细登记物品内容
Notices for registration:
做失物登记时应注意：
Content: date, time, room number, items (money or valuables) amount, and finder’s name.
登记内容：房号、物品（钱和物）数目、拾物人姓名。
Guest laundry group put the items in envelop, and write down the room number., item (money or valuables) amount on it
客检组负责将客人遗忘的钱或物用信封装好，在信封正面写明房号， 拾物名称及数目。
Laundry supervisor inform GST relation manager, then GST relation manager contacts the guests by telephone immediately. If:
洗衣房主管立即通知宾客关系经理（超过500元价值的现金或物品），宾客关系经理与客人电话联系,如果：
Successful, arrange the runner to return the items to the guest and ask for his/her signature that will be kept
联系上，服务员立即将钱或物送还客人，并请客人签收
Unsuccessful, inquire about when the guest will check out, if not now, and items are very valuable, inform front desk to leave a message in the guestroom, if not, contact the guest later on. If the guest will check out the next day, the items must be returned before evening
未联系上，查询客人何时离店，如客暂不离店，而钱数目大或物品很贵重，通知前台给客人房间留言，钱数目不大，稍后再与客联系送回；如客人第二天离店,必须当天晚上前送到客人手中。
When the runner delivers the valuables to the guest, the guest is required to sign on the sheet of “item left service”
当服务员将贵重物品送交客人时，必须让客人在“宾客物品服务表格”“遗失物品返还表”上签字。
