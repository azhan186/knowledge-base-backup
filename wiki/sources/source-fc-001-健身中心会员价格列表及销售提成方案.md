# 健身中心会员价格列表及销售提成方案（FC_001）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-FC_001 | **类别**: 健身中心（Fitness Center） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

健身中心 SOP: 健身中心会员价格列表及销售提成方案（健身中心运营标准（会员/泳池/布草））

## 关键要点

- 会员价格与销售提成

## 目的

（未提取到目的字段）

## 政策规定

All hotel employees who sell out the Health Center Membership Card successfully will enjoy the incentive program to encourage and increase the sales of membership.
酒店任何员工成功销售出健身中心会员卡, 则可享受到此提成方案之内容。以此来鼓励并提高会员卡的销售力度和营业额的增长。
DEFINATION 定义
The Membership Sell incentives program is for All hotel employees using, Excom is excluded in this program.
以上会员卡销售提成方案可针对酒店所有员,酒店行政委员会成员除外。
JW Marriott Marquis proposition: 酒店提议

## 程序

Hotel employees who introduce their guests or friends to enroll the Fitness Center membership must lead the guest to show Fitness Center facilities and explain the membership benefits and follow up the sales until they pay the full membership fee (that will be effective to receive the membership incentive)
酒店员工谁介绍客人或朋友加入健身中心会员，则该员工本人必须带领客人参观健身中心设施和讲解会员的福利并跟进销售直至完成付款(这样才能有效的提取会员奖励), 接着健身中心将在月度提成报告中记录该员工的销售和提成信息。
Fitness Center Manager will prepare a Monthly Membership Incentive Record according to Book 4 times system revenue report to record employee Name, ID, sales date, voucher number, membership fee and total commission before the 5th on next month，
健身中心经理每月5号前准备上个月会员销售提成记录表，记录表根据Book 4 times 系统销售报表作为凭证，表内记录员工姓名、员工号、销售日期、账单号、会员费和团队提成金额及个人提成部分，及团队经费部分。
Incentive Payment Record Please checks the attachment file.
提成当月汇总表参见附件内容。
Fill payment voucher, attached the Monthly Membership Incentive Record and the copy of the voucher deliver to Financial Department for approval procedure.
部门填写付款凭证，并附每月会员销售提成记录表和账单复印件，一同交至财务部进入审批流程。
The incentive will issue together with the next monthly salary in employee personal bank account, Income tax bear by individual.
每月提成将于下个月月度工资一起发放在员工个人银行账户内,个人所得税自付。
Terms and condition 条款与条件
1.	Any Dispute, Cancel will not apply for above procedure.
任何拒付，取消都将不予支付促销奖励。
2．	Once an employee is found to have upsell fraud or not compliance with the hotel              regulations, the employee's upsell incentive will be cancelled. Related associate will be subject to terms listed in Employee Hand Book.
一旦员工被发现欺诈或不遵照酒店制度的行为，此员工的提成将会被取消。相关员工将会按照员工手册中的有关条款进行处理。
The Director Of Rooms authorized GYM Manager to offer 10% discount for guest（exclude day pass & Corporate card）for any special selling and keep guest loyalty.
房务总监授权健身房经理可以提供给客人九折优惠来推广酒店健身房服务，以便提高客人的粘合度 （单次体验价及公司卡除外）。
GYM manager must get the permission from Director Of Rooms or GM sign, if the guest ask for a special discount.
如果客人需要更低的特殊折扣，房务总监或总经理的允许后才能安排.
If the membership card selling with 20% discount and above, no any individual incentive award， group incentive and team fund can be awarded;
如果会员卡折扣高于20%或以上，将不计员工个人提成，团队提成可计入；
1% Team fund can be accumulated if the budget is completed or exceeded monthly in the case of unused.
1% 团队活动基金在每月完成预算或超出预算以后在未使用的情况下可以累计。
REGULATION COMPLIANCE规则遵守
Any staff offending policy and procedures outlined in this policy through the up-selling scheme may be disciplined or terminated immediately.
任何员工违反本政策的增销提成计划所列出的政策和程序，将受到纪律处分或立即开除。
Incentive does not apply for the associate who has handed in resignation.
奖励计划将不适用于已经递交辞呈的员工。
None-qualify associate collect incentive from another employee is strictly prohibited, any misconduct will result in disciplinary action or terminated immediately.
严格禁止无资格员工向其他员工收取提成，任何不当行为将立即给予惩罚或给予解雇处分。
If any unethical actions were found in use transportation selling, the whole month incentive will be cancelled, and disciplinary action will be taken.
如发现员工为得到提成过程中有任何不道德行为，将取消整月的提成，并给予纪律处分。
