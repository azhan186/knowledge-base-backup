# 客人洗衣收费（LDY_002）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_002 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Charges are to be prepared, passed on to the asst. laundry manager for posting to the guest folio after checking careful（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 客衣服务
- 客人洗衣收费

## 目的

Charges are to be prepared, passed on to the asst. laundry manager for posting to the guest folio after checking carefully, and a copy will be sent to the guestroom with the clean laundry.
洗衣房员工或领班仔细核查洗衣单，确保无误后方可输帐，一联账单随干净洗衣一同送至客房。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Collect all the laundry lists and check off the items listed in the hotel count column.
收集所有洗衣单，检查酒店核点一栏
For express service, check in the system for departure date of the guest.  If the departure is on the same day, charges must be posted immediately.
当日离店客人,尤其是加快洗衣必须提前入账。
How to calculate guest laundry charges:
入账方法：
Post subtotal the charge of laundry, dry cleaning and pressing.
抛入干洗、水洗或熨烫的总费用
If have discount, enter the total cost after discount directly
如有折扣，直接入折扣后的总费用。
Add 50% surcharge if it’s an express service.
如果加快洗衣附加50%加快费用
Add service charge to get the total bill
以上价格再加入服务费得出整张洗衣单价格
Transfer the total amount onto the master daily laundry and valet report sheet, clearly stating the voucher number, room number and the breakdown of the various charges for laundry, dry cleaning and pressing.
每天记录当日洗衣服务营业情况，包括账单号、房号及各类服务价格统计。
The top copy is to be attached with clean laundry returned to guestroom.
洗衣单第一联随洗衣送返给客人
The second copy is to be attached to the master laundry list and handed to the finance department.
洗衣单第二联附当日洗衣服务收入统计交财务部。
The third & forth copy with daily collection/packaging/delivery report is to be kept in the laundry
洗衣单第三联和每日收衣、包装、送衣记录一同保存在洗衣房。
