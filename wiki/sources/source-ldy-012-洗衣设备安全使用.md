# 洗衣设备安全使用（LDY_012）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_012 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure laundry team members operate all laundry equipment with high efficiency and safety according to instructions.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 洗衣设备安全使用规范
- 可疑设备上报

## 目的

Ensure laundry team members operate all laundry equipment with high efficiency and safety according to instructions.
确保洗衣房员工必须按規定安全高效操作洗衣房各类机器。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Check if there is a leak at the generator or related section before connecting with the mains.
接通电源前，先检查电器或机器的电器部位是否有漏电处。
Before starting any equipment for processing any load, inspect and test running the equipment manually to ensure that they are in perfect working conditions.
正式工作前应先试机，以确定机器可以正常使用。
The machine should not be on without any team members present. It should always be observed to prevent accidents.
机器运行期间人不能离岗，随时注意观察运行情况，防止发生意外情况伤害人身或损坏设备。
The team members must act strictly according to the regulations while operating the irons. Make sure to cut the mains before you leave, to prevent fire or other accidents.
使用各类电熨斗时，应严格按操作规程操作，做到当人离开时电源也随即切断的原则，防止发
生火灾和其它事故。
It is strictly forbidden to keep the dry linen in the drying tumbler for long time. Rags and other linen dried must be put in the trolley after cooling down. Heavy stained linen should be kept far away from heat and be handled in time.
严禁将烘干的布巾长时间留存在烘干机内，烘干布巾及抹布等必须经冷风冷却后可装入布巾车中。 对油污较重的布巾要远离热源并要及时处理。（SPA的布草一定要冷风打足降温）
Report to the Laundry shift leader as soon as possible when there is something wrong with the machine during operation.
工作中如机器设备有故障应立即报告洗衣房经理/主管。
When finished, switch off the machine and cut the mains. All linen not finished must be kept far away from the mains, stacked in order, clean and well ventilated.
完成洗涤后，关闭机器和电源，没有完成的布草必须远离电源，整齐堆放在干净的通风处。晚上下班时，关闭所有机器电源。
All equipment must be operated and maintained according to the regulations.
所有机器设备应按规定的操作程序操作和进行维护保养。
