# 早班机器检查（LDY_003）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_003 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All Laundry equipment is to be checked before operating to ensure that it is safe and in good working conditions.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 标准操作流程

## 目的

All Laundry equipment is to be checked before operating to ensure that it is safe and in good working conditions.
洗衣房任何机器操作首先必须检查，确保机器处于良好工作状态。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Check pressure gauge for steam supplies, turn on the electric mains, steam inlet valves and turn on the compressor motors for less than 2 minutes.
开启蒸汽压力装置、主机、蒸汽阀门、空气压缩机，压缩机马达运转不超过2分钟。
Before starting any equipment for processing any load, inspect and test run the equipment manually to ensure that they are in perfect working conditions.
（在开机和装载洗涤衣物前）先手工操作机器进行测试，确保机器正常操作。
If any machine is found to be faulty or unusual noises refrain from operating it. Report the matter to the Laundry shift leader immediately.
如果机器存在问题或异响（立刻停止操作，关闭机器），立即报告洗衣房领导。
