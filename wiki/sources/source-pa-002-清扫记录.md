# 清扫记录（PA_002）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_002 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure frequent trip / check are made at all times. Sweep Log can also serve as information tool when accidents occur（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 清扫记录登记

## 目的

To ensure frequent trip / check are made at all times. Sweep Log can also serve as information tool when accidents occur.
确保经常性的检查得以进行。清扫记录还可作为发生事故时的信息工具。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of Hotel that Sweep Logs be used for all Restroom Areas in order to protect Hotel from slips and falls liability.
清扫记录可运用于所有洗手间区域，以避免酒店承担意外滑倒事故的责任。

## 程序

The attached form is to be put on the inside (out of guest view) of the Service Pantry Area for the corresponding Restroom each day.
所附的表格放在服务备品间里（客人看不到）。记录每天相应洗手间情况。
Every time the Restroom is cleaned, mopped or checked, it should be noted on the Sweep Log Form accordingly.
每次清扫，拖洗或检查洗手间后应根据实际情况填写清扫记录。
The supervisor should fill in the inspection time on the cleaning record when he/she is patrol to the corresponding area.
主管在巡视到相应区域时，需将巡视时间填写在清扫记录上
The form is to be collected by the graveyard shift with a new form being put in its place. Used forms are to be turned into Style for filing.
表格将被夜班员工收集并用新表替换，填好的表将被交往客房部存档。
Forms are to be kept for a 30-day period before being destroyed.
表格在销毁前将被保留30天时间。
