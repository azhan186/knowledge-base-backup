# 损坏客人物品（Floor_033）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_033 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 关键要点

- 物品损坏/遗失责任界定与赔偿
- 客人贵重物品
- 客人物品管理

## 目的

All guest items should be treated with care. If an accident happen causing breakage or damage to guest belongings, the room attendant should report the incident to the direct of service and the housekeeping manager immediately.
所有客人物品小心处理.如果发生意外,需马上报告服务总监或当班经理.

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

If the guest is present, apologize for the mistake immediately, report to floor supervisor.
如果客人在房间内，立即向客人道歉,并报告给主管
If the guest is not present, inform floor supervisor immediately.
如果客人不在,立刻通知主管。
Don’t discard the damaged items, wait for the supervisor
损坏客人的物品不要丢弃，需等主管来。
Supervisor must report to direct of service or housekeeping manager
主管需报告给服务总监或客房部经理。
Inform GRM to take photo, complete incident report and follow communication with guest by GRM.
通知宾客服务经理拍照，由宾客服务经理写事件报告并跟进与客人的沟通。
If damaged guest items by outsources associates, housekeeping manager must inform outsources vendor, and all the compensation fees shall be paid by the outsourcing company
如果是外包员工损坏客人物品，客房经理需通知外包公司负责人，所有一切赔偿费用由外包公司负责
If damage guest items by hotel associates, floor supervisor will make counsel coaching with associates, and keep the record on file.
如果是酒店员工，楼层主管将事后与员工进行谈话记录，并将记录留档。
If hotel staff make repeated mistakes, the progressive misconduct form will be given.
如果酒店员工屡次犯错，部门管理层将给予员工递进式处理方式
All associates shall immediately report any damage to guests' goods, and shall not conceal it. If the complaint is not reported in advance, the department will deal with it strictly.
所有员工一旦损坏客人物品需立即上报，不得隐瞒不报。如果事先不报，造成客人投诉的，部门将从严处理。
