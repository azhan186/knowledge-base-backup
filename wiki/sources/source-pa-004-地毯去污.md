# 地毯去污（PA_004）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_004 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All carpets will be kept clean without stains and damages at all times. Proper cleaning schedule should be followed at a（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 地毯去污专项
- 吸尘器使用规范

## 目的

All carpets will be kept clean without stains and damages at all times. Proper cleaning schedule should be followed at all times.
通过合理的计划使酒店内的地毯得到科学的保养,无污渍无破损

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Use machine to clean carpet
使用机器去清洁地毯
Remove dust by vacuuming the area thoroughly.
先将所要清洁区域彻底吸尘,去除上面污渍
Fill the Diluted Cleaner with warm water (1:20) in a pressure pot and sprinkle it on the carpet evenly. Let the cleaner remain on the carpet and let it take effect for a while.
用温水以1:20稀释倒入压力喷壶中,均匀喷在地毯表面.让其作用一会儿.
Fill the clean water tank with water on the extracting machine. Start cleaning from the inside area with both hands controlling the handle and walking backwards.
在抽洗机水箱中装满清水,推到清洗区域的最里端,启动机器,双手抓住机器手柄向后倒退行走.
Ensure every line is overlapped by the second one to avoid omitting any area (if necessary, extract a few times to remove the cleaner properly)
吸完一行后,再吸第二行,每一次都要与前一次重叠,避免遗漏. (必要时反复几次,确保清洁剂全部吸走)
Use the vacuum nozzle to clean the corners.
边角处要使用地毯吸水扒.
In order to prevent too much foam, foam solution should be put into the dirty water tank when extracting the carpet.
在污水箱中倒入适量消泡剂,防止泡沫过多.
Use discarded linen to cover the cleaned area after deep cleaning, and remove the water on the skirting board and furniture.
清洁完毕后,用报废床单将所清洁区域铺好,以免踩脏.擦干净地角线及家具上的水渍.
Clean all tools and machines and take them back to the storeroom.
清洁所有的机器,去除滚刷上的地毯毛等污渍.放回 库房.
Report any problem if any during carpet extraction to Shift leader as soon as possible.
及时向PA领班报告工作中出现的问题
Carpet cleaning with shampoo machine.
地毯香波清洗地毯
Vacuum thoroughly the areas which need shampooing.
当需要香波清洗时对地毯进行彻底的吸尘
Remove all spots in advance
首先去除所有的污迹。
Prepare all tools: single scrub/form tank/brushes/dry foam cleaner/vacuum
准备好所有工具：单速机/水桶/刷子/干泡沫清洁剂/吸尘器
Dilute concentrate dry foam cleaner according to situation
根据情况稀释浓缩干泡沫清洁剂。
Ensure that machine switch is in the off position before plugging into the power supply. The cord should always be behind you
保证在插头插好前机器处在关的状态。绳子应该始终在你身后。
Ensure that your hands are dry when you turn on the machine
当你开机器时保证你的手是干的
Move the machine to the inside of cleaning area. Fix brush and switch it on.
把机器移到清洁区域内。安装刷子然后开机器
Adjust the operation handle.
调整操作把手
Adjust the quantity of foam according to the situation.
根据现场情况调节出泡量
