# 铜器上光（PA_012）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_012 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All brass in the hotel should be kept clean and shining at all times.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 铜器上光工艺

## 目的

All brass in the hotel should be kept clean and shining at all times.
确保酒店内的铜器随时保持光亮、清洁。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Dust-wipe the brass surface.
先将铜器表面擦尘。
Apply a small amount of brass cream to a dry clean cloth.
取少量铜油/膏在一块干净的干抹布上。
Rub the cloth along brass, let it remain for a while, and then polish with the cloth until the brass becomes shining.
均匀地涂在清洁物的表面，停留一会儿，用抹布不断摩擦直到清洁物光亮。
Clean the remaining brass cream with another rag after polishing. For the remains hiding in cracks, a small toothbrush is helpful to clean them out.
结束后，用另一块抹布将残留的铜膏擦干净。缝隙中的残留物用小牙刷刷净。
Be careful so the brass is not scratched by hard objects while polishing.
保护铜器，不要用硬物摩擦，产生划痕。
Seal the brass cream and return it to the storeroom.
铜油使用完毕后密封保存。
