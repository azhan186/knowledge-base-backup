# 酒店外围清洁（PA_007）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_007 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

The driveway and the exterior of the hotel perimeter should be well cleaned and maintained and in good condition at all （公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 酒店外围清洁

## 目的

The driveway and the exterior of the hotel perimeter should be well cleaned and maintained and in good condition at all times.
经常对饭店外围及车道进行清洁维护，为客人提供高度清洁的环境。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

The hotel’s driveway and exterior perimeter should be cleaned and maintained every two hours per AM shift and PM shift.
酒店的车道及入口处日班及中班每隔二小时巡视清洁, 以保证良好的外观.
Driveway floor washing with diesel engine
用高压水枪，清洗车道。
The driveway should be cleaned with the sweeper and then hosed down with water. Only can do it in the midnight
车道外围每天清扫而且要用水去冲洗, 但只能在半夜进行
Ready tools: high pressure cleaner, water pipe, 220volt cycle, straight squeegee, extension poles.
准备好工具：高压水枪，水管，220伏特电压，橡胶扫帚，伸缩杆。
Block out the driveway and place the notice sign before washing the driveway.
清洗车道前先要将清洗区域的车道封闭并放警示牌
Ensure that machine switch is in the off position before plugging into the power supply. And use cycle for long distance. Pay more attention for no wet hand to touch electric.
保证插头没插上插座前设备处在关的状态。长距离的话循环使用。注意别用湿手碰电器。
Use the high press water to wash the floor from side to side, push the water to one another side
用高压水枪从一边到一边清洗地面
If has more water, please use straight squeegee to push the water.
如果有更多的水，请用橡胶扫帚推水
At last, use water vacuuming machine to remove the water which is on the driveway floor.
最后，用吸水机吸掉车道上的水
After job, clean all machine.
完成工作后，清洁所有机器。
Empty the water tank which is the high pressure cleaner.
清空高压清洁器中的水桶。
Report any problem to team leader as soon as possible.
出现任何问题尽快报告领班
All plants should be checked and all rubbish and cigarette butts removed.
所有的植物每天检查，将垃圾、烟头收走。
Empty and clean all rubbish bins and ash cans. All rubbish is placed into the garbage compactor every day.
清洁所有的烟灰筒, 每天将所有的垃圾放在指定位置.
All chewing gum and weeds will be removed from walkways.  Cracks and damages should be reported to the PA Supervisor.
所有口香糖和杂草从车道上清除, 发现裂缝、损坏及时汇报给主管
Dust and marks should be removed from all walls and pillars.
墙上及柱子上的污渍及时去除.
Doors should be properly cleaned and special attention given to lower parts where all marks should be removed.
门的清洁应注意门底部的部位，因为经常会有很多污渍。
The lobby windows will be cleaned using glass cleaner if necessary.
大堂玻璃清洁必要时使用玻璃清洁剂。
Lobby entrance door glass and door handle to be clean and polish in time.
大堂正门玻璃及门把手要及时清洁抛光。
Report any lights or malfunction on a maintenance request.
报修所有灯以及设备失灵的情况。
