# 客房瓶装沐浴用品的补充程序（Floor_042）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_042 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

It’s crucial for the Housekeeping Team to follow proper replenishment procedure of the Residential Bath Amenities during（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 客房洗浴用品管理

## 目的

It’s crucial for the Housekeeping Team to follow proper replenishment procedure of the Residential Bath Amenities during daily Housekeeping Cleaning.  Associate familiarize with the process of replenish  Residential Bath Amenities products, and master the standard of changing bath products.
在日常的客房清洁中，客房部团队遵循适当的洗浴设施补充程序是至关重要的。让员工熟知瓶装沐浴用品的补充程序

## 政策规定

Follow the correct procedure for cleaning bottled bath products in guest rooms
按照正确的流程补充客房瓶装沐浴用品

## 程序

Below are the 6 steps on the Residential Bath Amenity bottle replenishment procedures.
以下是客房内瓶装沐浴用品的补充程序：
Check: During stay over housekeeping service and up on checkout, the Room Attendant should check if the bath amenity level reaches the replacement line, damaged or missing. Room Attendant should replace the bottle if any of these scenarios occur.
检查：在打扫住客房或退房时，服务员应检查瓶装沐浴用品的剩余量是否达到更换线、是否损坏或遗失。如果出现上述任何一种情况，服务员应更换瓶装用品。
Removal from Bracket: If   the bath amenity level reaches the replacement line or is damaged, remove the bottle from the bracket with the designated key. Pull the bottle straight up to remove.
从架子上移除：如 果 瓶装洗浴用品剩余量达到更换线或瓶装沐浴用品有损坏，服务员应用指定的钥匙将瓶子从支架上取下。将瓶子笔直向上拉起移出即可。
Insert the new bottle: Before inserting the new bottle on to the bracket, the Room Attendant should ensure the bottles are new, cleaned, and not tampered. Check the bottle label.The bottles are installed in this order from left to right: (1) Shampoo, (2) Conditioner, (3) Shower Gel.
插入新瓶子：将新的瓶装沐浴用品放入支架之前，客房服务员应确保瓶装沐浴用品是全新的、干净的，且没有动过手脚的。查看瓶身的标签。瓶装沐浴用品的安装顺序从左到右依次是:(1) 洗发水(2)护发素(3) 沐浴露。
Prime: Fill the pumps with the bath amenities.  If pumps have an excess product in the nozzle, ensure the nozzle is cleaned accordingly.
准备好：向瓶装沐浴用品中补充对应产品。如果喷嘴上有多余的用品，请确保喷嘴清洁干净。
Final Check: Room Attendant should ensure the bottles are installed in order.  All pumps forward to the same direction as well as the bath amenity above the replacement line.
最终检查：客房服务员应确保瓶装用品是按照相应的次序摆放。所有喷嘴都朝同一方向，且所有用品余量都在更换线以上。
Disposal: Room Attendant should place the consumed Residential Bath Amenity bottle in the designated recycling containers. The bottles should not be disposal of in the normal trash stream.
处理：客房服务员应将使用过的瓶装沐浴产品瓶子放入指定的回收容器中。这些瓶子不应按照正常垃圾处理流程处置。
