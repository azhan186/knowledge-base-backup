# 烘干折叠布草（LDY_006）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_006 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Towels should be fluffy after drying and linen should be smooth after pressing, then folded in good order according to t（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转
- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

Towels should be fluffy after drying and linen should be smooth after pressing, then folded in good order according to the standard required by the housekeeping.
巾类烘干后洁白蓬松，床单类布草熨烫后平整无褶，并均按管家部要求及时折叠，摆放整齐。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

After washing, all towels are to be loaded into the dryers at the recommended capacity, heat to be set between 70 and 90 degrees centigrade for greatest efficiency.
洗过布草, 送去烘干。烘干机温度70至90摄氏度可保证最佳效果。
The drying process takes about 45 minutes before allowing it to run through a cooling down cycle in the dryer of 3 - 5 minutes to prevent wrinkles. This should be a pre-set cycle on the machine.
烘干过程大约需要45分钟，另还需要3至5分钟的冷风处理。
Unload the towels from the dryers promptly and sort them out into different categories and finish folding as soon as possible to prevent excessive wrinkles.
及将烘干布草从机器中取出，分类。巾类需要尽快折叠，避免过多皱纹出现。需要把脏毛巾挑出来回洗，把毛巾上的毛发去掉，毛边的毛巾做报废，少LOGO的需要缝补上商标。
Sacked in the required quantity parcels of 10 pieces for bath towels, 20 pieces for hand towels and bath mat, 50 pieces for face towels.
浴巾10条一打，中巾、地巾20条一打，面巾50条一打。小方巾30条一打装塑料袋。
The bed sheet, duvet cover and pillow case are received from the flatwork ironer and folded according to the standard required by the Housekeeping to improve efficiency between the departments. Then stacked into quantity parcels of 20 pieces for each item.
床单、被套、枕套从大烫机烫出后，按管家部要求折叠，20条一打。
All stained linens must be handled for stain remover treatment. Discarded linen to be marked with colorful mark and will then be used as rags so that none of these items become mixed up with the good linens.
有污迹的布草，必须挑出再处理。无法去除污渍，报废布草会做上不同颜色的记号，避免混淆。报废布草需要做报废流程后踩线做其它用处。
All linens after folding and pressing are to be stacked onto the linen mobile trolley and delivered to the Uniform/Linen room.
所有布草折叠熨烫后摆放于楼层布草车上，运至制服布草房。运送至楼层工作间的布草架上。
每次送干净布草时，需要把下面的布草放在面上（保证布草质量）。
