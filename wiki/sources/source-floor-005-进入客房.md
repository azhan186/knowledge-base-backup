# 进入客房（Floor_005）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_005 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure that guests are NOT being disturb while entering their rooms when services is needed.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 夜床（Turn-down）服务标准
- DND（请勿打扰）处理
- 进入客房礼仪与授权
- 进入客房

## 目的

To ensure that guests are NOT being disturb while entering their rooms when services is needed.
确保提供服务进入客房时客人不受到打扰。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that all hotel employees should follow proper procedures while entering guest rooms, and should respect guest privacy at all times.
所有酒店员工在进入客房时应遵循工作程序，并尊重客人隐私权。

## 程序

Guest Room Entry Time 进入客房的时间
Associates must not enter occupied guest rooms to provide services before 9:00 a.m.
员工不得早于上午9点进入住客房提供服务。
Associates must not enter occupied guest rooms to provide services after 9:00 p.m.; Nightly Refresh Service (Turndown) should be completed by 8:00 p.m. for Loyalty Program Platinum Elite level above members. For Luxury brands (e.g., EDITON, The Luxury Collection, The Ritz-Carlton) which have different standards on the hours of Nightly Refresh Service (Turndown), please follow the brand standards.
员工在晚上9点之后不得进入住客房提供服务；针对万豪旅享家尊贵白金会籍以上会员的夜床服务应在晚上8点前完成。个别奢华品牌（例如：艾迪逊、豪华精选、丽思卡尔顿）对夜床服务的时间有不同要求，请遵循其品牌标准。
Associates may enter occupied guest rooms during prohibited entry times only in the following scenarios:
如遇以下情形，员工可以破例在非许可的时间内进入住客房：
The guest has requested earlier or later guest room services (e.g., Cleaning or Turndown Service).
客人要求早于或晚于常规时间提供客房服务（例如：清洁服务或夜床服务）。
The guest has asked for other services (e.g., bell services, additional bottled water, or room accessories).
客人提出其它服务请求（例如：行李服务、额外的瓶装水或客房备品）。
Associate sees the guest leaving the room and receives guest's permission to enter.
员工看到客人离开房间，并且获得客人允许进入。
Associate has been advised that the guest has checked out.
员工收到客人已退房的通知。
Guest Room Entry Procedures 进入客房的流程
It is important to ensure that you do not inconvenience or embarrass the guest or show any disrespect.
请确保不要给客人带来不便或尴尬，且不要有任何不尊重客人的行为。
Attendant is knowledgeable of the guest’s name prior to knocking on the guest room door to appropriately address the guest by name.
进入客房前先确认客人的姓名以便称呼客人。
Ring the doorbell or knock on the door, announce department and reason for visit: e.g., ‘Housekeeping’, ‘Bell Service’.
按门铃或敲门的同时大声说明来意，例如：“客房服务” 或“行李服务”。
Wait for a response for 10 seconds. If there is no reply, repeat the procedures twice more, if necessary.
等待10秒钟，如无人应答，请重复以上步骤；10秒钟后如仍无人应答，请再次重复以上步骤。
Guest Responds
客人有回应
Give the guest an appropriate greeting, explain the purpose of your visit and request for permission to enter.
先问候客人，说明来意，征得客人同意后方可进入房间。
No Response
客人无回应
If there is no response or action by the third ring, the attendant opens the door at 15-degree, and announces his or her arrival loud enough for the guest to hear.
如果客人仍然没有应答，请打开门至15度角，再次大声说明来意以确保客人能听清。
If the guest is sleeping or if you hear a sound in the bathroom, close the door gently and leave immediately.
如果客人在休息或是您听到浴室里有动静，请轻轻关上门，立即离开。
Always allow sufficient time for guest to get to the door if he/she is inside.
如果客人在房间，应确保客人有充分的时间到门前响应。
If the guest is not present, Housekeeping associates must place the housekeeping cart directly in front of the guest room door when servicing the room. This is to block the area and to prevent other guests from approaching the room. If your hotel does not use housekeeping carts, guest room door must remain closed during the cleaning process. Place a sign on the door handle or doorframe (in brand voice where available) stating that the room is being serviced.
如客人不在房间内，在清洁房间时员工必须将清洁推车横放在客房门前以避免其他客人进入该房间。如您的酒店不使用清洁推车，则员工在清洁过程中需保持客房门关闭。在门外使用符合品牌标准的标识说明该客房正在“服务中”。
Guest Returns During the Service
员工提供服务的过程中客人返回房间
