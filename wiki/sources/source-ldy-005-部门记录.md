# 部门记录（LDY_005）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_005 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Departmental records will be maintained and kept up-to-date, to ensure proper control of costs, revenues and stocks.（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 部门生产记录

## 目的

Departmental records will be maintained and kept up-to-date, to ensure proper control of costs, revenues and stocks.
不断维持更新部门报表，确保部门成本、收入及库房的协调运转

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Production record
生产记录
Collect all the daily production records from all sections and set up each item figures and transfer them into monthly master production report.
洗衣房每天各区域工作量记录，月底汇总。（洗涤/制服公斤数），烘干机清理毛尘记录，晚上关闭所有电源记录，客衣报表记录，客衣洗涤打折/免费熨烫记录等等。
Chemical stock
化学药品情况记录
Do a monthly check on the level of chemicals stock balance to ensure sufficient supply. Request from general stores or raise a purchase requisition if necessary.
化学药品每月盘点一次，确保仓库固定量的储备，如有需要，向总库申请或开采购单定购。每周一次仓库药剂盘点
Printing/package item stock
印刷/包装用品情况记录
Check the level of stock balance monthly for laundry printing/package items. Request from general stores or raise a purchase requisition if necessary.
洗衣房印刷/包装用品每月盘点一次，确保仓库固定量的储备。如有需要，向总库申请或开采购单定购。
Consumption report
物资消耗记录
Maintain monthly chemical and printing/package item consumption reports and submit the complete reports to director of service, including content as below:
维护每月化学品和印刷/包装的消耗报告，并向服务总监提交完整的报告，包括以下内容。
Chemical cost of per kilo laundry
每公斤水洗物品化学药品成本价格
Chemical cost of per kilo dry cleaning
每公斤干洗物品化学药品成本价格
Monthly laundry & valet revenue report, submit to director of service and finance department monthly
洗衣服务营业收入统计,每月交予服务总监及财务部
Linen Inventory submit to housekeeping, and finance every quarter
布草盘点报告每季度上报管家部和财务部
