# 加床标准（Floor_036）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_036 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Rollaway beds for extra guests staying in rooms should be neatly made with spotless bedding.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 婴儿床/加床提供标准

## 目的

Rollaway beds for extra guests staying in rooms should be neatly made with spotless bedding.
为房间内其它客人准备的加床应该和房间内的已经设置好的床保持一致的标准：干净，舒适无污迹

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Collect 1 twin size sheet, 1 rollaway bed duvet with duvet case, 1 rollaway bed  and bed pad,1 pillow with pillowcase.
准备一张标准床的床单，加床被和被套，保护垫，枕头和枕头套。
Check mattress protector for stains or damaged elastic.  Replace if necessary.
检查床护垫是否有污迹或损坏，如果需要及时更换
Place all required linen on a clean sofa.
将所有准备好的布草, 放在干净的沙发上。
To make a rollaway bed please see HOW TO MAKE UP BED.
做加床参看标准与程序 “如何做床”
For storage of bed, place strips firmly over bed, cover with rollaway bed- always store in an upright position on the back area when not in use.
对于加床的存放，将加床用绷带绑紧,用加床套罩好-当不用时将加床立起来放置在后区。
Every rollaway bed should be prepared, ready for use and placed on assigned floor every day.
每一个加床每天应该准备好放在指定的楼层。
Extra amenities will be placed in a room using a rollaway, consisting of:
当房间需要加床时, 以下物品也应该同时补充到房间内：
Extra face towel, hand towel, bath towel, shampoo, condition, bath gel, toothbrush, shower cap, mouth wash, tumbler, bathrobe and slipper
额外增加面巾，中巾，浴巾，洗发水，护发素，沐浴露，牙刷，浴帽，漱口水，漱口杯，浴袍及拖鞋
If is kids, provide kid’s toothbrush, slipper and bathrobe.
如果知道是小孩，就放相应的儿童牙刷、拖鞋及浴袍
Room attendants will change linen daily and replenish extra guest amenities as required.
服务员应该根据要求每天更换客用品和房间内的巾类。
Upon departure all extra amenities will be removed from the room. The room attendant should remove all linen to uniform, and the rollaway bed return to original positions.
如果客人离店，所有备品应该从房间内移出。服务员应该将所有脏布草收出来, 并将加床送回原来的位置
