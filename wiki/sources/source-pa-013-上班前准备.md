# 上班前准备（PA_013）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_013 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Check last shift work, assign working items according to associates’ duty during the shift to finish work on time. Prope（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 标准操作流程

## 目的

Check last shift work, assign working items according to associates’ duty during the shift to finish work on time. Proper hand-over between all shifts should be carried out, to ensure work is completed and all areas in good order.
检查上班次的工作，根据本班次的员工合理安排工作项目，能够保证各班次之间的交接，确保工作任务的完成。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Shift leader to be on duty 10 minutes in advance, handing over and checking work with previous Supervisor.
当班主管每天提前十分钟到岗，与上班次主管交接，查岗。
Record emphasis of work for the shift, special case and uncompleted work from previous shift.
记录当天的工作重点，特殊情况及上班次未完成工作。
Check previous shift work, ensure all areas are clean during hand over time, and machines are in a good condition. Record details in hand over logbook.
检查上班次的工作情况，确保所有区域在交接时清洁，设备完好。将交接结果记录在交班本上。
Inspect and follow up maintenance issues.
检查工程维修事项，并跟催。
Check the cleaning schedule; arrange work according to hand over result and current situation.
查看当天的清洁计划，综合交班内容及实际情况制定当天的工作安排
Check PA attendants grooming, personal hygiene and appearance before start working.
上岗前，根据当班员工的排班检查签到时间和签名，确保签到与实际人数相符。
Check attendance record before start working, ensure PA attendance record is the same as the duty roster.
检查员工的仪容仪表(头发，制服，鞋，首饰等)。
Distribute the working sheet to concerned associate ensure no absence.
将做好的工作单分发给员工，确保人员位置无空缺。
Praise and conclude yesterday’s work on the meeting.
表扬和总结昨天的工作。
Explain today’s task, key points and special work, and answer any questions associates raise.
讲解当日内容，工作要点及特殊工作，语言简练，并询问员工有何问题。
Distribute tools and equipment, and assign associates to their positions.
到库房发放清洁工具和机器，安排员工到位。
