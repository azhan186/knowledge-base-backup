# OOO房间程序（Floor_043）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_043 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Lock and unseal the maintenance room in the OPERA system（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- OOO 房间状态管理
- 客房服务员自放房资格政策
- OOO 房间状态标记

## 目的

Lock and unseal the maintenance room in the OPERA system
在OPERA系统中进行维修房锁房与解封

## 政策规定

Standardize the management system of the maintenance OOO room, so that the guest rooms can reach the maximum sales rate.
规范维修OOO房间的管理制度，使客房达到最大的销售率。

## 程序

1. When the room has the following problems, the （OOO） room needs to be locked in the opera     system:
当房间出现如下问题需要在OPERA系统中锁OOO房
1）There is major maintenance in the room (such as, the ceiling of the room leaking badly and needs to be repaired, room renovation, and so on).
房间出现重大维修的，（如房间天花严重漏水需修补天花、房间翻新改造）等。
2）When the rooms could not be provided to sale due to emergency reasons for inventory control.
因紧急特殊原因，进行房态控制，房间无法对外销售。
2. After confirming with the engineering department and the executive housekeeper, the floor supervisor/floor manager shall report it to the Resident manager, and the executive housekeeper/assistant executive housekeeper will lock the "OOO" room in the system
以上情况楼层主管/楼层经理与工程部和行政管家确认之后，汇报驻店经理并由行政管家/助理行政管家在系统中锁“OOO”房。
3.  At the same time, under the first circumstance, the HSKP must place the order in the TRG system, and the date and reason must be exactly the same as the OOO room in the opera system. In any of the above circumstance, Executive Housekeeper/Assistant Executive Housekeeper Email to Finance Day Audit, Engineering Director, CC to Duty Manager, Finance Director, Director of Rooms, Resident Manager.
同时，在第一种情况下，客房部在TRG系统中下单，日期和原因必须和opera系统中OOO房间完全一致。发生以上任意情况均需由行政管家/助理行政管家发邮件给到财务日审，工程总监，抄送给值班经理，财务总监，房务总监及驻店经理。
3. Floor supervisor/floor manager shall go to the room to check the status of "OOO" room every day, follow up the maintenance of the engineering department and report the maintenance status to the executive housekeeper, until the room is repaired and restored to be ready for sale.
楼层主管/楼层经理每天到房间检查“OOO”房状态，持续跟进工程部的维修并汇报给行政管家维修情况，直到修好恢复成可卖房为止。
4. The executive housekeeper shall check and approve the “OOO” room with the engineering director/engineering manager to ensure that the project problems are repaired, and the executive housekeeper/assistant executive housekeeper will unlocked the room in the system.
行政管家与工程总监对“OOO”房进行核查与审批确保工程问题修复之后由行政管家/助理行政管家从系统解锁。
5. Review Procedure:
The HSKP coordinator prints T114 Out Of Order By Reason Report in Opera every day, selects "Both" in the Room Status option for printing, submits to Executive housekeeper, Director of Engineering, Income Audit for review and sign-off. This report will be included in Daily Executive Packet and reviewed by Director of Finance and General Manager.
审查程序：
每天由客房部文员打印Opera报表T114 Out Of Order By Reason Report，Room Status需选择”Both”。该报表交由行政管家、工程总监、财务审计进行审核并签字确认，纳入每日行政包内，最终交由财务总监与总经理审阅。
