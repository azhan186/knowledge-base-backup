# 失物招领-公共区域（PA_022）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_022 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure Lost and Found procedure are followed properly in order to response to all guest inquires in a timely manner. （公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 涉及失物招领流程（编号/登记/上报）

## 目的

To ensure Lost and Found procedure are followed properly in order to response to all guest inquires in a timely manner. And all PA employees are aware of L&F's operating procedures
确保失物招领程序能被完全地执行，以便在客人询问时能及时地反应。并且所有PA员工都知道L&F的操作流程

## 政策规定

To unify the administration of Lost and Found to ensure the safety of guest’s belonging is to show respect to guest. To ensure the Safety of guest belonging is to show your respect to them and make him/ her feel safe in the hotel. Proper care of guest’s Belongings show our respect. By taking care with our work we show our guest that we are sincere in our efforts to please them.
统一管理失物招领，确保客人财产安全显示出我们对客人的尊重。确保客人财产安全显示出你对他/她的尊重并感到在酒店是安全的。恰当的关注客人财产显示出我们的敬意。我们细致的工作及努力显示出我们的诚挚，让顾客满意

## 程序

LOST AND FOUND-PA
失物招领-公共区域
Finding a lost property item
发现客人遗留物品
The Lost & Found Property Department is situated within the Housekeeping  Department
失物招领处设在客房部办公室内
Items found in any public area of the hotel (guest area or staff area) should be handed in immediately and should not be left elsewhere.It is important that all items found in public areas left by guests or staff are treated as items left behind and recorded.
在酒店内的任何公共区域地方（客人区域或员工区域）发现的物品都应该立即上交，而不能留在其他的地方。所有公共区域发现客人或员工留下的物品都将被视为遗留物品，并将做好记录，这一点很重要。
The found item must be handed to the Housekeeping coordinator on Duty and a Lost
property label and Master book must be filled in, clearing stating.
所有发现的遗留物品必须上交给客房部当班文员，当班文员应清晰的做好遗留
物品标签并在记录本上做好清楚的登记。包括：
Reference number
编号
Date of item found            Name of owner (if known)                  Location where found
发现物品的日期               物主的姓名（如果知道）                    发现物品的位置
Description and condition of found item                                   Name and department of finder
物品描述和情况                                                                      发现者的姓名和部门
Location at which the item is being stored.         Signature of the responsible coordinator
存放地点                                                                        文员签名
The Lost Property Master book should also clearly state the followings for future
use:
遗留物品记录本上还应清楚的填写以下信息做进一步使用：
Date of collection owner / finder and signature
发现者的日期和签名
ID verified
工作证号码
Witnessed by Security staff and signature for valuables only.
如果是贵重物品应有作为见证人的保安部员工的姓名和签字
After all the items being logged in HSKP, the valuable items should be reported to GSM and Security supervisor on duty who is responsible to check the item.
客房部对客人或员工遗留的物品进行登记后，贵重物品需及时报告大堂经理和保安部当值主管，保安部当值主管需对物品进行核对。
Questionable items like baggage, sealed boxes or parcels should be reported to SEC and GSM without being opened or removed. These questionable items need to be checked and confirmed with no danger before delivering to HSKP.
对箱包，包裹纸盒和一切无法判定之物品，酒店员工一旦发现，立即报告保安部和GSM，不得随意打开、搬动，需经检查无危险后送至HSKP保存。
All items regardless of their apparent value (or lack thereof) will be handed into the HSKP for Safekeeping and recording purposes.  The items in question will be held in storage for a period of ninety (90) days or if recognized as a   valuable and attractive item, one hundred and eight (180) days in the Front Office’s safe.
所有物品不论价值必须被上缴客房部保存及记录。这些物品将被保存90天，若属高价值物品将被保存在前厅部保险箱180天。
Valuable items such as credit cards, jewelry, necklaces, mobile phones, cameras and/or cash must be secured in a safety deposit box assigned for L&F only and the separate keys will be held by Guest Service Manager. Both of the Security supervisor and GSM are required to make a registration and sign upon each time opening the deposit box.
贵重物品如信用卡，珠宝，项链，手机，照相机，现金必须保存在指定的前台的遗留物品专用的保险箱内，钥匙由大堂经理保存。每次开保险箱都必须由保安和大堂经理一起登记，签字。
Housekeeping clerk will input the L&F record into the opera system every day, to ensure the employee can reply immediately when guest ask.
客房文员每天将遗留物品记录输入opera系统，以便客人查询时可以第一时间回复
