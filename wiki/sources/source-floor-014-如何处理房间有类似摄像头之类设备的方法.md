# 如何处理房间有类似摄像头之类设备的方法（Floor_014）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_014 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Instructing employees on how to find and handle devices like cameras in the room.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 可疑电子设备（疑似摄像头）处置
- 可疑设备上报

## 目的

Instructing employees on how to find and handle devices like cameras in the room.
指导员工如何发现和处理房间有类似摄像头之类的设备。

## 政策规定

Ensure all the employees know how to deal with, and apply what they have learned to the work.
让所有员工都知道处理方法,将所学到的运用到工作当中去。

## 程序

When room attendant enter the guestroom, they should conduct a quick visual inspection to see if there are any out-of-place devices that resemble a hidden camera.
当客房服务员进入客人房间时，应该进行快速的视觉检查，看看是否有不合适的设备，如摄像头。
If the room attendant notices that there is a camera-like device in the room, they should report it immediately to their supervisor and security.
如服务员发现类似摄像头等设备，应该报告上级和保安部。
Management will notify the guest that we have discovered a camera-like device operating in their guestroom and will confirm with the guest if the camera-like device belongs to them. If it does not belong to the guest, we will remove the said device and turn it in to Security for investigation and follow up with the police if any illegal recording is noticed. If it belongs to the guest, we would let the guest know that we respect their privacy and would not disturb and/or interrupt the recording as such. Therefore, we will suspend servicing of the said guestroom until the camera is turned off and properly stowed. This is to protect our room cleaning LSOP which should remain confidential and to protect the privacy of our associates who may not have consented to be recorded while working in a guestroom.
管理部门将通知客人，我们发现一个摄像装置运行在他们的客房，与客人确认摄像之类的设备是否属于他们。如果不属于客人，我们将移除该装置并将它交至安保部，并调查是否有违法记录被录入。如有违法摄像记录，应与执法部门联系做相应的处理。如果属于客人，我们会让客人知道我们尊重他们的隐私，不会干扰或中断的记录等。所以，我们将暂停该客房服务直到摄像设备关闭，妥善存放。这是保护我们房间清洁工作程序的保密性，以确保那些可能不同意工作过程被记录的员工的隐私。
