## 政策规定

Every day, the fitness center sends towels using by FC linen cart. Before sending them to Laundry space tap the number and make a record.
每日健身中心送洗毛巾装入指定布草车，送往洗涤中心前自行轻点数字并做好记录。
Daily deliver linen time at 8:00am, and received at 12:00, in high-season additional received at 4pm per every day.
每日送洗时间为8:00am，每日取回时间为12:00，夏季高峰期16:00额外增加一次送回。
After retrieving the linen daily, counting the towel and fold it into the towel cabinet.
每日取回布草后进行清点并折叠好放入装用毛巾柜。
