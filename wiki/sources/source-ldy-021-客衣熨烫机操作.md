# 客衣熨烫机操作（LDY_021）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_021 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure all garments are not wrinkled or as few creases as possible after being pressed carefully to meet or exceed guest（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

Ensure all garments are not wrinkled or as few creases as possible after being pressed carefully to meet or exceed guest expectations.
所有客衣必须仔细熨烫，确保无褶皱或少褶皱，达到甚至超过客人期望。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Ensure that the pressing machines are in perfect working condition with clean pads/surfaces before any operation.  Inform Laundry shift leader if there is any fault.
操作前检查机器状态，拍机垫布是否干净。如有问题及时通知洗衣房领班。
The pressing machine is to be switched on at least half an hour before operation. The presser must be heated and maximum steam supply has to be checked before any items can be pressed.
机器预热半小时，确保蒸汽已充分到位再操作。
Sort the garments into categories.
衣物的分类
Hand iron garments
手工熨烫衣物
Shorts and handkerchiefs
短裤、手帕类
Long and short sleeved shirts
长短袖衬衫
Ladies items and trousers
女式衣物、西裤
Put aside any torn or stained items for mending or re-spotting
存在问题衣物
Express item and garment with special instruction are separately pressed in advance.
加快或有特别要求的衣物提前单独熨烫。
Items need hand iron such as handkerchief and shorts must be given to presser first after handling Express item and garment with special instruction.
除非加快或特殊要求，手帕短裤类首先交烫工熨烫。
Pressing a Shirt
熨烫衬衫
Using the collar and cuff machine, place the shirt collar and cuff with the inside part facing the buck, and press the pressing buttons by using both hands at the same time. Press for 10 seconds and release it by pressing the raise button.
使用领袖烫机：将衬衫领袖放置机器表面拱起处，面料整理平整。双手同时按动熨烫启动键，10秒 种后单手按动熨烫板升起键。取出领袖。
Using the sleeve machine, put one sleeve flatly on pressing board then press the pressing buttons.
使用烫袖机：将袖子平放入机器，固定。按动熨烫启动键。
Using the collar and cuff machine, place the shirt collar and cuff with the inside part facing the buck, and press the pressing buttons by using both hands at the same time. Press for 10 seconds and release it by pressing the raise button.
使用领袖烫机：将衬衫领袖放置机器表面拱起处，面料整理平整。双手同时按动熨烫启动键，10秒种后单手按动熨烫板升起键。取出领袖。
Using the sleeve machine, put one sleeve flatly on pressing board then press the pressing buttons.
使用烫袖机：将袖子平放入机器，固定。按动熨烫启动键。
Press for 10 seconds and release it, take out the sleeve carefully without any crease. Follow same step to press another sleeve.
10秒种后按动烫板升起键，小心取出袖子。同样方法熨烫另一片衣袖。
Press two shirt shoulders by using the body bosom yoke press.
用小烫机分别熨烫衬衫两肩部。
Fit in the front part of shirt on pressing board. Press the pressing buttons to get two pressing boards clipped, holding shirt neck.
双手将衬衫 一前片放入机器，固定。按着熨烫启动键，使上下烫板合起，夹住衬衫颈部。
Release two pressing boards and remove shirt forward to press lower front part of the shirt.
按动烫板升起键，将衬衫向前提，同样操作方法重点熨烫衬衫下摆
Press the raise button to release the shirt and follow the same steps to press another side of shirtfront and two pieces of shirt back. Check the area for parts that need to be touched up such as pleats on the cuff, armpit, under the collar and do the finishing by using the steam iron.
再次按动烫板升起键取出衬衫，同样方法熨烫衬衫前片另一面及两后片。检查机器较难烫到的位置如：领下、袖处、褶皱等。如不平整必须用熨斗再次修整。
