# 布草清洗程序（LDY_016）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_016 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All linen must be washed with the correct procedures, using proper detergent to ensure the best washing procedure to ext（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 布草盘点/管理/轮转

## 目的

All linen must be washed with the correct procedures, using proper detergent to ensure the best washing procedure to extend service life of linen and improve customer satisfaction.
以正确程序洗涤所有布草，加入适量清洁剂，确保最佳洗涤程序来延长布草使用寿命，并提高客人的使用感受。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Prepare the proper weight load in the linen trolley and subsequently load directly into the washing machine for process. Close the machine door.
准备适当的布草洗涤量装机，关紧机器门。早上检查药剂是否充足，80%的装载量。
Select the right designed program and press the formula number. Start the computer program and wait for the beep sound to start running.
选择合适的洗涤程序号码，启动电脑程序，等待机器声响提示开始运行。
Chemical supplies are automatically inserted by the dispensers which are at the top right-hand side of the machines. Time, temperature, power, water level, chemical and mechanical actions are programmed and flashed in the micro processed display panel for a complete process.
机器的右上侧是自动冲料口。机器内部时间、温度、能量、水位、洗衣粉用量及机器运作都已设定。任何步骤均有机器内部电脑提示。
Every washing cycle will refer to the above three steps to ensure good effect.
任何洗涤程序需要参照以上三步程序，以确保良好的洗涤效果。
For F&B linen, 30 oz of starch is added during the last rinse in large washer. (250 pounds)
餐饮布草漂洗过程中250 磅机器需要加入30盎司浆粉。（目前晚安巾需要加浆粉）
Do not shorten the program as this would result in poor effect.
严禁缩短洗涤时间或步骤影响洗涤效果。
Preparation of soiled linen for the next load to process should be taken place once the machine is set in for operation.
准备下一机需洗涤布草。
When the washing cycles are completed, unload machine and distribute laundry.
洗涤程序结束后，停止机器，取出布草。
Put the clean bed sheets and duvet covers into the clean trolley, to be sent directly to the flat work section for immediate ironing.
洗干净的床单、被套装入干净布草车内，直接送至大烫机熨烫。
Table cloth, napkin and pillow case are loaded into the drying tumbler for conditioning on cool temperature for about 5 minutes, then put them into the clean trolley, and sorting them out for feeding into the flat work ironer for finishing process.
餐饮棉台布、口布、晚安巾、楼层枕套冷风烘干5分钟后,放在干净布草车内，再送至大烫机处分好类别后熨烫。
Towels are unloaded from the washer to the clean trolley and loaded into the drying tumbler for drying process which takes about 50 minutes.
用干净布草车将洗过巾类装车并烘干约50分钟。
Check linen quality after washing and rewash it if needs.
检查布草洗涤质量，如有需要返洗。
