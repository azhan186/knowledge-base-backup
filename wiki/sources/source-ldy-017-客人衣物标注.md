# 客人衣物标注（LDY_017）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_017 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All Guests garments are must be carefully checked and verified number of pieces. All guest laundry must be labelled at p（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- VIP/贵宾房准备与鲜花预定
- 客衣打码/标注防错

## 目的

All Guests garments are must be carefully checked and verified number of pieces. All guest laundry must be labelled at proper positions before the laundry process.  Garment with problem outlined below must be picked out for guest contact.
所有客衣洗前必須仔细检查，核对件数,在合适的位置打码或系上码条，存在以下问题的客衣必须挑出待与客人联系。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Express and VIP Laundry must be picked out for checking and marking first.
提前检查并打码加快及贵宾的客衣。
Open one guest laundry bag at a time, sort and check that the number of garments tallies with the guests’ list, write in the count of ‘Hotel Count’ column. Then commence the marking of the garments in that bag. Remember to mark one bag at a time to avoid confusion.
一次打开一包客衣，对照洗衣单按类別检查件数，在酒店核点一栏内注明件数，杜绝与其它房间的衣物混淆。
Switch on the marking machine for ten minutes for heating before starting to mark. Replenish the marking tape daily according to load system.
打开打码机，预热10分钟，确定码条的装载情況。
Check all the guest garments including underwear. Any damage, piece difference, color fade and washing influence must be noted on laundry list. After being checked again by GRM and guest contacted by telephone, the garments can be washed /dry cleaned if guest permits. If guest could not be informed, a “laundry discrepancy/defect note” will be completed and put into guestroom as soon as possible. Garment with problem will not be handled until guest approves.
检查包内所有衣物，包括内衣。所有影响衣物洗涤的因素如破損、染色褪色、件数的多与少等都必須详细记录在洗衣单上，同時报告宾客服务经理，及时与客人电话联系，客人允许后方可洗涤，如客人暂时无法联系，填写“洗涤衣物缺陷/不符通知”卡，从房门下送入客房，以便及时通知客人。存在问题的客衣必須得到客人的许可后方可处理。
All buttons must be undone and the pockets to be cleared. Prepare correct room number tallies with the laundry list. Try to mark right number on list first.
所有纽扣必须解开，所有口袋必須清理。选择合适的号码。先在洗衣单上试打房号。
Shirts/Blouses:  The marking numbers to be at the second last button hole from the bottom of garment.
衬衫: 衬衫下摆最后两个扣眼之间
Trousers/Shorts:  On the cloth of back pocket
西裤/短裤: 后口袋布上
Dress/T-Shirt:  At the inside seam on the front bottom of the garment
连衣裙/T恤 衫: 衣物前片下摆内侧缝边
Jacket/Silk:  Using a piece of marking tag marked with right number and pin it in the buttonhole with a safety pin.
外套: 用安全别针将码条別在扣眼里
Under Garment:  At the inside seam or inside on the back waistline
内衣: 下摆内侧缝边或后腰面料较厚处
Socks:  Mark on the edge of the foot part
短袜: 袜根处 打在码条上
The laundry which found valuable article inside the pocket, if the value below 500 CNY need to package in the plastic bag, mark the room number and report to laundry supervisor.
送洗衣物口袋内如有客人物品，价值在500元以下的。需用包装袋放客人的物品，标注房号并报告给洗衣房主管。
If the value above 500 CNY, need to report laundry supervisor and GRM, then handle it with lost and found process, and given to GRM kept in safe box.
送洗衣物口袋内客人物品，如价值在500元以上的。需立刻报告给洗衣房主管和宾客服务经理，然做遗留物品流程后交至前台保险箱。
Remove old marking tags during checking or marking guest garments.
宾客衣物上陈旧码条必须撕去。
For marking Express and VIP garments, red color tape is used attached together with the marking tape.
加快及贵宾洗衣打码時系上红色码条。
For special instructions: starch, or hold Laundry, the different color fiber roll tape is used and means different instruction.
特別要求的客衣如上浆、小心洗涤等用不同顏色的码条系上以示区分。
The guest garments’ making with refer to the following.
客衣打码参照下面。
For example: First laundry bag of 16th , Second laundry bag of 16th , Third laundry bag of 16th
如：16号第一包衣服（16G001）、16号第二包衣服（16G002）、16号第三包衣服（16G003）
To mark the same number on the laundry list, and tally with the garments’ number.
洗衣单上打一样的码，和衣服上的码匹配。
Express and VIP Laundry after marking must be handed over to wash group for      process without delay.
加快及VIP客衣打码后提前交洗涤组洗涤。
