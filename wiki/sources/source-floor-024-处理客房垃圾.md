# 处理客房垃圾（Floor_024）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_024 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure all guestroom trash are handled in a safe manner.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 垃圾/废纸篓处理
- 客房垃圾分类

## 目的

To ensure all guestroom trash are handled in a safe manner.
确保所有客房垃圾被最可靠地处理掉。

## 政策规定

All other trash is emptied into designated container in the service area immediately.
所有其他的垃圾应被立即倒进服务区指定的容器内。

## 程序

The garbage in the room is recyclable and non-recyclable. Employees classify the rubbish by category when dumping into trash bag
客房垃圾分可回收及不可回收。员工在倾倒垃圾时，根据类别归类。
The employee must confirm that the garbage is collected in the room before it is collected. If the garbage is in the trash bin, it should also be confirmed before it is dumped
员工在客房收垃圾之前一定要确认是垃圾才能收出，如果是在垃圾桶内的垃圾在倾倒之前也需确认
Empty all bedroom and bathroom trash containers into trash container in the service area.
清空洗手间所有垃圾，将其倒入服务区的垃圾容器中。
Employees shall not directly grab rubbish to avoid hand injury
员工不得将手直接抓取垃圾，以免手受伤
Do not leave trash in the guest corridor.
客人走廊上不得留下垃圾
Do not put trash or Cigarette butts down the toilet.
不得将垃圾冲入马桶。
If the garbage has strong odor, it should be poured into the garbage bag and tied tightly at the mouth of the bag. The smelly garbage should be taken away from the floor area and thrown into the garbage room as soon as possible
如果垃圾有浓重的异味，需倒入垃圾袋并将袋口打结扎紧，尽快将有异味垃圾带离楼层客区，扔至垃圾房
If the rubbish is broken glass, it should be put into the garbage bag separately and sent to the garbage room separately
如果垃圾为碎玻璃，需单独装入垃圾袋，并单独送至垃圾房
