# 沙发清洁（PA_015）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_015 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure daily presentation of the sofas and chairs should be are maintained to the highest standard of clean at all times（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 吸尘器使用规范
- 沙发清洁

## 目的

Ensure daily presentation of the sofas and chairs should be are maintained to the highest standard of clean at all times.
确保饭店内沙发、椅子得到合理的保养。在任何情况下都保持清洁。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Vacuum the sofa thoroughly and remove spots on them and stains from it.
先将沙发彻底吸尘，去除污渍。
Lay discarded condemn linen under the sofa before doing cleaning, to prevent carpet floor from getting dirty.
在沙发下铺好报废布草。以免弄脏地面
Fix all necessary parts into Upholstery Dry Foam Extractor and test it before cleaning.
检查沙发机。安装好全部配件
Pour the diluted carpet liquid into the Upholstery Dry Foam Extractor clean tank.
稀释地毯水，倒 入沙发机净水箱中。
Place notice signs around cleaning area.
在清洁区域周围设置告示牌。
Start the machine. When you see the foam coming out of the brush, you can start cleaning the surface of the sofa. When finished, vacuum away the remaining foams remained.
启动机器。当泡沫从刷头中出来后，用刷子刷洗沙发表面。并用吸扒将表面残余的泡沫吸走。
Wipe the woodwork of the sofa with a clean rag.
清洗完毕后，用抹布将周围木器上的清洁剂擦净并用鼓风机吹干。
Clean the Upholstery Dory Foam Extractor before put returning it into the storeroom.
清洁沙发机，存放到库房
