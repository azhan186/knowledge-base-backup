# 失物招领（Floor_004）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_004 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure lost and found procedure are followed properly in order to respond to all guest inquires in a timely manner. A（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 涉及失物招领流程（编号/登记/上报）

## 目的

To ensure lost and found procedure are followed properly in order to respond to all guest inquires in a timely manner. And all employees are aware of L&F's operating procedures (including front office, housekeeping and security)
确保失物招领程序能被完全地执行，以便在客人询问时能及时地反应。并且所有员工都知道失物招领的操作流程（包括前厅部，管家部和安保部的所有员工）

## 政策规定

Any item picked up within the confines of the hotel are required to be delivered to housekeeping department. Any lost & found items are required to be properly logged and bagged. Employees should record information including their name, the room number and the date the item was found, and all items should be kept in a plastic bag and be attached a tag for easy identification. Then the clerk should turn it into the lost & found.
在酒店范围内拾到任何物品均需立即交由客房部，所有丢失物品应被登记及包装，应记录包括员工姓名，房号，日期等相关信息。并装袋封存贴上标签，以便认别。然后文员将遗留物品保存在失物招领处。

## 程序

Should any item be reported lost within the confines of the hotel, all inquiries are to be directed to the HSKP which is responsible for the recording and storage of all unclaimed items.
任何在酒店范围内丢失的物品提出丢失，应直接向客房部提出，该部门负责记录及保存所有无人认领的物品。
These items must be logged and include:
所有失物应被登记，包括以下项目：
Item description
物品描述
Picking up date, time and location
拾获地点、日期、时间
Picked up by who (name, department, contact telephone number)
拾获人部门、姓名、联系电话
Location at which the item is being stored.
存放地点
Return or disposal information
归还及处理信息
After all the items being logged in HSKP, the valuable items should be reported to GRM and security supervisor on duty who is responsible to check the item.
客房部对客人或员工遗留或遗留物品进行登记后，贵重物品需及时报告宾客服务经理和保安部当值主管，保安部当值主管需对物品进行核对。
Questionable items like baggage, sealed boxes or parcels should be reported to SEC and GRM without being opened or removed. These questionable items need to be checked and confirmed with no danger before delivering to HSKP.
对箱包，包裹纸盒和一切无法判定之物品，酒店员工一旦发现，立即报告保安部和宾客服务经理，不得随意打开、搬动，需经检查无危险后送至HSKP保存。
All items regardless of their apparent value (or lack thereof) will be handed into the HSKP for safekeeping and recording purposes.  The items in question will be held in storage for a period of ninety (90) days or if recognized as a valuable and attractive item, one hundred and eight (180) days in the front office’s safe.
所有物品不论价值必须被上缴客房部保存及记录。这些物品将被保存90天，若属高价值物品将被保存在前厅部保险箱180天。
Valuable items such as credit cards, jewelry, necklaces, mobile phones, cameras and/or cash must be secured in a safety deposit box assigned for L&F only and the separate keys will be held by GRM. Both of the security supervisor and GRM are required to make a registration and sign upon each time opening the deposit box.
贵重物品如信用卡，珠宝，项链，手机，照相机，现金必须保存在指定的前台的遗留物品专用的保险箱内，钥匙由宾客服务经理保存。每次开保险箱都必须由保安和宾客服务经理一起登记，签字。
Housekeeping clerk will input the L&F record into the opera system every day, to ensure the employee can reply immediately when guest ask.
客房文员每天将遗留物品记录输入opera系统，以便客人查询时可以第一时间回复
Days of found items kept in L&F:
物品的保存期限
90 days for regular items
一般物品保存90天
365 days for valuables and cash (the item is valued at more than RMB500.00)
贵重物品及现金保存365天 (价值500元以上的物品)
Beverage: opened bottles should be poured out after 48 hours. Unopened bottles should be kept as regular items.
酒水：开瓶的，48小时后销毁；未开封的，按一般物品保存90天
Food: opened food disposed after 48 hours; unopened food kept for 3days(in accordance with the food condition)
食品：开封的，48小时后销毁；未开封的，保存3天。（视食品状况为准）
Disposal of unclaimed items:
物品保存到期后无人认领的处理方法
Regular personal items: will be given to the person who picked it up with director of service approval.
一般私人物品：经服务总监批准，可将物品交给拾获者
Beverage, cash & values: will be credited to the associates’ relation account at the discretion of GM.
酒水、现金、贵重物品，由总经理决定物品的处理方式。
