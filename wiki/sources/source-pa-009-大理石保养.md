# 大理石保养（PA_009）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_009 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Marbles will be crystallized with the right method to ensure clean and shining marble at all times.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 大理石地面保养

## 目的

Marbles will be crystallized with the right method to ensure clean and shining marble at all times.
使酒店的大理石得到合理的保养，保持其清洁光亮。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Marble Crystallization
大理石结晶
Prepare all necessary accessories.
准备清洁所需的全部工具。
Single Scrub Machine         b)  Wet/dry vacuum cleaner          c)  Needle Base
单擦机                                          吸水机                                             针盘
d)   Red Mat              	              e)   Measure cup                             f) Bucket
红垫                                	        量杯                                                 水桶
g)   Notice sign               	  h)   Polishing machine
告示牌                                          抛光机
Place notice signs around cleaning area.
在清洁区域周围设置告示牌。
Wash the cleaning area thoroughly with single scrub machine and purpose cleaner.
先将清洁区域彻底洗净。
Compound the crystal power 1:1 in measure glass with water. Apply on the marble evenly. NOTE: The area should be around 3-5m2 at a time.
用量杯以1:1配制结晶粉。均匀地涂在理石上。注意每次面积不要太大(3-5M2)
Fix needle base and Red Mat on the single scrub machine. Turn on the machine, rubbing the areas applied the compound until the marble become shinning.
使用单擦机。安装针盘、红片。在涂好结晶剂的区域研磨，直至地面光亮。
If the corner area is encountered, it can be polished by hand throwing machine (especially small areas can be polished by hand red Mat)
如遇到边角区域可使用手抛机进行打磨（特别小的区域使用手工红片打磨）
After crystallization, wash the area again with single scrub machine and absorb the dirty water with wet vacuum cleaner
结晶完毕后，用吸水机将地面吸净，并用尘推将地坪推干净
Clean all accessories and take them back to the storeroom.
清洗全部的工具，存放在库房。
Daily cleaning.
日常清洁
Mop every day to remove any stains discovered.
每天推尘，发现污渍及时去处
Mop should be pushed in a straight line, do not omit any area.
推尘需以直线的推，避免遗漏
