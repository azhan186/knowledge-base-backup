# 室内禁止吸烟标准（Floor_008）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_008 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure all associates know clearly about the non-smoking indoor standard, and the importance of dissuasion and report（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 室内禁烟标准与处置

## 目的

To ensure all associates know clearly about the non-smoking indoor standard, and the importance of dissuasion and reporting. Fully understand the whole operation process
让员工清楚地知道酒店相关禁烟制度，知道劝阻及上报的重要性。 清楚地知道并掌握整个的操作流程。

## 政策规定

Comply with national regulations to create a non-smoking environment for the hotel
遵守国家规定，为酒店创造一个无烟的环境

## 程序

According to <Shanghai Non-Smoking Ordinance> effective from March 1st, 2017. Smoking will be banned in all indoor public area, workplaces and public transport in Shanghai. Violators can be fined up to 200 yuan for each individual and 30,000 yuan for each venue.
根据《上海公共场所控制吸烟条例》规定，2017年3月1日起，对上海的室内公共场所，室内工作场所，公共交通工具内部将会全面禁止吸烟。违者个人最高罚款200元，场所最高罚款30000元。
No smoking signs will be set up in public area and restaurants of hotel. Guest room are completely smoke - free. When checking in, the guest will be reminded that if he/she smokes in the room, he/she will be charged a cleaning fee (RMB 1,800), which will be specified on the registration form of the guest.
酒店在公共场所及餐厅将设立禁止吸烟标志。客房全面禁烟，客人在办理入住时将会提醒客人如果在房间里吸烟，将会收取一定的清洁费（人民币1800元），在客人的入住登记单上会有详细的注明。
All employees shall be responsible for supervision and reporting
全体员工应承担监督和举报职责。
The hotel has designated smoking points in the outdoor area. Guests must smoke at the designated location at the front entrance of the hotel, and employees must smoke in designated places.
酒店在室外区域设立指定吸烟点，客人必须在酒店正门口指定地点吸烟，员工必须在员工指定地点吸烟。
Staff must promptly dissuade if guest smoking in public area. Report and hand over to GRM if guest doesn’t listen.
酒店员工如果在公共区域看到客人吸烟，应及时进行劝阻。如不听劝阻的应及时报宾客服务经理，宾客服务经理会进行跟办。
If found employee smoke indoor of hotel, the discipline action will be given.
如发现员工在大楼的任何场所吸烟，将给予违纪处理。
The procedure of if found guest smoking in the room
客房发现抽烟的操作流程：
Staff must promptly dissuade if found guest smoking in room, and report to clerk and supervisor
客房服务员在做房间时，若发现有客人正在吸烟应及时进行劝阻并报给办公室文员及主管。
If guest not in room and found cigarette butt in the room, take pictures and report to the office clerk and supervisor.
当客房服务员做房时, 发现有烟蒂要及时进行拍照，并报给办公室文员及主管。
Clerk immediately inform GRM and make a record after received report.
文员在接到报告后, 及时联系宾客服务经理并做好记录。
GRM shall continue to follow up the guest's persuasion upon receiving the report. If the guest continues to smoke in the room, remind guest that the hotel will charge cleaning fee, then write the incident report.
宾客服务经理接到报告需持续跟进对客人劝说。并提醒客人如客人还是在客房内吸烟的话，酒店将收取相应的清洁费,并写事件报告
On second day, if room attendant found same guest still smoking, report to office clerk and supervisor immediately。The supervisor must report to GRM and record in supervisor daily hand over book.
客房服务员发现相同的客人，第二天仍在房间吸烟，应立刻报给办公室文员及主管。楼层主管必须通知宾客服务经理并在每天交接本上做好登记.
Before stat to clean the room, take photo and send to GRM
服务员拍照取证后打扫房间，照片发给经宾客服务经理。
The GRM informed guest we will charge the cleaning fee.
大堂经理再次跟客人沟通，收取清洁费。
The procedure of found the guest smoking in the public area and restaurant.
公共区域及餐厅发现抽烟的操作流程：
Staff must promptly dissuade if found guest smoking in public area, report and handover to GRM immediately if guest doesn’t listen. (The PA attendant need inform office clerk and PA supervisor)
酒店员工如果在公共区域看到客人吸烟，应及时进行劝阻。如不听劝阻的应及时报宾客服务经理，他/她进行跟办。（客房部员工及时报给办公室文员及主管）
The GRM and L&P on-duty arrived at the scene to dissuade.
宾客服务经理与防损部到达现场对客人进行劝阻。
