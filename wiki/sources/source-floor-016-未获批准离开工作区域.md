# 未获批准离开工作区域（Floor_016）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_016 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure consistent shift coverages and know the employee’s location in case of emergency.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 员工擅自离岗处理

## 目的

To ensure consistent shift coverages and know the employee’s location in case of emergency.
确保工作时间的一贯性并在发生紧急情况时能知道员工的位置。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that the management team are responsible for the whereabouts scheduled employees in their department and for maintaining proper staffing at all times. For this reason, you must not leave your assigned work area without first notifying your supervisor or manager. If you need to leave the area due to certain reasons, you must notify your immediate supervisor or manager.
管理人员负责其部门员工的行踪，并且在任何时候保持员工的人数。因此你不能在未通知主管或经理的情况下离开工作区域。如因特殊原因要离开，则必须通知直属主管或经理。

## 程序

Employee must first notify their supervisor if they are going to leave the work area.
员工如需要离开工作区域则必须首先通知主管。
Employees leaving for lunch，once received call from office to arrange the task, he/she should put down his/her cutlery immediately and finish the task before you continue eating.
员工离开午餐,一旦接到办公室电话有任务安排，需立刻放下手下餐具，完成任务后再继续用餐。
Employees cannot leave an occupied room unfinished (unless there is an emergency.)
员工不得离开还未打扫完的住客房（除非遇紧急情况）。
Employees leaving for lunch or went to another floor for clean should inform floor supervisor.
员工离开午餐或去其他楼层打扫等原因，必须告知主管。
If an employee needs to leave the floor for personal reasons, he/she must obtain the consent of housekeeping manager or director of service before leaving.
员工如因为个人原因需请假离开楼层的，必须经过客房部经理或服务总监同意方能离开。
