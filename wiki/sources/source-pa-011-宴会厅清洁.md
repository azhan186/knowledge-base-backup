# 宴会厅清洁（PA_011）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_011 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

The banquet rooms should be maintained clean and tidy at all times.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 餐厅/宴会厅清洁
- 吸尘器使用规范

## 目的

The banquet rooms should be maintained clean and tidy at all times.
确保宴会厅每天保持高标准的清洁状态。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Check Daily Event Banquet Orders for access.
检查当天的宴会记录。
Vacuum the carpet area in banquet, ballroom and foyer. Remove spots if necessary.
宴会厅及休息区地毯吸尘, 局部去渍。
All windows should be kept clean. Wipe the windowsill with wet clean cloth.
所有窗户清洁, 湿擦窗台及护板。
Pictures, wallpaper and mirrors should be dusted every day and deep cleaned twice a month.
壁画、壁纸、镜子每日抹尘, 每月两次彻底清洁。
Brass articles should be wiped every day and polished twice a week
所有铜器每日抹尘，每周两次抛光。
Clean all power points switches （not use wet duster） and telephones in public area. Sterilize the telephones once a week.
清洁所有公共区域电器插座(不用湿抹布)、开关及电话, 电话每周一次消毒。
Maintain the cleanliness in restrooms and elevator halls. Empty and clean standing ashtray regularly. Change white sands in standing ashtray every day. Replenish restroom amenities.
保持电梯间及洗手间的卫生。随时倾倒清洁烟灰桶，白砂每天更换一次。及时补充洗手间的备品。
Shampoo the carpet of the ballroom monthly or at other times if needed.
每月清洁宴会厅地毯。
Clean the air vents monthly.
每月计划清洁空调回风口。
All wooden furniture should be waxed and polished periodically.
计划抛光公共区域木制家具。
Clean banquet rooms when events are over.
每次宴会结束后及时清洁。
Report maintenance and unusual situation to HKP office and PA Supervisor as soon as possible.
及时向管家部办公室及PA主管报告工程维修事项及异常情况。
