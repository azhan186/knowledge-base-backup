# 客房房量计算标准（Floor_040）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_040 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To make sure the hotel can be meet guest requirements at high occupancy.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 客房房量计算
- DND（请勿打扰）处理

## 目的

To make sure the hotel can be meet guest requirements at high occupancy.
确保酒店在客房率较高的情况下,能依照客人不同需求提供高质量的房间。

## 政策规定

Ensure the room attendant get the excess work’s money.
保证客房服务员的超额清洁工作获得回报

## 程序

The room is cleaned by each room. Standard room credit 1, suite room credit 1.5, vice presidential suites credit 4 and presidential suites credit 5. Turn down service 10 rooms’ credit 1. The daily workload standards is 10 rooms.(Trainee daily workload is 8 rooms.) Room exceeding the standard of work is called an excess room, make up that room time is no longer calculated as overtime hours.
服务员房间清洁量按每间房来计算。标准房算1间，套房算1.5间，副总统套房算4间和总统套房算5间,   夜床10间算1间。每天的最低工作标准为10间 ( 实习生每天工作标准8间 )。超出工作标准清洁的房间称为超额清洁的房间，超额清洁房间的时间不再计算为加班时间。
For general cleaning room: Standard room credit 2, suite room credit 4, vice presidential suites credit 5, presidential suite credit 6.  Empty room dust water clean floor
大清洁房间：标准房算2间，套房算4间，副总统套房算5间,总统套房算6间）空房间擦灰放水吸尘拖地4间算1分
According to the above standard row houses, there are still additional need to clean the room, in the room attendant on a voluntary basis by the day shift leader unified arrangements.
如按上述工作标准排房后，仍有额外需清洁的房间，将在客房服务员自愿的基础上由当天早班主管统一安排。
Clean an extra room need to pay RMB 20 for room attendant or trainee.
每超额清洁1间工作标准之外累计的房间数，按照人民币20元的标准支付给客房服务员或实习生。
The room attendants who work overtime on the day off are all over occupied, make up that room time is no longer calculated as overtime hours
休息天加班的客房服务员完成工作量都是超房量，超额清洁房间的时间不再计算为加班时间。
The room attendants who work overtime on the public holiday account as to the country regulation, no over exceeding work in public holiday.
国定假期加班根据国家规定，国定假期加班不安排超额清洁房间。
If the guest is DND/SO/NNS, attendant need report to floor supervisor change another room.
员工如遇客人外宿、请勿打扰或不要打扫等不特定原因，无法完成当天工作量的，需及时报告给楼层主管，调换其他区域房间清洁。
Regulatory measures 管理步骤：
Step one: Regional floor supervisor daily room attendant over clean room volume in the << Daily additional cleaning room statistics >> record.
步骤一：各区域楼层主管每日将客房服务员超额清洁房间的房量,在 “每日超额清洁房间统计表”上作记录。
Step two: after the registration and then by the room attendant himself and the district supervisor to confirm the signature to the housekeeping manager.
步骤二：登记完后再由客房服务员本人及本区域主管确认签字后交给客房经理。
Step three: Housekeeping manager will be registering extra room number in the monthly report.
步骤三：客房经理将每日客房服务员超额清洁的房间数,登记在月度报表。
Step four: the final day of the month, the room attendant to check the total excess clean room number and monthly over clean room statistics, signed confirmation.
步骤四：每月的最后一天，由客房服务员核对当月总的超额清洁的房间数, 并在“月度超额清洁房间统计表”签字确认。
Step five: delivery to director of service for approval.
步骤五：交至服务总监批准。
Step six: after the approval by DOR and submitted to the ministry of DHR and DOF for review, finally submitted to the general manager for approval.
步骤六：房务总监批准后，交由人力资源部及财务部审查后，最后递交给总经理批准。
