# 客衣营收超预算的奖励（LDY_026）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_026 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To maximize Hotel Guest Laundry Revenue by improving laundry service quality and customer satisfaction, as well as to mo（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 客衣营收超预算奖励机制

## 目的

To maximize Hotel Guest Laundry Revenue by improving laundry service quality and customer satisfaction, as well as to motivate the Laundry associates and to reduce turnover.
通过提高客衣洗涤质量及客户对洗衣服务的满意度，最大限度地提高酒店客人洗衣收入，以及激励洗衣员工工作积极性并减少人员流动。

## 政策规定

This is the policy of JW Marriott Shanghai Hotel to motivate and recognize all Laundry Associates contribution to maximize the Hotel Revenue and Profit.
这是上海 JW 万豪酒店的政策，旨在激励和认可所有洗衣房员工为最大限度地提高酒店收入和利润所做的贡献。
DEFINATIAO定义
The incentives program is for Laundry   teams only.
此激励计划只针对洗衣房团队

## 程序

The Laundry department income of 2026 yearly target to achieve and exceed 2026 laundry service revenue. The monthly revenue targets as following:
2026年洗衣房客衣洗涤收入目标为达到并超过2025年洗衣收入预算，每月洗衣收入预算设定如下：
When the actual revenue is achieved and over budget revenue, a 10% percentage of exceed amount as a team reward, used for team building。
当实现实际收入超预算收入，超出预算金额的10%将作为奖励给洗衣房团队用于团队建设。
The actual revenue and budget figures are verified by income auditor base on the monthly P&L.
实际收入和预算数字由收入审计员根据月度损益表进行核实.
Any laundry service rebates and cancellations are not eligible for incentive calculations.
任何洗衣服务折扣和取消都不符合奖励计算。
Once an employee is found to have committed fraud or not compliance with the hotel regulations, the employee’s upsell incentive will be cancelled. Related associate will be subject to terms listed in Employee Hand Book.
一旦员工被发现欺诈或不遵照酒店制度的行为，此员工的提成将会被取消。相关员工会按照员工手册中的有关条款进行处理。
Incentive forms should be settled and signed by Laundry Manager then approved by Director of Rooms. After Director of Rooms approval, it will be sent to Finance for further process.
激励表格应由洗衣房经理填写并签字，然后由房务总监批准。经房务总监批准后，发送给财务部进行进一步审核批准.
Team can be accumulated if the budget is completed or exceed in the case of unused.
团队提成在完成预算或超出预算以后在未使用的情况下可以累计。
REGULATION COMPLIANCE规则遵守
Any staff offending policy and procedures outlined in this policy through the up-selling scheme may be disciplined or terminated immediately.
任何员工违反本政策的增销提成计划所列出的政策和程序，将受到纪律处分或立即开除。
None-qualify associate collect incentive from another employee is strictly prohibited, any misconduct will result in disciplinary action or terminated immediately.
严格禁止无资格员工向其他员工收取提成，任何不当行为将立即给予惩罚或给予解雇处分。
If any unethical actions were found in use transportation selling, the whole month incentive will be cancelled, and disciplinary action will be taken.
如发现员工为得到提成过程中有任何不道德行为，将取消整月的提成，并给予纪律处分。
