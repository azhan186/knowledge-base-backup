# 壁灯、顶灯、台灯及落地灯的清洁（PA_018）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_018 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Ensure guests have a comfort & clean room to stay - delight customer.（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 灯具清洁

## 目的

Ensure guests have a comfort & clean room to stay - delight customer.
确保客人逗留期间感到舒适和干净光亮-让顾客满意。

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Regularly general clean wall and ceiling lamp once time per two weeks, desk or standing lamp once per one week
壁灯及顶灯每两周定期彻底清洁一次，台灯或落地灯每周彻底清洁一次。
Turn off the power to ensure safety at work
关闭电源确保工作中的安全
Wait for the cooling of the lamps and lanterns after cleaning, prevent wet dishcloth to clean when burst
待灯具冷却后清洁，防止湿抹布清洁时爆裂
Remove the lampshades from the wall/ceiling/table/standing
清洁时将灯罩取下放在旁边
Wipe the inside and outside of the lampshades with a wet cloth
用干净的湿抹布清洁灯罩的内外
Clean the lamp with dry cloth carefully.
再用干净的抹布清洁一次
Wipe holder to remove the dust.
用抹布清洁灯架上的 灰尘
Replace lampshades and switch on to test.
将灯罩安装复位并开通电源测试灯具
When using a ladder, safety- precautions must be followed. i.e. assistance to hold the ladder etc.
当使用梯子时，一定要注意安全。例如有人在梯子旁守护
