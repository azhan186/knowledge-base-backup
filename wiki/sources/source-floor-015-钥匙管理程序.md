# 钥匙管理程序（Floor_015）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_015 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

All hotel keys must sign in / out properly in order to ensure guests safety.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 钥匙管理与授权

## 目的

All hotel keys must sign in / out properly in order to ensure guests safety.
所有酒店钥匙的收发必须登记，以确保客人的安全。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel that employees who are issued floor master keys and office keys are held accountable for their proper use and prompt return to the HSKP office at the end of their shift.
所有因需合理使用而被发放楼层钥匙及办公室钥匙的员工，应负责保管好钥匙，并在交班时将它们及时交还给客房部办公室。

## 程序

Guest Room Keys 客房钥匙
Keys left by guest from check-out, vacant room should be passed to the office clerk who will in turn hand over to the front desk counter.
离店房客人留下的钥匙应被交到办公室文员那里，由其将它们交还给前台。
Floor Master Keys楼层主匙
The policy is associates who are issued floor master keys and office keys are held accountable for their proper use and pronto return to the housekeeping office at the end of their shift.
策略是:签署领取万能钥匙的员工必须保证全程控制钥匙的使用，绝不允许将钥匙给其他员工使用，且员工不能
Open a guest room for another associate
为其他员工开启客房门
Use the key for an unauthorized entrance into a guest’s room
未经授权便使用钥匙进入客房
Keys cannot be exchanged / borrowed by others
钥匙不得被交换/借出
Loan the master key to another associate
将钥匙借予其他员工
Allow guest entrance to their room – identification is necessary to ensure the name on the identification matches the registered guest
协助客人进入客房, 须知协助客人进入客房之前，必须确认客人信息，以确保求助人与登记人信息相符。
Return of Key:  钥匙的归还
Keys must be returned upon completion of the assigned tasks but no later than the end of the associate's shift.
员工在使用完钥匙后必须归还钥匙，最迟不得超过该员工下班。
All keys must be sign in at the HSKP office at the beginning of each shift, always follow proper procedure
所有主匙应在交接班前在客房部办公室签收，永远遵守程序处理
Keys are not to be taken off hotel property. If you discover you have taken a key off property, notify your department’s shift supervisor or loss prevention or MOD immediately and return the key the same day.
员工绝不允许将钥匙带离酒店区域。如果你发现你误将钥匙带离了酒店，立刻通知你本部门的当班主管、保安部或值班经理，并在当天归还钥匙。
A daily key audit will be conducted by clerk/ supervisor at the end of the day.
一天工作结束时，文员/主管应每日进行钥匙核对。
Lost keys are to be reported to DOS, DOR, loss prevention or DOE immediately.
钥匙如若丢失必须汇报给服务总监、房务总监、保安部、或工程部总监。
Keys must be returned upon separation of employment or transfer and a note should be made in the file of each associate issued a key.
如若员工合同终止或调离酒店，则必须归还钥匙，并且需在该员工档案中进行备注。
Discipline: 惩罚
In the event of violation of the rules/procedures governing key use, an associate may be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including suspension and discharge, based upon the particulars of the situation.  However, absent extenuating circumstances, the following disciplinary guidelines apply:
如若员工违反相应钥匙管理制度，他/她或将受到相应惩罚。根据实际情况，最高将面临停职或解雇。然而，除非情有可原，否则将严格按照以下条款执行：
If key is taken home, or not turned in at the end of the day or is lost, immediately notify DOS, DOR, loss prevention or DOE.  If the key is not returned that day or is lost, this generally will result in a written warning under Marriott’s progressive discipline policy.
如若员工将钥匙带回家，或未在当天归还或已丢失，立即通知服务总监、房务总监、保安部以及工程部。如若钥匙未在当天归还或已丢失，根据万豪程序，对该员工发一份书面警告
Loaning a key to another person or opening a room for another person, without following proper procedures generally will result in a written warning under Marriott’s progressive discipline policy.
如若员工未按相关规定，将钥匙借予其他人或为其他人打开客房，则根据万豪程序，对该员工发一份书面警告。
Unauthorized entrance into a guest room generally will result in suspension pending termination.
如若员工未经授权便使用房卡进入客房，则将面临停职直至解雇。
Note:
All keys must be sign in at the housekeeping office at the beginning of each shift, always follow proper procedure.
