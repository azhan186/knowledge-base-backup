# 遗失和损坏（Floor_035）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_035 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To be able to monitor losses and breakages incurred in guestroom. To ensure that all losses and breakages replaced in ti（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 物品损坏/遗失责任界定与赔偿

## 目的

To be able to monitor losses and breakages incurred in guestroom. To ensure that all losses and breakages replaced in time.
控制客房物品的遗失和损坏。确保遗失或损坏物品的及时跟换。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Check out rooms (FITs)
退房
The loss and breakage form should be filled up by room attendants and floor supervisors, as soon as possible, when there is a loss or breakage of any hotel property.
当酒店财产有遗失或损坏时，楼层服务员和领班要及时填写物品遗失报损单。
A separate losses and breakage form for linen (terries) losses and breakages should be filled up and handed into linen room so that item can be replaced and included in the linen losses and breakages monthly report.
布草的遗失和报损要单独填写一张遗失报损单交给布草房，确保及时跟换新的布草及每月布草遗失报损报告。
Likewise, a separate losses and breakage form for china glassware and other guestroom amenities is prepared and should be submitted to HK office so that items can be replaced.
同样，瓷器、玻璃器具及其它客房用品的遗失报损单要交给管家部办公室，确保及时跟换新的物品。
If check due out room found losses and breakage, must report to front office and floor supervisor, communication with guest by front office team
如果查退房时发现客房有遗失和报损，需报给前台，前台会跟进与客人的沟通。
Tour groups
团队
The room attendants or supervisor shall use only one loss and breakage form both for linen, china glassware and other guestroom amenities losses and breakages.
楼层服务员或领班只能使用一张布草、瓷器、玻璃器具和客用品的遗失报损单。
The supervisor or HK clerk shall place selling price for each item taken or broken by guest.
主管或管家部文员应提供被客人带走或损坏物品的价格。
Total amount of the loss or breakage will be placed on the L&B form and will be submitted to the director of services for signature.
所有遗失或损坏物品的数量都要记录在遗失报损单上，然后交给服务总监签字。
The loss and breakage form will then be photocopied into two copies, distribution will be as follows:
遗失报损单应再复印2份，分别分发到以下部门：
Original   --  HK Office                                          原件    –  管家部办公室
1 Copy    --  For Linen                                            复印件  –  布草房
1 Copy    --  For GSM            		               复印件  –  大堂经理
Front office manager will receive a copy of the loss or breakage form and will contact the leader of group
前厅部经理会收到遗失报损单的复印件，然后联系团队领队。
Engineering related losses & breakages
与工程有关的遗失和损坏
The floor supervisor shall prepare a loss or breakage form, incident report and repair order for the losses or breakages discovered on the floors.
当在楼层上发现物品遗失或损坏时，楼层领班要填写遗失报损单、事件报告、维修单。
The loss or breakage form, incident report and repair order shall be submitted to the director of services signature.
要把遗失报损单、事件报告、维修单交给服务总监签字。
HK clerk will photocopy duly signed reports.
管家部文员要复印签过名的报告。
Original copies of the L&B form, incident report and repair order will be attached to purchase request or repair order whichever is necessary for replacement.
当需要购买新的物品时，遗失报损单原件、事件报告和维修单要与请购单附在一起。
Other guidelines:
其它
Storekeeper shall also submit a monthly losses and breakage report of guestroom supplies to director of services.
仓库管理员汇总每月的遗失及报损的数量，将报告交给服务总监。
Linen supervisor shall also submit a monthly losses and breakage report of linen to director of services.
