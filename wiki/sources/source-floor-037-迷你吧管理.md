# 迷你吧管理（Floor_037）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_037 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Minibars in the guest rooms should be fully stocked posting and replenished daily by the room attendant, then hand over （客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 迷你吧商品管理与盘点

## 目的

Minibars in the guest rooms should be fully stocked posting and replenished daily by the room attendant, then hand over the minibar list to the office clerk's for additional drinks
客房内的迷你酒吧内的酒水，必须由服务员每天按时抛帐,补充并保持标准配量，然将酒水单交至办公室文员处领取补充的酒水。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Every day, minibars in vacant clean rooms must be checked, to ensure they are at the required par.  Report any discrepancies to the Shift leader.
每天服务员都要检查可卖空房内的酒水, 以确认达到标准配量。如有任何差异，向主管报告。
During check the room status and collect laundry, the room attendant need check minibar consumption in all occupied and check-out rooms.
在每天早上查房态收洗衣的同时，查看所有住客房和离店的脏房内的酒水。
Any minibar consumption must be posted to guest folio through telephone posting.
任何酒水消费都应该用电话抛帐，及时输入到客人房帐内。
Ensure that charge bills are filled in correctly with consumed items, the date, room status and room number, record person checking.
确保所有的酒水单都正确的填写消费的项目，日期，房间号码，房态及查房人的姓名。
Room attend must record it on the worksheet for future reference.
服务员必须在工作单上记录下来以备日后查询。
If guest is in the room, never use the telephone, instead go back to the pantry and call housekeeping coordinator to post the charge into the guest accounts.
假如客人在房内，不要使用电话，到工作间内打电话到管家部办公室，请文员将客人的酒水消费输入到客人的房帐内。
Room attendant forward minibar list to the office clerk to double check the charges in the computer and get new minibar during lunch time or anytime need to office.
楼层服务员在用餐时间或任何需要到办公室的时候，将酒水消费记录单交到文员处，以便和电脑内的记录做核查。并领取补充的酒水。
Room attendant will replenish the minibar items from their trolley when they make up room . It is also recommended that 2or 3 minibars be replenished at one time if rooms are vacant, to reduce the need to return to the service area pantry stock and inconvenience to the guests.
当服务员做房时，会为客人补充酒水，服务员最好能够同时补充几个房间酒水，这样可以节约时间并减少对客人造成的不便。
Ensure all items are correctly aligned with labels facing front.
确保每样酒水的商标向外并整齐的摆放在小冰箱内同时确保酒水是新鲜的。
