# 火警安全程序（LDY_013）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_013 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Every Laundry team member must follow Laundry safety instruction, evacuation routes and take preventive measures during （洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 火警/安全应急响应

## 目的

Every Laundry team member must follow Laundry safety instruction, evacuation routes and take preventive measures during work.
每位洗衣房员工必须熟记熟知洗衣房安全措施及安全疏散路线并在实际工作中防患未然。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Smoking is prohibited in the laundry
洗衣房内禁止吸烟。厕所里也不能吸烟
Without permission nobody can move the firefighting facilities except in emergencies
除紧急情况任何人未经批准，不得随意挪动洗衣房消防器材
Every team member in the laundry must be familiar with the positions of the firefighting facilities and know how to apply foam fire extinguisher and dry powder fire extinguisher
洗衣房每位员工必须熟悉洗衣房内消防器材的摆放位置，会使用泡沫灭火机和干粉灭火器。
Be familiar with firefighting procedures: when something catches fire, turn on the alarm, inform the switch board and fire control center, cut the electric mains and apply the fire extinguisher nearby. If the fire situation is hard to control, leave through the nearest evacuation route immediately
熟悉消防程序：发生火情立即按动手动报警器并电话通知消防值班中心切断电源就近取消防器材灭火。如火情较难控制，立即通过最近的安全疏散路线撤离。
Press group should not only clean the surface of equipment in working area every day, but also thoroughly clean the inaccessible areas every week to prevent the dust accumulated to catch fire.
烫衣组除每天清洁工作区域机器表面卫生外，必须每周对不易清扫的地方进行彻底清除。防止尘毛积累过多成为易燃源。（大平烫机两侧及顶部易积灰）
Wash group must clean the dirt receiver of the drying tumbler during every shift to remove excess lint to prevent fire hazard and allow air to flow freely through the dryer so they can operate effectively. Dry linens cannot be left in the drying tumbler for long time. The linens dried must be stacked in the linen trolley after cooling down.
水洗组每班次必须清除烘干机接尘器的尘毛。保持空气流通，确保高效工作，避免火灾隐患。严禁将烘干布巾长时间留存在烘干机内。烘干布巾及抹布等必须经冷风冷却后方可卸车装入布巾车里。（每天下班前清理绒毛收集器）
Heavy stained linens must be put away from the electric mains, heating source and be handled as soon as possible
洗衣房内任何油污较重的布巾等必须远离电、热源并及时处理
One team member must be arranged specially to be in charge of the chemical room.
专人负责化学药品房的管理工作（洗衣房主管/经理负责仓库药剂堆放，及检查洗涤区域药剂堆放、使用）
The press should be operated according to the policy that the mains should be cut immediately before the operator leaves
手烫组使用电熨斗时必须坚持：人离开时，电源也随即切断的原则
It’s the responsibility and duty of the team members to observe and check the mains and cables as often as possible to prevent fires caused by short circuit.
洗衣房员工有责任和义务随时观察和检查电源线路，防止因电源短路引起火灾
Organize power & fire safety and security training regularly
定期做电源动力，消防及安全培训
Check the safety & security facility regularly. Handle problems in time when necessary.
定期检查安全保卫设施。必要时及时处理问题。
It is strictly forbidden to apply and install any equipment and set cables privately. That must be done by the engineering department.
严禁私自使用和安装任何设备和电缆。这必须由工程部来做。
