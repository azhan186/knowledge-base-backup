# 木制面及家具清洁上蜡（PA_021）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-PA_021 | **类别**: 公共区域（Public Area） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To show guest our highest standard of cleanliness. Keep the wooden surface smooth and shinning（公共区域清洁与服务标准）

## 关键要点

- 木制面/家具上蜡

## 目的

To show guest our highest standard of cleanliness. Keep the wooden surface smooth and shinning
展示我们最高的清洁标准给客人。保证木制品表面光滑和光亮

## 政策规定

Let employee know the job announcements, and require the employee to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

HOW TO POLISH WOODEN SURFACE
如何给木制表面抛光上蜡
In order to keep the surface shiny, wax the wood furniture (clean it with a wet cloth first, dry it with a dry cloth, and then wax it).
为了保持表面有光泽，要对木质家具上蜡（先用湿抹布清洁后用干抹布擦干最后上蜡）
Remove any items from the surface, however, never touch or remove guest’s belongings, so, always polish the wooden
移走表面上的任何物品，但不要碰或挪走客人的物品，只是抛光上蜡木制品
Spray furniture wax on a clean folded rag and allow it to absorb the wax rather than spray it directly on the furniture.
在干净折叠好的抹布上喷家具蜡后，再对折让其充分吸收蜡，而不是直接喷在家具上
Wipe the wooden surface with a cloth in order from left to right, and observe from the side whether the wax is evenly shining.
从左到右来回均匀的方式按顺序用抹布擦拭木制表面，并从侧面观察是否上蜡均匀光亮
Replace the removed items back to the surface as before.
将先前移走的物品复位
