# 上午下午差异房报告（Floor_025）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_025 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure room status are accurate at all times in order to avoid discrepancies, and to maintain a correct accounting re（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 差异房报告（上午/下午）
- DND（请勿打扰）处理

## 目的

To ensure room status are accurate at all times in order to avoid discrepancies, and to maintain a correct accounting record as well.
确保房态正确性，以避免不符情况，同时也使差异房记录保持正确。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of hotel to define a communication process between HSKP and front office that ensures an accurate and timely tracking and resolution process for discrepant rooms.
在客房部与前厅部之间建立沟通程序，以确保不符房跟踪解决程序的正确性与及时性。

## 程序

AM DISCREPANT ROOMS REPORT
上午不符房间报告
The AM checklist (see attached) is used to determine if the status of a room, as reported on the morning breakout sheets, is in fact the correct status from the preceding night.  The primary function of the AM report is to ascertain that revenue is reported for every room that was occupied the night before.
上午检查表（见附表）用来对照早交班表的报告与前一晚的房态是否一致。上午不符房间报告一个最基本的功能是确定报告前一晚住客房的收入。
Room attendants must physically check every guest room status on their own floor and send room status report to Housekeeping office by 11am.
客房服务员必须现场检查所在楼层每间客房的房态，并在上午11点前将房态报告交到客房部办公室。
HSKP clerk collects all floors status report.
客房部办公室员工收集所有房态报告。
The floor supervisor input the room discrepancy into the computer system according to the reports collected by the staff
楼层主管根据员工收集的房态报告，将房态有差异的房间输入电脑系统内
If any discrepancy occurs, GRM will double check and inform housekeeping clerk whether the particular room status needs to be changed.
如发生不符情况，宾客服务经理应再次检查并通知客房部文员需要修改该房间的房态。
Clerk received the request from GRM, make record and inform floor supervisor, then change the room status.
文员接到宾客服务经理通知后，需记录下来并通知主管，然更改房态。
By 2:30pm, room attendant should check room status again.
客房服务员在下午2:30分，第2次现场检查所在楼层每间客房的房态。
The floor supervisor input the room discrepancy into the computer system again according to the reports collected by the staff
楼层主管根据员工收集的房态报告，再次将房态有差异的房间输入电脑系统内
HSKP clerk print and send the room discrepancy report to GRM by 3:30pm together with the DND report.
客房部办公室员工在下午3:30分前将房态差异表及DND报告交往宾客服务经理。
Clerk received the request from GRM, make record and inform Floor Supervisor again, then change the room status.
文员接到宾客服务经理通知后，再次记录下来并通知主管，然更改房态。
Floor supervisor's responsibility in discrepant room reporting:
楼层督导对差异房报告的责任：
The floor supervisor is responsible for the following:-
楼层督导对差异房报告的责任如下：
Reporting of any discrepant rooms during shift.  The floor supervisor must double check it
在当班期间的所有异常情况的房间, 主管必须亲自检查确认。
The identification of a discrepant room is as follows:-
不符房按如下标准认定：
OD showing no sign of occupancy is reported as SKIP.  The supervisor should check wardrobes and bathroom for any sign of luggage or belongings. A turned down bed may be a sign of a No-show room.  If in doubt supervisor should immediately contact asst. floor manager for further information.
OD显示没有客人入住的迹象，将被报为SKIP。主管应检查衣柜及洗手间，看看有无客人行李或私人物品。如做了夜床而床未动可视为客人未到。如有任何疑问，主管应立即联系楼层副经理。
(VD/VC/VR) Room showing signs of occupancy is reported as "Sleeper", i.e. someone has stayed in the room but is not registered.  A note should be made of any names on luggage to help front office with guest identification.
(VD/VC/VR)显示住客房，将被报为SLEEPER，例如未登记的住客。应记下房间行李上的任何名字以便前厅部认定客人。
Discrepant Rooms fall under the following:-
差异房应归入以下房类：
Vacant Room (VD/VR/VC) found to be occupied must be reported as a sleeper.
空房(VD/VR/VC)发现被住,必须报告为 sleeper。
System shows occupied but no luggage or guest belongings in room. Must be reported as a Skip/No-show.
电脑显示客房处于入住状态，但房间内没有任何行李。必须被报告为Skip/未到。
