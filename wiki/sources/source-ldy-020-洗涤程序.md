# 洗涤程序（LDY_020）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-LDY_020 | **类别**: 洗衣房（Laundry） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

Both guest laundry and team members uniforms must be washed with proper procedures for the machines to ensure the best p（洗衣房运营标准（制服/客衣/布草/设备））

## 关键要点

- 洗涤/熨烫/折叠工艺标准

## 目的

Both guest laundry and team members uniforms must be washed with proper procedures for the machines to ensure the best possible result.
客衣或员工制服必须按照机器设定的相应程序完成洗涤,同时确保最佳洗涤效果。

## 政策规定

Ensure employees are aware of the job notes, and require the employees to operate strictly according to the requirements
让员工了解工作注意事项，要求员工严格按照要求操作

## 程序

Guest laundry service time is base on the instruction of the guest laundry list. In case of emergency laundry, the GRM contact laundry staff which is designated. The relevant staff name list will be given to GRM each Friday. Employee’s uniforms are processed at 8:30am.
客衣的洗涤时间按照客用洗衣单洗衣服务时间操作，万一有紧急洗衣，宾客服务经理联系洗衣房相关的员工，名单每周五提前提交。制服洗涤早上8:30开始。
Ensure that uniforms and guest laundry is sorted as per laundry and dry cleaning
首先将客衣、制服干水洗分类。
Inspect the garments for defects like color bleeding, burnt holes, color fading, grease, oil etc. Bright color must be checked for color fastness
检查衣物，有无掉色、褪色、破损等现象。鲜艳颜色必须测试是否掉色。
Defective garments are not to be cleaned until the guest is informed and approval is obtained.  Garments with stains must be pre-spotted before washing.
存在问题的衣物必须与客人联系，经客人同意后再处理。有污渍的客衣必须洗前处理。
Follow the special instructions on clothes label very carefully. Delicate items must be hand washed and hanged to dry.  Express garments are to be washed carefully, separately and given to the pressing unit without any delay.
根据衣物洗涤标签操作。柔软精巧的物品小心手洗挂干。加快客衣必须及时单独进行洗涤、烘干、熨烫等程序的操作。
Prepare the first load for washing according to the capacity recommended. Select the right program and the required temperature.
根据机器装载量，装机。选择合适的程序。
Chemical supplies are injected automatically into the machine by means of dispenser. Do not disturb the machine while it is on operation.
机器自动冲料期间严禁打断程序。
Once the washing process is completed, unload the machine, sort the items and put inside the dryer for drying before being sent to the pressing unit.
机器停止运转后，取出物品，送去烘干，熨烫。
Washing equipment is to be cleaned daily at the end of the operation.
每天工作结束后清洁机器。
