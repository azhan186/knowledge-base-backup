# 使用婴儿床（Floor_022）

> **LSOP No.**: RM-HSKP-Floor_022 | **类别**: 楼层（Floor/Housekeeping） | **生效日期**: 2019-08-08
> 编制：服务总监 | 批准：总经理

## 摘要

To ensure high quality baby crib are provided for babies.（客房楼层服务标准（清洁/物品/安全/特殊服务））

## 关键要点

- 婴儿床/加床提供标准

## 目的

To ensure high quality baby crib are provided for babies.
确保为婴儿准备高质量的婴儿床。

## 政策规定

It is the policy of the hotel to provide baby crib to our guest. All babies should also be treated like our regular guests.
为方便客人而提供婴儿床，并且所有婴儿应被像正规客人一样对待。

## 程序

Bring one set of baby bedding (1 baby bed sheet, 1 baby duvet and 1 duvet cover) from the linen room. Sign out the baby bedding deliver record each time before taking the baby bedding.
从布草房拿出一套婴儿床上用品（1个婴儿床单，1床婴儿被，1床婴儿被套）。每次从布草房拿出的婴儿床上用品需填写“取走单”。
Bring one baby amenity bag from HSKP office.
从客房部办公室拿一套婴儿用具。
Make sure baby crib is in good condition and will not cause accident to the baby.
确保婴儿床状态良好，并且不会导致意外发生。
Make the baby crib according to hotel standard and then sent to the guest room.
按酒店标准准备好婴儿床然后送往客房。
Baby crib making procedures:
婴儿床准备程序：
Fix the bed frame
固定床框
Put the bed mattress in the baby crib
将床垫放入婴儿床内。
Interlock each joint of the bed frame.
将连接处锁定。
c)  Double check the baby crib and make sure it is stable and locked   properly.
再次检查婴儿床，确保其稳固并被锁定牢靠。
Make up the crib
整理婴儿床
Check all the bedding.
检查所有的床上用品。
Use Kids sheet for the mattress.
在床垫上铺上儿童床单并包好。
Put the duvet in the duvet cover.
套上被套。
Fold the duvet and place it on the lower right corner at the end of the king or twin size bed (place on the twin size bed which is near the window). Don’t put inside the baby crib.
叠好被子放在大床或双床尾部右下角处(双床放靠窗的小床)。 不要放在婴儿床内
Change the bed bedding on daily basis.
每天更换床上用品。
In order to avoid loss, send the soiled baby bedding to the linen room and sign in the baby bedding delivery record.
为避免丢失，我们应将脏的婴儿床上用品送回布草房，并在婴儿床上用品发送记录上签字。
After the guest checked out, return the soiled baby bedding to the linen room and sign in the baby bedding delivery record again.
客人离店后，我们应将脏的婴儿床上用品送回布草房，并再次在婴儿床上用品发送记录上签字。
Room set up extra 4 bottles of mineral water and one tissues box every day.
房间每天额外多放4瓶矿水，及1盒面巾纸。
